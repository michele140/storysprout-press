# Leo the Light-Up Firefly

## Character Descriptions

*   **Leo**: A small firefly with a round belly and oversized, transparent wings. He has very large, kind eyes. His tail-end is a soft, matte yellow, unlike the shiny ones of his friends.
*   **Luna**: A sleek, fast firefly with a bright, neon-yellow light. She is confident and moves with a "zig-zag" pattern. She is Leo's best friend.
*   **Grandfather Glow**: A very old firefly with a slightly dusty-looking shell and a light that flickers like a warm candle. He sits very still and speaks slowly.
*   **Bella the Beetle**: A shiny purple beetle with six legs and two long antennae. she is grounded but very observant and friendly.
