# World Cup Series Book #1: MetLife's Big Match

**Target Age:** 5-9 years
**Theme:** Sportsmanship, excitement, dreams, family fun.
**Characters:**
- **Alex:** A 7-year-old soccer super-fan with a Team USA jersey.
- **Dad:** Alex’s supportive and equally excited father.
- **MetLife Stadium:** A friendly giant with silver metal skin, glowing light-bulb eyes, and goalpost arms.
- **The Players:** Star athletes from Team USA and Team Argentina.

---

## Page 1
**Visual:** A bright, colorful title page. Alex is kicking a soccer ball in his backyard, with the giant, friendly MetLife Stadium peeking over the trees in the distance, waving.
**Text:** 
MetLife’s Big Match
By StorySprout Press

## Page 2
**Visual:** Alex’s bedroom. It’s early morning, and the sun is just peeking through the window. The room is filled with soccer posters, a soccer-ball-shaped rug, and a shelf full of trophies. Alex is jumping on his bed, holding a Team USA scarf high.
**Text:**
The sun hadn't even finished waking up, but Alex was already wide awake! Today wasn't just any Saturday. Today was the day of the World Cup Match! 

“Alex, are you ready?” Dad called from the hallway.

“I’ve been ready since last year, Dad!” Alex shouted back, pulling on his lucky jersey.

---

## Page 3
**Visual:** Alex and Dad in their car, driving on a big highway (the New Jersey Turnpike). In the distance, the massive, shimmering silver shape of MetLife Stadium rises from the Meadowlands. It looks like a giant silver crown resting on the grass.
**Text:**
As they drove down the long highway, Alex pressed his face against the window. There it was! MetLife Stadium. 

It looked like a giant silver palace. Did you know MetLife is the biggest stadium in the NFL? It has over 82,000 seats! Alex wondered if he could count them all.

---

## Page 4
**Visual:** Close-up of the Stadium coming to life. The silver panels of the stadium shift slightly. Two giant corner lights blink like eyes. Alex looks up in wonder as they walk toward the entrance.
**Text:**
As Alex stepped out of the car, he heard a deep, rumbling hum. It wasn't the wind—it was the stadium itself!

MetLife was a friendly giant. He had big lights for eyes that glowed a warm yellow, and his skin was made of shimmering glass and metal. He looked down at the fans and gave a silent, metallic wink.

---

## Page 5
**Visual:** The stadium’s goalpost arms are stretched out wide as if to hug the thousands of fans streaming through the gates. Alex is high-fiving a giant silver hand made of stadium panels.
**Text:**
The giant stadium stretched out his goalpost arms to welcome everyone. Alex felt like he was walking into the heart of a friendly giant. 

“Welcome to the Meadowlands!” the giant seemed to whisper in the rustle of the flags. Alex high-fived a silver panel on the wall. It felt cool and strong.

---

## Page 6
**Visual:** Inside the stadium concourse. It's a sea of colors—people wearing jerseys from all over the world. There are vendors selling giant pretzels and flags. Alex is wide-eyed, taking it all in.
**Text:**
Inside, the stadium was buzzing like a beehive! Fans from Argentina wore blue and white stripes, while fans from the USA wore red, white, and blue. 

The air smelled like popcorn and excitement. Alex could hear music playing and people singing songs in different languages. Everyone was a friend today.

---

## Page 7
**Visual:** Alex and Dad walking through the tunnel out into the seating area. The bright green grass of the pitch is dazzling under the sun. The stadium is massive and open to the sky.
**Text:**
Then, they walked through the tunnel. *WHOOSH!* 

The stadium opened up! The bright green grass looked like a velvet carpet. The sky was a brilliant blue, and the silver walls of the stadium reached up toward the clouds. Alex had never seen anything so big!

---

## Page 8
**Visual:** The pre-game ceremony. Huge flags are being unfurled on the field. Fireworks are shooting off from the top of the stadium walls. The MetLife giant is "clapping" his goalpost arms.
**Text:**
Suddenly, the music got louder. *BOOM!* Fireworks exploded over the silver rim of the stadium, painting the sky with sparkles. 

The pre-game show had begun! Dancers moved across the grass, and giant flags fluttered in the breeze. Alex’s heart beat fast, matching the rhythm of the drums.

---

## Page 9
**Visual:** The players from USA and Argentina walk out onto the field. They are holding hands with young mascots. Alex is leaning over the railing, waving his scarf.
**Text:**
“There they are!” Dad shouted.

The players walked out, looking like heroes in a storybook. Team USA looked strong, and Team Argentina looked fast. Alex cheered as loud as he could. The MetLife giant blinked his light-bulb eyes in excitement.

---

## Page 10
**Visual:** The referee blows the whistle. The game starts! A player is kicking the ball right at the start. The crowd is a blur of motion in the background.
**Text:**
*TWEET!* The whistle blew, and the match began!

The ball zipped across the green grass like a shooting star. Argentina’s star player dribbled the ball between his feet. He was as fast as a rabbit! But the USA defenders were as strong as lions.

---

## Page 11
**Visual:** Action shot. A USA player is sprinting down the sideline with the ball. Alex is standing on his seat (with Dad holding him steady), shouting.
**Text:**
Back and forth they went! Alex watched every move. He learned that in soccer, you have to work together with your team. 

“Pass it! Pass it!” Alex yelled. The USA player kicked the ball to his teammate. They were moving closer to the goal!

---

## Page 12
**Visual:** The big moment! A USA player heads the ball toward the goal. The Argentinian goalkeeper is diving through the air to stop it. 
**Text:**
The ball flew high into the air. A USA player jumped up and—*BONK!*—he hit the ball with his head! 

The goalkeeper dived to the left. The ball spun to the right. Everyone in the stadium held their breath. The MetLife giant leaned forward, his light-bulb eyes wide.

---

## Page 13
**Visual:** **GOAL!** The ball is in the back of the net. The USA players are hugging. The crowd is erupting. Fireworks go off again.
**Text:**
*GOALLLLLLLL!*

The stadium erupted! The roar was so loud that Alex felt it in his toes. The USA players did a happy dance on the grass. Alex and Dad hugged, jumping up and down. Even the MetLife giant seemed to be shivering with joy!

---

## Page 14
**Visual:** Half-time. Alex is eating a hot dog and looking at the giant video board. The board shows a "Fan Cam" with Alex and Dad on the big screen!
**Text:**
Between periods, the field was quiet for a moment, but the fans were still busy. Alex enjoyed a delicious stadium hot dog. 

Suddenly, he looked up at the giant video board. “Dad, look! It’s us!” Alex and Dad were on the big screen, waving their flags for everyone to see.

---

## Page 15
**Visual:** The next period starts. Argentina has the ball now. They are passing it quickly. The USA goalkeeper is alert, knees bent.
**Text:**
The next period began, and Argentina was determined. They moved the ball like a whirlwind! 

“They’re very good,” Alex said. 

“They are,” Dad agreed. “That’s why the World Cup is so special—the best teams in the world play here.”

---

## Page 16
**Visual:** Argentina scores a goal. The Argentinian fans are celebrating. Alex looks a little sad, but Dad is clapping for the great play.
**Text:**
Argentina kicked a powerful shot. *SWISH!* It went right into the corner of the net. 

Now the score was tied! The Argentina fans cheered and sang. Alex felt a little sad, but then he saw the USA players high-five the Argentina players. 

---

## Page 17
**Visual:** Close-up of the USA and Argentina players shaking hands after a tough play.
**Text:**
“That was a great goal,” Dad said. “In soccer, we cheer for our team, but we also respect the other team. That’s called sportsmanship.”

Alex nodded. He began to clap for the great play too. It was a wonderful match!

---

## Page 18
**Visual:** The final minutes of the game. It’s intense. A player is about to take a corner kick. The sun is starting to set, making the stadium’s metal facade glow orange.
**Text:**
The clock was ticking down. Only a few minutes left! 

The sun began to set, and the MetLife giant’s silver skin turned a beautiful orange and gold. He turned on his light-bulb eyes to help everyone see the finish.

---

## Page 19
**Visual:** A final shot by USA hits the post and bounces away. The MetLife giant’s goalpost arm seems to "vibrate" from the sound of the ball hitting the metal.
**Text:**
USA took one last shot. *CLANG!* It hit the goalpost and bounced away. 

The stadium giant felt the vibration of the metal. Everyone groaned, then laughed. It was so close!

---

## Page 20
**Visual:** The final whistle. The players are all shaking hands and swapping jerseys. The score on the big screen shows a 1-1 tie.
**Text:**
*TWEET! TWEET! TWEET!* 

The match was over. It was a tie! Both teams had played their best. Alex saw the players trading jerseys. They were rivals on the field, but friends when the game ended.

---

## Page 21
**Visual:** Alex and Dad leaving the stadium. Alex is wearing a new World Cup hat. He looks back at the stadium, which is now glowing bright blue in the twilight.
**Text:**
As they walked back to the car, Alex felt tired but very happy. He had a new World Cup hat and a souvenir program to remember the day. 

“That was the best day ever,” Alex said. “I want to play on that grass someday.”

---

## Page 22
**Visual:** Alex in the backseat of the car, fast asleep. His new hat is tilted on his head, and he's clutching his souvenir ball. Outside the window, the stadium is a glowing beacon in the night.
**Text:**
In the car, Alex’s eyes grew heavy. He dreamed of dribbling the ball through the Meadowlands, scoring the winning goal in the World Cup Final. 

Dad smiled and turned the radio down low.

---

## Page 23
**Visual:** The MetLife giant is settling down for the night. His light-bulb eyes are dimming, and he has a peaceful expression. The stars are out.
**Text:**
The fans went home, and the stadium grew quiet. The MetLife giant settled back into the grass. 

He had a big job coming up in 2026—hosting the World Cup Final! He needed his rest so he could be ready to welcome the world again.

MetLife Stadium is ready for the world! Will you be there to cheer?
