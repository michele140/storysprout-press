# Benny the Bus: A Big Day for a Friendly Bus

## Character Descriptions

*   **Benny the Bus**: A bright yellow, shiny school bus with a big, friendly smile on his front grille. His headlights look like large, curious eyes. He has a sturdy stop sign arm that pops out from his side.
*   **Depot Dan**: A kind older man with gray hair and a blue jumpsuit. He always carries a shiny wrench and a cheerful rag for polishing.
*   **Mia**: A small girl with pigtails and a bright purple backpack. She looks a little nervous at first but has a sweet smile.
*   **Liam**: A boy in a striped shirt who carries a small green toy dinosaur everywhere.
*   **The Stop Sign**: Benny's red, octagonal arm that pops out with a friendly "click-clack."
