# Noah's Big Boat Activity Book (Outline)

**Target Audience:** Ages 2-6
**Theme:** Bible Series - Animals, Faith, and God's Promises

---

## Page-by-Page Outline (24 Pages)

### Page 1: Cover Page
*   **Activity:** Title and Illustration
*   **Description:** "Noah's Big Boat: A Bible Adventures Activity Book!" A large, happy illustration of Noah waving from the Ark's window with a giraffe and an elephant peeking out.

### Page 2: This Book Belongs To...
*   **Activity:** Name Plate
*   **Description:** A frame shaped like a wooden plank from the Ark.
*   **Text:** "This Ark Helper is: [Name]"

### Page 3: Coloring Page - Noah and the Garden
*   **Scene:** Noah among flowers and butterflies, looking up at the sky.
*   **Text:** "Noah loves God and the beautiful world. Color Noah's garden!"

### Page 4: Tracing Activity - A-R-K
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the word "ARK".
*   **Text:** "Noah built a big boat called an ARK. Can you trace the letters?"

### Page 5: Coloring Page - Building the Ark
*   **Scene:** Noah holding a mallet, working on the wooden boat.
*   **Text:** "Noah is building the big boat. *Clunk, clunk, clunk!*"

### Page 6: Tracing Activity - Ark Shape
*   **Activity:** Shape Tracing.
*   **Description:** A large dashed outline of the sturdy, rectangular Ark.
*   **Text:** "Trace the big boat! It's going to be a safe home."

### Page 7: Coloring Page - The Animals Arrive
*   **Scene:** A long line of animals approaching the Ark.
*   **Text:** "Look! Here come the animals!"

### Page 8: Simple Maze - Help the Animals find the Ark
*   **Activity:** Maze.
*   **Description:** A simple path helping a pair of lions find their way through some trees to the Ark's ramp.
*   **Text:** "The lions need to get to the boat! Can you show them the way?"

### Page 9: Coloring Page - The Elephants
*   **Scene:** Two elephants walking up the ramp.
*   **Text:** "Two big elephants go *stomp, stomp, stomp*."

### Page 10: Counting Activity - Animal Pairs
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** Pictures of several animal pairs (2 lions, 2 birds, 2 giraffes).
*   **Text:** "Noah's animals come in twos. How many pairs can you count? [ ]"

### Page 11: Coloring Page - The Giraffes
*   **Scene:** Two giraffes with their long necks peeking over the side of the boat.
*   **Text:** "The giraffes are so tall! Use your yellow and orange crayons."

### Page 12: Tracing Activity - Animal Tracks
*   **Activity:** Line Tracing.
*   **Description:** Paths of paw prints and hoof prints leading from left to right.
*   **Text:** "Follow the tracks to the big boat!"

### Page 13: Coloring Page - Rain, Rain, Go Away
*   **Scene:** The Ark floating on gentle blue waves with raindrops falling.
*   **Text:** "The rain begins to fall. *Pitter-patter, pitter-patter.*"

### Page 14: Connect-the-Dots - The Big Ark
*   **Activity:** Connect-the-dots (Numbers 1-10).
*   **Description:** An outline of the Ark's hull and roof.
*   **Text:** "Connect the dots to finish the boat!"

### Page 15: Coloring Page - Cozy Inside
*   **Scene:** Animals napping on hay inside the warm Ark.
*   **Text:** "Inside the boat, it is warm and cozy."

### Page 16: Spot the Difference - Ark Scenes
*   **Activity:** Spot 3 Differences.
*   **Description:** Two pictures of Noah feeding the animals.
*   **Differences:** (1) The number of birds in the window, (2) The color of Noah's staff, (3) A small mouse on a hay bale.

### Page 17: Coloring Page - The Dove Flies
*   **Scene:** Noah letting the little white dove fly out the window.
*   **Text:** "Fly, little dove! Go and look for land."

### Page 18: Tracing Activity - N-O-A-H
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the name "NOAH".
*   **Text:** "Noah was a very kind man. Trace his name!"

### Page 19: Coloring Page - The Olive Branch
*   **Scene:** The dove returning to Noah with a green leaf in its beak.
*   **Text:** "The dove is back! She found a green leaf."

### Page 20: Matching Activity - Animal Names
*   **Activity:** Matching.
*   **Description:** Drawing a line from a simple animal picture to its name (Elephant, Lion, Giraffe, Dove).
*   **Text:** "Can you match the animal to its name?"

### Page 21: Coloring Page - The Beautiful Rainbow
*   **Scene:** A large rainbow appearing over the Ark on the mountain.
*   **Text:** "God sent a beautiful rainbow. Use every color!"

### Page 22: Counting Activity - Raindrops
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** A cloud with several large raindrops falling.
*   **Text:** "How many raindrops are falling? [ ]"

### Page 23: Drawing Activity - My Favorite Animal
*   **Activity:** Creative Drawing.
*   **Description:** A blank space with a decorative border.
*   **Text:** "Which animal would you invite on the Ark? Draw it here!"

### Page 24: Certificate of Achievement
*   **Activity:** Award Page.
*   **Description:** "Congratulations! [Name] is a Certified Noah's Helper!" Decorated with a rainbow and a dove.
