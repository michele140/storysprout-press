# The Curious Little Penguin

## Character Descriptions

*   **Pip**: A very fluffy, grey-and-white baby penguin calf. He has enormous, dark eyes that always seem to be wide open with wonder. His flippers are a bit too long, and he often trips over his own big, orange feet.
*   **Waddles**: Pip’s loving parent. Waddles is a large, sturdy penguin with a very sleek coat and a gentle way of nudging Pip along.
*   **Professor Puffin**: A colorful puffin with a large, orange-and-yellow beak. He wears a pair of tiny, round spectacles on the end of his beak and a small bowtie made of dried seaweed.
*   **Squiggles**: A sleek, silver-spotted seal pup. He is very bouncy and loves to slide on his belly. He has long, twitchy whiskers and a playful bark.

## Story Pages

### Page 1: Title Page
**Text:** The Curious Little Penguin
**Scene Description:** Pip is standing on a bright white iceberg, looking down into the deep blue water where a school of small silver fish is swimming.

### Page 2
**Text:** In the middle of the Great White North, where the ice is thick and the wind whistles tunes, lived Pip.
**Scene Description:** A wide shot of the snowy Antarctic landscape. A large colony of penguins is gathered in the distance.

### Page 3
**Text:** Pip was a small penguin with a very big question for everything he saw.
**Scene Description:** Pip looking at a snowflake on his flipper. He looks like he’s trying to count its points.

### Page 4
**Text:** "Why is the snow white?" Pip asked. "Why is the water cold?"
**Scene Description:** Pip talking to Waddles. Waddles is smiling and gently patting Pip’s head.

### Page 5
**Text:** Waddles would laugh. "That's just how the world is, Pip. Stay close to the colony, it’s safe here."
**Scene Description:** The colony of penguins huddled together for warmth. Pip is looking away, toward the horizon.

### Page 6
**Text:** But Pip’s curiosity was like a little engine. He wanted to see what was beyond the next snowy hill.
**Scene Description:** Pip walking away from the colony on his tippy-toes while the other penguins are busy napping.

### Page 7
**Text:** He waddled up the hill. *Slip, slide, thump!* He tumbled down the other side into a valley of blue ice.
**Scene Description:** Pip rolling down a hill and landing in a pile of soft snow. He looks surprised but excited.

### Page 8
**Text:** "Oh my!" Pip gasped. The ice here wasn't white. It was bright, glowing blue!
**Scene Description:** Pip standing in a cave made of shimmering blue ice. It looks like a crystal palace.

### Page 9
**Text:** "It's blue because it's very old," said a voice. Pip looked up and saw Professor Puffin.
**Scene Description:** Professor Puffin perched on an ice ledge, looking down through his spectacles.

### Page 10
**Text:** "Is the whole world blue?" Pip asked. "Not at all!" the Professor chirped. "The world is as many colors as a rainbow!"
**Scene Description:** Professor Puffin gesturing with his wing. He looks like he’s lecturing a class.

### Page 11
**Text:** Pip was amazed. He wanted to see more colors! He thanked the Professor and waddled on.
**Scene Description:** Pip waving goodbye to the Puffin. He’s walking with more confidence now.

### Page 12
**Text:** He came to a frozen slide. *Whoosh!* Squiggles the Seal zipped past him on his belly.
**Scene Description:** Squiggles sliding down a long ice ramp, leaving a shiny trail. Pip is watching with wide eyes.

### Page 13
**Text:** "Want to race?" Squiggles barked. "I... I'm not supposed to go fast," Pip said. "But I am curious!"
**Scene Description:** Pip looking at the slide. Squiggles is at the bottom, waving his flipper.

### Page 14
**Text:** Pip lay on his belly. *One, two, three... GO!*
**Scene Description:** Pip sliding down the ice. His flippers are tucked in, and he has a huge, joyful grin.

### Page 15
**Text:** They slid over the ice and under the frozen arches. Pip saw the silver ocean and the golden sun.
**Scene Description:** A beautiful shot of the two friends sliding toward the sea. The sun is low and bright.

### Page 16
**Text:** But then, the wind began to growl. A big, grey cloud covered the sun.
**Scene Description:** The sky turning dark. Large snowflakes are starting to fall. Pip looks a bit worried.

### Page 17
**Text:** "A storm is coming!" Squiggles said. "I have to go back to my mama. You should too, Pip!"
**Scene Description:** Squiggles diving into a hole in the ice. Pip is standing alone in the wind.

### Page 18
**Text:** Pip looked around. The snowy hill looked very different in the dark. He didn't know which way was home.
**Scene Description:** Pip surrounded by swirling snow. Everything looks white and confusing.

### Page 19
**Text:** "Waddles? Mama? Papa?" Pip called out. But the wind just whistled back.
**Scene Description:** A small, lonely Pip in the middle of a big, white page.

### Page 20
**Text:** Pip remembered what Professor Puffin said. "The stars can show the way when the sun is gone."
**Scene Description:** Pip looking up at the sky. A few bright stars are peeking through the clouds.

### Page 21
**Text:** He saw the brightest star. It was right over the hill where the colony lived!
**Scene Description:** Pip pointing at a large star. He looks determined.

### Page 22
**Text:** *Waddle, trip, waddle!* Pip pushed through the snow. He was tired, but he kept going.
**Scene Description:** Pip struggling through a snowdrift. He’s almost at the top of the hill.

### Page 23
**Text:** At the top, he saw a line of penguins. Waddles was at the front, calling his name.
**Scene Description:** The colony is visible now. Waddles is running toward Pip with open flippers.

### Page 24
**Text:** "I found so many colors, Waddles!" Pip chirped as he was hugged. "The world is big, but I'm glad I'm home."
**Scene Description:** A warm hug between Pip and Waddles. The other penguins are gathered around, looking happy.

**The End.**
