# Benny the Bus Activity Book (Outline)

**Target Audience:** Ages 2-6
**Theme:** Transportation, Community Helpers, and Daily Routines

---

## Page-by-Page Outline (24 Pages)

### Page 1: Cover Page
*   **Activity:** Title and Illustration
*   **Description:** "Benny the Bus's Big Day Activity Book!" Large, friendly illustration of Benny the Bus waving with his headlights.

### Page 2: This Book Belongs To...
*   **Activity:** Name Plate
*   **Description:** A decorated box for the child to write or trace their name. Surround it with small bus and stop sign icons.

### Page 3: Coloring Page - Benny at the Depot
*   **Scene:** Benny parked at the bus depot with Depot Dan.
*   **Text:** "Good morning, Benny! Color Benny and Depot Dan at the bus depot."

### Page 4: Tracing Activity - Road to School
*   **Activity:** Tracing dashed lines.
*   **Description:** Long, curvy dashed lines representing a road.
*   **Text:** "Help Benny stay on the road! Trace the lines from the depot to the first stop."

### Page 5: Coloring Page - Picking up Mia
*   **Scene:** Benny at the first stop, Mia is boarding with her backpack.
*   **Text:** "Mia is ready for school! Color Mia's bright purple backpack."

### Page 6: Tracing Activity - Wheels on the Bus
*   **Activity:** Tracing circles.
*   **Description:** Four large dashed circles representing Benny's wheels.
*   **Text:** "The wheels on the bus go round and round. Trace the circles to finish Benny's wheels."

### Page 7: Simple Maze - Help Benny Find the School
*   **Activity:** Maze.
*   **Description:** A simple path with one or two dead ends. Start is Benny, end is the school building.
*   **Text:** "Beep beep! Can you help Benny find the way to school?"

### Page 8: Coloring Page - Liam and the Dinosaur
*   **Scene:** Liam sitting on the bus holding his toy dinosaur.
*   **Text:** "Liam brought his dinosaur to school! Color the dinosaur green."

### Page 9: Counting Activity - Bus Windows
*   **Activity:** Counting and Number Tracing.
*   **Description:** A side view of Benny with 6 large windows.
*   **Text:** "How many windows does Benny have? Count them and trace the number: 6"

### Page 10: Coloring Page - The Happy Singers
*   **Scene:** A group of children on the bus singing and clapping.
*   **Text:** "Everyone loves to sing on the bus! Color the happy children."

### Page 11: Connect-the-Dots - School Bus Shape
*   **Activity:** Connect-the-dots (Numbers 1-10).
*   **Description:** A simple outline of a bus.
*   **Text:** "Connect the dots from 1 to 10 to see what Benny looks like!"

### Page 12: Coloring Page - Arriving at School
*   **Scene:** Benny parked in front of the school as kids get off.
*   **Text:** "We made it! Color the big brick school house."

### Page 13: Spot the Difference - Benny's Rest Time
*   **Activity:** Spot 3 Differences.
*   **Description:** Two almost identical pictures of Benny parked in the lot with birds nearby.
*   **Differences:** (1) A bird on the roof, (2) Benny's eyes closed, (3) A different number of flowers.
*   **Text:** "Benny is resting. Can you find 3 things that are different?"

### Page 14: Tracing Activity - Stop Sign Shape
*   **Activity:** Tracing Octagons.
*   **Description:** Large dashed octagons.
*   **Text:** "Benny's stop sign says STOP! Trace the shape of the stop sign."

### Page 15: Coloring Page - The Birds in the Lot
*   **Scene:** Close up of the birds Benny watches while he waits.
*   **Text:** "Benny makes friends with the birds. Color the blue birds."

### Page 16: Counting Activity - Bus Wheels
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** A picture showing multiple wheels.
*   **Text:** "How many wheels can you see? Let's count them together! 1, 2, 3, 4."

### Page 17: Coloring Page - Afternoon Pickup
*   **Scene:** Children running out of school back to Benny.
*   **Text:** "School is over! Color the children as they run to Benny."

### Page 18: Tracing Activity - Zig-Zag Road
*   **Activity:** Tracing zig-zag lines.
*   **Description:** Sharp up-and-down dashed lines.
*   **Text:** "The road has some bumps! Trace the zig-zag lines."

### Page 19: Coloring Page - Dropping off Mia
*   **Scene:** Mia waving goodbye to Benny at her house.
*   **Text:** "See you tomorrow, Mia! Color Mia's happy home."

### Page 20: Simple Puzzle - Match the Items
*   **Activity:** Matching/Drawing lines.
*   **Description:** Match characters to their items. (Mia -> Purple Backpack, Liam -> Dinosaur, Dan -> Wrench).
*   **Text:** "Who owns what? Draw a line to match the friend to their special thing."

### Page 21: Coloring Page - Sunset Depot
*   **Scene:** Benny back at the depot with the sunset in the background.
*   **Text:** "The day is done. Color the beautiful sunset sky."

### Page 22: Drawing Activity - Design Your Own Bus
*   **Activity:** Creative Drawing.
*   **Description:** A large blank rectangle with two circles at the bottom.
*   **Text:** "If you had a bus, what would it look like? Draw your own bus here!"

### Page 23: Coloring Page - Sleepy Benny
*   **Scene:** Benny in his spot with eyes closed, Depot Dan saying goodnight.
*   **Text:** "Goodnight, Benny. Sweet dreams!"

### Page 24: Certificate of Achievement
*   **Activity:** Award Page.
*   **Description:** "Congratulations! [Name] is an Official School Bus Helper!" Decorated with stars and a picture of Benny.
