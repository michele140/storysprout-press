# World Cup Series Book #6: Houston's Big Heart

**Target Age:** 5-9 years
**Theme:** Diversity, friendship, space exploration, cosmic energy.
**Characters:**
- **Ravi:** A 9-year-old from Houston who loves space and soccer. He dreams of being an astronaut-soccer-player.
- **Tía Maria:** Ravi’s aunt, who works at NASA and is a huge soccer fan.
- **NRG Neo:** A powerful, boxy stadium character with a retractable roof that looks like a spaceship hatch and glowing "cosmic" blue lights.
- **Team Mexico:** The "El Tri" team in their vibrant green and red kits.

## Page 3
**Visual:** Ravi and Tía Maria driving through the sprawling streets of Houston. They pass the Space Center and people of many different backgrounds waving flags.
**Text:**
Houston is one of the most diverse cities in the world. As they drove, Ravi saw flags from Mexico, Nigeria, Vietnam, and many more flying from porches. 

“Houston has a big heart, Ravi,” Tía Maria said. “We welcome everyone to our cosmic home.” 

Ravi looked at the massive NRG Stadium appearing on the horizon. It looked like a giant silver spaceship had landed in the middle of Texas!
