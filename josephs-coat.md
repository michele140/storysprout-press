# Joseph's Colorful Coat

## Character Descriptions

*   **Joseph**: A happy young boy with a bright smile. He has brown curly hair and wears a magnificent, long coat with every color of the rainbow.
*   **Jacob**: Joseph’s father. A kind old man with a long white beard and a warm, loving face. He wears a simple tan robe and a woven belt.
*   **The Brothers**: Joseph's ten older brothers. They wear simple shepherd's clothes in earthy colors like brown and green. They look a little bit grumpy but not mean.
*   **Snowball**: A small, very fluffy white sheep who follows Joseph everywhere. Snowball has a little bell around her neck.

---

## Story Pages

### Page 1: Title Page
**Text:** Joseph's Colorful Coat
**Scene Description:** Joseph is standing in a field, wearing his bright, multi-colored coat and spinning around happily. Snowball the sheep is jumping next to him.

### Page 2
**Text:** Once there was a boy named Joseph. Joseph lived in a big, happy home with his father and ten older brothers.
**Scene Description:** Joseph is standing with his family in front of their large tents. His father, Jacob, is patting him on the head.

### Page 3
**Text:** Joseph’s father, Jacob, loved him very much. One day, Jacob had a very special surprise for Joseph.
**Scene Description:** Jacob is holding a large, folded bundle behind his back. Joseph is looking up at him with curious, excited eyes.

### Page 4
**Text:** Jacob gave Joseph a beautiful coat. It had stripes of red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple!
**Scene Description:** Joseph is wearing the new coat for the first time. It is long and shiny. Jacob looks very proud.

### Page 5
**Text:** Joseph loved his new coat. “Thank you, Father!” he said. He felt like a prince.
**Scene Description:** Joseph is hugging his father. The coat stands out beautifully against the simple colors of the camp.

### Page 6
**Text:** But Joseph’s brothers were a little bit jealous. They wished they had colorful coats, too.
**Scene Description:** The brothers are standing in a group, whispering and looking at Joseph's coat with slightly grumpy faces.

### Page 7
**Text:** One night, Joseph had a very special dream. He saw the bright sun, the moon, and eleven stars dancing in the sky.
**Scene Description:** A dream bubble above Joseph as he sleeps. Inside the bubble, a happy sun, moon, and stars are twinkling.

### Page 8
**Text:** Joseph told his family about his dream. “The stars were all bowing to me!” he said with a smile.
**Scene Description:** Joseph is at the breakfast table, gesturing with his hands. His brothers look even more grumpy now.

### Page 9
**Text:** Joseph had another dream. He saw bundles of golden wheat in a field. His bundle stood tall while the others bowed.
**Scene Description:** Another dream bubble. Tall, golden wheat bundles are standing in a circle under a bright blue sky.

### Page 10
**Text:** The brothers did not like Joseph’s dreams. They decided to play a trick on him to take his coat away.
**Scene Description:** The brothers are huddled together in a field, planning something. One of them is pointing toward Joseph in the distance.

### Page 11
**Text:** One day, when Joseph came to find them in the field, they put him into a deep, dry well.
**Scene Description:** Joseph is sitting at the bottom of a shallow, sandy pit. It looks like a big, empty sandbox. He looks confused but not scared.

### Page 12
**Text:** The brothers took Joseph's colorful coat. Joseph felt very lonely at the bottom of the well.
**Scene Description:** Joseph is looking up at the sky from the pit. Snowball the sheep is peeking over the edge, looking down at him.

### Page 13
**Text:** Soon, some kind travelers came by on their way to a far-away land called Egypt.
**Scene Description:** A line of camels with colorful rugs is walking past the pit. The travelers are wearing big sun hats.

### Page 14
**Text:** The travelers found Joseph and helped him out of the well. “Come with us to Egypt!” they said.
**Scene Description:** A traveler is reaching down and helping Joseph climb out of the sandy pit. Snowball is wagging her tail.

### Page 15
**Text:** Joseph traveled a long way on a tall, bumpy camel. Egypt was very big and had many beautiful buildings.
**Scene Description:** Joseph is sitting on a camel, looking at the giant pyramids and palm trees of Egypt. He looks amazed.

### Page 16
**Text:** In Egypt, Joseph worked very hard. He was so helpful that the King of Egypt made him a very important leader.
**Scene Description:** Joseph is in a beautiful palace, wearing a clean white tunic and a gold necklace. He is talking to the King (Pharaoh).

### Page 17
**Text:** Joseph had an important job. He saved up lots of grain so that everyone would have plenty of food to eat.
**Scene Description:** Joseph is standing in a giant room full of big bags of grain. He is checking a list on a scroll.

### Page 18
**Text:** Many years passed. Back home, Joseph’s family ran out of food. They heard that there was food in Egypt.
**Scene Description:** Jacob and the brothers are looking at their empty baskets. They look very hungry and sad.

### Page 19
**Text:** The brothers traveled to Egypt to ask for help. They did not know that the important leader was their brother, Joseph!
**Scene Description:** The brothers are kneeling in front of Joseph in the palace. Joseph is sitting on a high chair, looking down at them.

### Page 20
**Text:** Joseph saw his brothers. He felt his heart get very warm. He still loved them very much.
**Scene Description:** A close-up of Joseph’s face. He has a big, kind smile and tears of joy in his eyes.

### Page 21
**Text:** Joseph told them who he was. “I am your brother, Joseph!” he cried. The brothers were very surprised!
**Scene Description:** Joseph has stepped down from his chair and is holding out his arms. The brothers' eyes are wide with wonder.

### Page 22
**Text:** Joseph forgave his brothers for their trick. He gave them a big hug and lots of yummy food to take home.
**Scene Description:** A big group hug in the palace. Everyone is smiling and happy. Snowball (now an older sheep) is there, too!

### Page 23
**Text:** Joseph’s whole family moved to Egypt to live with him. Even his father, Jacob, came to see the beautiful palace.
**Scene Description:** Joseph is hugging his old father, Jacob. Jacob is wearing Joseph's old colorful coat, which Joseph had kept.

### Page 24
**Text:** Joseph knew that God had a special plan for him all along. His heart was as bright as his colorful coat.
**Scene Description:** The whole family is having a big feast together in a garden. Joseph is sitting at the center, looking very happy.

**The End.**
