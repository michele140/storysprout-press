# The First Christmas Activity Book (Outline)

**Target Audience:** Ages 2-6
**Theme:** Bible Series - Love, Giving, and Wonder

---

## Page-by-Page Outline (24 Pages)

### Page 1: Cover Page
*   **Activity:** Title and Illustration
*   **Description:** "The First Christmas: A Bible Adventures Activity Book!" A large, glowing illustration of the stable under the big Christmas star.

### Page 2: This Book Belongs To...
*   **Activity:** Name Plate
*   **Description:** A frame decorated with tiny stars, angels, and bells.
*   **Text:** "This Christmas Star Helper is: [Name]"

### Page 3: Coloring Page - Mary and the Angel
*   **Scene:** Mary in her garden, looking up at the bright, smiling Angel.
*   **Text:** "The Angel has good news! Color the Angel's bright wings."

### Page 4: Tracing Activity - S-T-A-R
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the word "STAR".
*   **Text:** "A bright star is in the sky. Can you trace the word?"

### Page 5: Coloring Page - The Long Journey
*   **Scene:** Mary riding the gentle donkey while Joseph leads the way.
*   **Text:** "*Clip-clop, clip-clop.* Color the little donkey."

### Page 6: Tracing Activity - Star Shapes
*   **Activity:** Shape Tracing.
*   **Description:** Different sizes of dashed stars.
*   **Text:** "The sky is full of stars! Trace the star shapes."

### Page 7: Coloring Page - No Room at the Inn
*   **Scene:** Joseph knocking on a door while Mary waits nearby.
*   **Text:** "The town is very full. Color the wooden doors."

### Page 8: Simple Maze - Follow the Star to the Stable
*   **Activity:** Maze.
*   **Description:** A simple path through the streets of Bethlehem to reach the stable.
*   **Text:** "Can you find the way to the cozy stable?"

### Page 9: Coloring Page - The Cozy Stable
*   **Scene:** Mary and Joseph settling into the stable with a friendly cow and sheep.
*   **Text:** "It is warm and dry in the stable. Color the golden hay."

### Page 10: Tracing Activity - J-E-S-U-S
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the name "JESUS".
*   **Text:** "The Baby's name is Jesus. Can you trace His name?"

### Page 11: Coloring Page - Baby Jesus
*   **Scene:** A close-up of Baby Jesus sleeping peacefully in the manger.
*   **Text:** "Baby Jesus is here! Color His soft blankets."

### Page 12: Counting Activity - Gifts for Baby
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** Three ornate boxes (the gifts from the Wise Men).
*   **Text:** "The Wise Men brought special gifts. How many are there? Count them: [ ]"

### Page 13: Coloring Page - Joyful Angels
*   **Scene:** A group of bright Angels singing in the night sky.
*   **Text:** "The Angels are singing! Color their joyful faces."

### Page 14: Connect-the-Dots - The Big Star
*   **Activity:** Connect-the-dots (Numbers 1-10).
*   **Description:** An outline of the large Christmas star.
*   **Text:** "Connect the dots 1-10 to see the bright star!"

### Page 15: Coloring Page - Shepherds and Sheep
*   **Scene:** Friendly shepherds and their fluffy sheep looking at the Angels.
*   **Text:** "The shepherds are surprised! Color the woolly sheep."

### Page 16: Counting Activity - Fluffy Sheep
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** A group of 5-6 fluffy sheep in the field.
*   **Text:** "How many fluffy sheep can you see? Count them: [ ]"

### Page 17: Coloring Page - The Three Wise Men
*   **Scene:** The Wise Men riding their tall camels across the desert.
*   **Text:** "The Wise Men are on a journey. Color their colorful robes."

### Page 18: Matching Activity - Christmas Friends
*   **Activity:** Matching.
*   **Description:** Draw a line from the person to their item.
*   **Items:** Mary -> Blue shawl, Shepherd -> Wooden staff, Wise Man -> Gift box, Angel -> Glowing light.
*   **Text:** "Can you match the friends to their things?"

### Page 19: Coloring Page - Visiting the Stable
*   **Scene:** The shepherds and Wise Men gathered around Mary, Joseph, and the Baby.
*   **Text:** "Everyone is happy to see Baby Jesus. Color the happy stable."

### Page 20: Spot the Difference - The Stable Night
*   **Activity:** Spot 3 Differences.
*   **Description:** Two pictures of the stable under the star.
*   **Differences:** (1) A small lantern on the wall, (2) The number of sheep, (3) The pattern on Mary's shawl.

### Page 21: Drawing Activity - My Nativity Scene
*   **Activity:** Creative Drawing.
*   **Description:** A large empty frame.
*   **Text:** "Draw your own picture of Mary, Joseph, and Baby Jesus!"

### Page 22: Counting Activity - Bright Angels
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** A group of Angels in the sky.
*   **Text:** "How many Angels are singing today? Count them: [ ]"

### Page 23: Coloring Page - Peace and Love
*   **Scene:** Mary holding Baby Jesus while the big star shines above.
*   **Text:** "Christmas is a time of love. Color the peaceful night."

### Page 24: Christmas Star Helper Certificate
*   **Activity:** Award Page.
*   **Description:** "Congratulations! [Name] is a Christmas Star Helper!" Decorated with stars, angels, and a little manger.
