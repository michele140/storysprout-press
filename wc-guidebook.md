## 🌍 THE WORLD CUP STORY: HISTORY & LEGENDS

### 📜 A Century of Soccer
The FIFA World Cup is the biggest sporting event on the planet! 
*   **The First Kick:** The first-ever World Cup was held in **Uruguay in 1930**. Only 13 teams played back then!
*   **The Golden Prize:** The winners take home the **FIFA World Cup Trophy**. It is made of solid 18-carat gold and depicts two human figures holding up the Earth.
*   **Most Wins:** Brazil holds the record with **5 World Cup titles**, followed closely by Italy and Germany.

### 🌟 What’s New for 2026?
*   **Expansion:** For the first time ever, **48 nations** will compete (up from 32)!
*   **North American Trio:** This is the first World Cup hosted by **three countries**: the USA, Mexico, and Canada.
*   **The Marathon:** There will be a record-breaking **104 matches** played over several weeks.
