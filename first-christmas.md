# The First Christmas

## Character Descriptions

*   **Mary**: A kind young woman with a gentle smile and calm eyes. She wears a soft white dress and a light blue shawl draped over her head and shoulders.
*   **Joseph**: A strong, kind man with a brown beard and warm eyes. He wears a simple brown robe and carries a sturdy wooden staff for walking.
*   **Baby Jesus**: A tiny, peaceful baby with a soft glow. He is wrapped in swaddling clothes (strips of soft white cloth) and lies in a wooden manger filled with golden hay.
*   **The Angels**: Bright, joyful beings with big, white, glowing wings. They have kind, smiling faces and wear robes that shimmer like starlight.
*   **The Shepherds**: Friendly men with weathered faces and warm woolly cloaks. They carry shepherds' staffs and are often accompanied by fluffy white sheep.
*   **The Wise Men**: Three kings in rich, colorful robes (purple, deep red, and emerald green). They wear golden crowns and carry beautiful ornate boxes (gifts).
*   **The Donkey**: A small, gentle grey donkey with big ears and a kind expression.

## Story Pages

### Page 1: Title Page
**Text:** The First Christmas
**Scene Description:** Mary and Joseph are standing by a stable under a very large, bright star in a deep blue night sky. Mary is holding the tiny Baby Jesus.

### Page 2
**Text:** Long ago, in a town called Nazareth, lived a kind young woman named Mary. She loved God with all her heart.
**Scene Description:** Mary is in a simple, sunny garden, tending to some flowers. She looks very peaceful.

### Page 3
**Text:** One day, a bright Angel appeared! “Do not be afraid, Mary,” the Angel said with a smile. “God has a special gift for the world.”
**Scene Description:** A glowing Angel stands before Mary in her garden. Mary looks surprised but not scared. The Angel is shining brightly.

### Page 4
**Text:** Mary was going to have a baby boy. His name would be Jesus, and He would be very special.
**Scene Description:** Mary is sitting down, looking happy and thoughtful, with the Angel still nearby. A soft light surrounds her.

### Page 5
**Text:** Mary was married to a kind man named Joseph. Joseph promised to take care of Mary and the new baby.
**Scene Description:** Joseph is in his carpenter shop, holding a piece of wood and smiling at Mary, who is standing in the doorway.

### Page 6
**Text:** One day, they had to go on a long journey to a town called Bethlehem.
**Scene Description:** Mary is sitting on the gentle donkey, and Joseph is leading the way with his staff. They are leaving Nazareth.

### Page 7
**Text:** *Clip-clop, clip-clop.* The little donkey walked and walked. The road was long, and the sun was warm.
**Scene Description:** A wide shot of Mary, Joseph, and the donkey traveling along a winding path through some hills.

### Page 8
**Text:** When they reached Bethlehem, the sun was setting. The town was very full of people.
**Scene Description:** Bethlehem at twilight. There are many people walking around, and the lights in the houses are starting to glow.

### Page 9
**Text:** Joseph knocked on many doors. “Is there any room for us?” he asked. But every house was full.
**Scene Description:** Joseph is talking to a man at the door of an inn. The man is shaking his head "no." Mary is waiting on the donkey, looking tired.

### Page 10
**Text:** Finally, one kind man said, “You can stay in my stable. It is warm and dry with lots of hay.”
**Scene Description:** An innkeeper is pointing toward a small stable at the back. It looks cozy and has a warm light coming from inside.

### Page 11
**Text:** Inside the stable, it was quiet and peaceful. Friendly animals were resting there.
**Scene Description:** Inside the stable. A cow and some sheep are resting in the straw. Mary and Joseph are settling in.

### Page 12
**Text:** That very night, Baby Jesus was born! Mary wrapped Him in soft cloth and laid Him in a manger.
**Scene Description:** A close-up of Mary looking lovingly at Baby Jesus, who is tucked into the wooden manger filled with hay.

### Page 13
**Text:** Nearby, in the dark fields, some shepherds were watching their fluffy sheep.
**Scene Description:** A group of shepherds sitting around a small campfire at night. The sheep are huddled nearby.

### Page 14
**Text:** Suddenly, the sky filled with light! A bright Angel appeared, and the shepherds were very surprised.
**Scene Description:** The shepherds are looking up in wonder as a bright Angel appears in the sky above them.

### Page 15
**Text:** “Good news!” the Angel sang. “A special Baby is born in Bethlehem! Go and see Him.”
**Scene Description:** Many joyful Angels are now in the sky, singing and shining. The shepherds are smiling and pointing toward the town.

### Page 16
**Text:** The shepherds ran to the town. “Look!” they cried. “Just like the Angel said!”
**Scene Description:** The shepherds are entering the stable and looking at Baby Jesus in the manger. They look very happy.

### Page 17
**Text:** Far away in the East, three Wise Men saw a new, bright star in the sky.
**Scene Description:** The three Wise Men, dressed in their colorful robes, are standing on a balcony looking up at a very large, twinkling star.

### Page 18
**Text:** “That star will lead us to the new King!” the Wise Men said. They packed their bags and began their journey.
**Scene Description:** The Wise Men are riding on tall, friendly-looking camels, following the bright star across the desert.

### Page 19
**Text:** The star moved across the sky, leading them all the way to the little stable in Bethlehem.
**Scene Description:** A wide shot of the desert at night with the Wise Men on camels. The star is shining a bright beam of light down onto the stable in the distance.

### Page 20
**Text:** The Wise Men walked into the stable. They knelt down and gave Baby Jesus special gifts.
**Scene Description:** The Wise Men are inside the stable, presenting their ornate gift boxes to Mary, Joseph, and the Baby.

### Page 21
**Text:** There was gold, frankincense, and myrrh. The Wise Men were so happy to find the special Baby.
**Scene Description:** A close-up of one of the Wise Men opening a golden box to show a gift. Baby Jesus is reaching out a tiny hand.

### Page 22
**Text:** Everyone in the stable felt the wonder and love of that first Christmas night.
**Scene Description:** A group shot of Mary, Joseph, the shepherds, the Wise Men, and the animals all gathered around the manger.

### Page 23
**Text:** High in the sky, the big star kept shining, telling the whole world the good news.
**Scene Description:** An outside view of the stable under the giant, glowing star. The night is beautiful and blue.

### Page 24
**Text:** The first Christmas was a night of love and joy. And it all started with a tiny Baby in a manger.
**Scene Description:** Mary is holding Baby Jesus, and they are both surrounded by a warm, golden light.

**The End.**
