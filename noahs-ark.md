# Noah's Big Boat

## Character Descriptions

*   **Noah**: A kind, smiling old man with a long white beard and a friendly face. He wears a simple blue tunic and carries a wooden staff.
*   **Mrs. Noah**: A warm, grandmotherly figure with grey hair tied in a neat bun. She wears a green dress and always has a gentle smile.
*   **The Dove**: A small, bright white bird with a tiny yellow beak. She is gentle and fast.
*   **The Animals**: A variety of friendly animals including big grey elephants, tall spotted giraffes, and orange lions with fluffy manes. They all look happy and peaceful.

---

## Story Pages

### Page 1: Title Page
**Text:** Noah's Big Boat
**Scene Description:** Noah stands proudly in front of the massive wooden Ark. He has a long white beard and a friendly, welcoming smile.

### Page 2
**Text:** Long ago, there was a man named Noah. He was very kind and he loved God very much.
**Scene Description:** Noah is in his colorful garden, surrounded by flowers and butterflies. He is looking up at the bright blue sky with a peaceful expression.

### Page 3
**Text:** One day, God spoke to Noah. "Build a big, big boat," God said. "It will be a safe home for you and the animals."
**Scene Description:** Noah is listening intently, with one hand to his ear. A soft, warm light shines down from a fluffy cloud, representing God's message.

### Page 4
**Text:** Noah started to work right away. *Clunk, clunk, clunk!* He used big pieces of wood to build the boat.
**Scene Description:** Noah is holding a wooden mallet, working on a large wooden frame. The base of the Ark is starting to take shape, and there are wood shavings on the ground.

### Page 5
**Text:** Mrs. Noah helped too. She packed many baskets of yummy food for everyone to eat.
**Scene Description:** Mrs. Noah is in a cozy kitchen area, filling woven baskets with bright red apples, yellow grain, and green vegetables. She looks happy to be helping.

### Page 6
**Text:** Some people watched and laughed. "A boat on dry land?" they asked. But Noah just smiled. He knew God had a wonderful plan.
**Scene Description:** A few neighbors stand nearby, looking confused and pointing at the Ark. Noah is in the background, continuing his work with a steady smile.

### Page 7
**Text:** Soon, the big boat was finished. It was huge! It had a big wooden door and a little window at the very top.
**Scene Description:** The Ark is now completely finished. It is a large, sturdy wooden boat with three levels. It looks very strong and safe.

### Page 8
**Text:** Then, something amazing happened. From near and far, the animals began to arrive!
**Scene Description:** Noah stands at the bottom of a wide wooden ramp. In the distance, a long, colorful line of many different animals is approaching the Ark.

### Page 9
**Text:** They came two by two. Two big, grey elephants came *stomp, stomp, stomp* up the ramp.
**Scene Description:** Two large, friendly elephants with their trunks raised are walking up the ramp. Noah is waving a friendly hello to them.

### Page 10
**Text:** Two tall, spotted giraffes came *stretch, stretch, stretch* with their long necks.
**Scene Description:** Two giraffes with very long necks and bright orange spots are stepping carefully onto the ramp. Their heads reach high into the air.

### Page 11
**Text:** Two orange lions came *pad, pad, pad* with their soft paws. Every animal was invited to the safe home.
**Scene Description:** Two lions—one with a big, fluffy mane—are walking calmly toward the door. They look like large, friendly kittens.

### Page 12
**Text:** Birds of every color flew inside. *Flap, flap, flap!* The big boat was filling up with friends.
**Scene Description:** A beautiful swirl of colorful birds, like parrots, toucans, and blue jays, are flying through the air and entering the Ark's large door.

### Page 13
**Text:** Once everyone was safe and sound inside, the big wooden door slowly closed. 
**Scene Description:** A close-up of the Ark's large wooden door as it slowly swings shut. You can see the curious eyes of a few animals peeking through the gap.

### Page 14
**Text:** Then, the rain began to fall. *Pitter-patter, pitter-patter.* The big boat began to float on the water.
**Scene Description:** The Ark is now floating on a vast blue ocean. Gentle raindrops are falling from grey, puffy clouds. The boat looks very steady.

### Page 15
**Text:** It rained and it rained for a long time. But inside the boat, it was warm and very cozy.
**Scene Description:** A cross-section view of the Ark. On one level, different animals are huddled together on soft hay. It looks like a giant, comfortable bedroom.

### Page 16
**Text:** The lions napped. The elephants played. Noah and his family were safe and happy in their floating home.
**Scene Description:** Noah is sitting on a hay bale, gently petting a lion's mane. Mrs. Noah is nearby, feeding a tall giraffe a tasty green leaf.

### Page 17
**Text:** One day, the *pitter-patter* stopped. The sun began to peek through the clouds again.
**Scene Description:** Noah is looking out the small window at the very top of the Ark. The sky is changing from grey to a beautiful, soft yellow.

### Page 18
**Text:** Noah opened the window wide. He sent a little white dove to fly out and look for land.
**Scene Description:** Noah is holding the little white dove gently in his hands before letting her fly out of the window into the clear blue sky.

### Page 19
**Text:** The dove flew and flew, but she only saw water. She came back to the boat to rest with Noah.
**Scene Description:** The dove is flying back through the window. Noah reaches out his hand for her to land on. The world outside the window is still covered in water.

### Page 20
**Text:** A few days later, Noah sent the dove out again. "Find us a sign," he whispered.
**Scene Description:** The dove is flying away from the Ark again. This time, the sun is shining very brightly, making the water sparkle below her.

### Page 21
**Text:** This time, the dove returned with something special—a bright green olive branch! "Land is near!" Noah cheered.
**Scene Description:** A close-up of the dove's beak holding a small, fresh green olive branch with leaves. Noah's eyes are wide with excitement and joy.

### Page 22
**Text:** The big boat came to rest on the top of a high mountain. The sun was warm, and the water was going away.
**Scene Description:** The Ark has settled safely on the rocky peak of a mountain. Fresh green grass and small flowers are starting to grow all around the base of the boat.

### Page 23
**Text:** Everyone came out of the boat. The animals ran, hopped, and flew for joy! It was a brand new day.
**Scene Description:** All the animals are happily coming down the ramp. They are spreading out across the green mountain, jumping and playing in the sunshine.

### Page 24
**Text:** Look up! A beautiful rainbow stretched across the sky. It was God's colorful promise to always love the world.
**Scene Description:** A huge, vibrant rainbow with every color arcs across the entire page. Noah, Mrs. Noah, and many happy animals are looking up at it in wonder.

**The End.**
