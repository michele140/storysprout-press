# Jonah and the Big Fish Activity Book (Outline)

**Target Audience:** Ages 2-6
**Theme:** Bible Series - Listening, Second Chances, and the Sea

---

## Page-by-Page Outline (24 Pages)

### Page 1: Cover Page
*   **Activity:** Title and Illustration
*   **Description:** "Jonah and the Big Fish: A Bible Adventures Activity Book!" A large, happy illustration of Jonah waving from the beach, with the tail of the Big Fish splashing in the waves.

### Page 2: This Book Belongs To...
*   **Activity:** Name Plate
*   **Description:** A frame made of bubbles and seashells.
*   **Text:** "This Deep Sea Explorer is: [Name]"

### Page 3: Coloring Page - Jonah under the Tree
*   **Scene:** Jonah sitting peacefully under a big leafy tree.
*   **Text:** "Jonah is resting. Color the big green leaves!"

### Page 4: Tracing Activity - S-E-A
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the word "SEA".
*   **Text:** "Jonah went to the big blue SEA. Can you trace the word?"

### Page 5: Coloring Page - The Harbor
*   **Scene:** The busy harbor with the wooden boat and white sails.
*   **Text:** "Look at the big boat! Color the white sails."

### Page 6: Tracing Activity - Wavy Waves
*   **Activity:** Shape Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed wavy lines representing the ocean waves.
*   **Text:** "The water goes up and down. Trace the waves!"

### Page 7: Coloring Page - Jonah’s Nap
*   **Scene:** Jonah sleeping cozily on blankets inside the boat.
*   **Text:** "Shhh... Jonah is taking a nap. Color his warm blankets."

### Page 8: Simple Maze - Help the Boat find the Way
*   **Activity:** Maze.
*   **Description:** A simple path through some clouds to help the boat sail across the sea.
*   **Text:** "The boat needs to sail! Can you help it find the way?"

### Page 9: Coloring Page - The Stormy Sky
*   **Scene:** Dark clouds and rain falling on the boat.
*   **Text:** "The sky turned grey. Use your grey and blue crayons!"

### Page 10: Counting Activity - Raindrops
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** A picture of a cloud with raindrops to count.
*   **Text:** "How many raindrops can you see? Count them: [ ]"

### Page 11: Coloring Page - Splash!
*   **Scene:** Jonah falling into the water with lots of bubbles.
*   **Text:** "Splash! Jonah is in the water. Color the bubbly sea."

### Page 12: Tracing Activity - Bubble Bubbles
*   **Activity:** Shape Tracing.
*   **Description:** Different sizes of dashed circles representing bubbles.
*   **Text:** "The water is full of bubbles! Trace the circles."

### Page 13: Coloring Page - The Big Fish
*   **Scene:** The giant blue fish swimming toward Jonah.
*   **Text:** "Here comes the Big Fish! He has a very big smile."

### Page 14: Connect-the-Dots - The Friendly Fish
*   **Activity:** Connect-the-dots (Numbers 1-12).
*   **Description:** An outline of the Big Fish’s tail and body.
*   **Text:** "Connect the dots to see the Big Fish!"

### Page 15: Coloring Page - Cozy Inside
*   **Scene:** Jonah sitting inside the warm, cave-like belly of the fish.
*   **Text:** "It is warm and cozy inside the Big Fish."

### Page 16: Spot the Difference - Inside the Fish
*   **Activity:** Spot 3 Differences.
*   **Description:** Two pictures of Jonah praying inside the fish.
*   **Differences:** (1) A starfish on the wall, (2) The pattern on Jonah's robe, (3) A small glowing plant.

### Page 17: Coloring Page - Jonah Prays
*   **Scene:** Jonah kneeling and talking to God inside the fish.
*   **Text:** "Jonah said, 'I'm sorry.' God heard his prayer."

### Page 18: Tracing Activity - J-O-N-A-H
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the name "JONAH".
*   **Text:** "Can you trace Jonah's name?"

### Page 19: Coloring Page - Wiggle, Wiggle, Burp!
*   **Scene:** Jonah landing safely on the sandy beach.
*   **Text:** "Jonah is back on dry land! Color the yellow sand."

### Page 20: Matching Activity - Sea Friends
*   **Activity:** Matching.
*   **Description:** Drawing a line from an animal to its shadow (Fish, Crab, Starfish, Whale).
*   **Text:** "Can you match the sea friends to their shadows?"

### Page 21: Coloring Page - Nineveh
*   **Scene:** Jonah talking to a group of happy people in the city.
*   **Text:** "Jonah told everyone about God's love. Everyone is happy!"

### Page 22: Counting Activity - Happy Faces
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** A group of people smiling.
*   **Text:** "How many happy friends are there? Count them: [ ]"

### Page 23: Drawing Activity - My Favorite Fish
*   **Activity:** Creative Drawing.
*   **Description:** A blank space under the water.
*   **Text:** "What does your big fish look like? Draw it here!"

### Page 24: Sea Friend Certificate
*   **Activity:** Award Page.
*   **Description:** "Congratulations! [Name] is a Great Sea Friend!" Decorated with waves and a happy fish.
