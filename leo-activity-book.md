# Leo the Light-Up Firefly Activity Book (Outline)

**Target Audience:** Ages 2-6
**Theme:** Insects, Night Sky, Kindness, and Being Special

---

## Page-by-Page Outline (24 Pages)

### Page 1: Cover Page
*   **Activity:** Title and Illustration
*   **Description:** "Leo the Light-Up Firefly's Lantern Activity Book!" A large, smiling Leo glowing with a steady golden light.

### Page 2: This Book Belongs To...
*   **Activity:** Name Plate
*   **Description:** A frame decorated with stars and fireflies for the child to write/trace their name.

### Page 3: Coloring Page - Leo in the Meadow
*   **Scene:** Leo sitting on a blade of grass under a starry sky.
*   **Text:** "Leo is a special firefly. Color the beautiful night sky!"

### Page 4: Tracing Activity - F-I-R-E-F-L-Y
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the word "FIREFLY".
*   **Text:** "Leo is a firefly! Can you trace the word?"

### Page 5: Coloring Page - Firefly Disco
*   **Scene:** Many fireflies flashing their lights in the grass.
*   **Text:** "Blink! Flash! Glow! Color the busy fireflies."

### Page 6: Tracing Activity - Zigs and Zags
*   **Activity:** Line Tracing.
*   **Description:** Sharp "zig-zag" dashed lines representing Luna's flight path.
*   **Text:** "Luna is very fast! Trace her zig-zag flight."

### Page 7: Coloring Page - Meet Luna
*   **Scene:** Luna landing next to Leo, glowing brightly.
*   **Text:** "Luna is Leo's best friend. Color her bright yellow light."

### Page 8: Simple Maze - Find Grandfather Glow
*   **Activity:** Maze.
*   **Description:** Help Leo and Luna find the Great Mushroom where Grandfather Glow sits.
*   **Text:** "Help the friends find wise Grandfather Glow!"

### Page 9: Coloring Page - Grandfather Glow
*   **Scene:** Grandfather Glow sitting on top of a large mushroom.
*   **Text:** "Grandfather Glow is very wise. Color the giant mushroom!"

### Page 10: Tracing Activity - Round Mushrooms
*   **Activity:** Shape Tracing.
*   **Description:** Dashed outlines of various mushrooms.
*   **Text:** "Trace the mushroom shapes in the meadow."

### Page 11: Coloring Page - Bella the Beetle
*   **Scene:** Bella the Beetle waving an antenna at Leo.
*   **Text:** "Bella is a friendly beetle. Color her shiny purple shell!"

### Page 12: Connect-the-Dots - The Big Moon
*   **Activity:** Connect-the-dots (Numbers 1-10).
*   **Description:** An outline of a crescent moon.
*   **Text:** "What shines in the night sky? Connect the dots!"

### Page 13: Coloring Page - Baby Bunny in the Thorns
*   **Scene:** Barnaby the Baby Bunny looking scared in the bushes.
*   **Text:** "Barnaby is lost! Color the fluffy baby bunny."

### Page 14: Counting Activity - Thorny Branches
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** A picture with several thorny branches.
*   **Text:** "Count the thorns on the branch: [ ]"

### Page 15: Coloring Page - Leo's Steady Glow
*   **Scene:** Leo landing in front of the bunny and starting to glow.
*   **Text:** "Leo has a steady lantern-light. It feels warm and safe."

### Page 16: Tracing Activity - Soft Glow
*   **Activity:** Line Tracing.
*   **Description:** Soft, circular dashed lines around Leo.
*   **Text:** "Trace the circles of light around Leo."

### Page 17: Coloring Page - Lighting the Way
*   **Scene:** Leo flying slowly ahead of Barnaby through the grass.
*   **Text:** "Follow the light! Leo is showing the way home."

### Page 18: Spot the Difference - Bunny's Burrow
*   **Activity:** Spot 3 Differences.
*   **Description:** Two pictures of Barnaby entering his cozy burrow.
*   **Differences:** (1) A star in the sky, (2) A flower near the hole, (3) A leaf on the ground.

### Page 19: Coloring Page - Happy Reunion
*   **Scene:** Barnaby with his mama at the burrow.
*   **Text:** "Barnaby is home! Color the happy bunnies."

### Page 20: Tracing Activity - Path to the Burrow
*   **Activity:** Line Tracing.
*   **Description:** A path through the grass with dashed lines.
*   **Text:** "Trace the path back to the cozy home."

### Page 21: Coloring Page - Lantern Light
*   **Scene:** All fireflies gathered around Leo, admiring his light.
*   **Text:** "Everyone loves Leo's lantern-light! You are special, Leo."

### Page 22: Drawing Activity - My Night Light
*   **Activity:** Creative Drawing.
*   **Description:** A large blank area.
*   **Text:** "What helps YOU feel safe at night? Draw your favorite night-light here!"

### Page 23: Matching Activity - Friend Match
*   **Activity:** Matching.
*   **Description:** Match the character to their special trait (Leo -> Lantern, Luna -> Zig-zag, Bella -> Purple Shell, Grandfather -> Wise).

### Page 24: Meadow Hero Certificate
*   **Activity:** Award Page.
*   **Description:** "Congratulations! [Name] is a Brave Meadow Hero!" Decorated with fireflies and stars.
