# World Cup Series Book #8: LA Dreams Big

**Target Age:** 5-9 years
**Theme:** Glamour, creativity, inclusion, futuristic design.
**Characters:**
- **Chloe:** A creative 7-year-old from Inglewood who loves movies, fashion, and soccer.
- **Dad:** Chloe’s dad, a cinematographer who loves the "visuals" of everything.
- **The Infinity Giant:** A sleek, futuristic stadium character with a translucent "skin" and a heart made of a giant double-sided video ring (the Infinity Screen).
- **Team Japan:** The "Samurai Blue" team in their sharp blue and white kits.
