# World Cup Series Book #2: Philly's Big Game

**Target Age:** 5-9 years
**Theme:** Passion, perseverance, community, sportsmanship.
**Characters:**
- **Leo:** A spunky 8-year-old from Philadelphia who loves the energy of her city.
- **Uncle Joe:** Leo’s uncle, a legendary Philly sports fan who knows every chant.
- **The Linc:** A strong, winged stadium character made of dark steel and glass, with a glowing green heart.
- **Team Brazil:** The world-famous "Samba" soccer team in their iconic yellow jerseys.

## Page 2
**Visual:** Leo’s backyard in a row-home neighborhood. She’s practicing her footwork, weaving between orange cones. Uncle Joe is sitting on the back steps, tapping a rhythm on a small drum.
**Text:**
In Philadelphia, sports aren't just games—they’re part of the city’s heart! Leo had been practicing her "Samba" kicks all week. 

“Uncle Joe, do you think Brazil will play the Philly way today?” Leo asked, spinning the ball on her finger.

“The Philly way is with heart and hustle, Leo,” Uncle Joe laughed. “And tonight, the Linc is going to roar!”

## Page 3
**Visual:** Leo and Uncle Joe on the Broad Street Line (subway). The car is packed with fans. Some are wearing Brazil jerseys, some have Philly scarves. Everyone is smiling and chatting.
**Text:**
To get to the game, they hopped on the Broad Street Line. The subway car was a sea of yellow, green, and Philly blue. 

“Go Brazil!” someone shouted.
“Go Philly!” Leo shouted back, making everyone cheer. 

Philadelphia fans are famous for being the loudest and most passionate in the world. Leo was ready to prove it!

## Page 4

As they walked toward Lincoln Financial Field, the "Linc" stood tall. It looked like a modern fortress of steel and glass. 

But the Linc wasn't just a building. He was a protector of the game! He had "eyes" made of high-tech cameras and a heart that glowed with the power of the sun, thanks to thousands of solar panels on his roof.

Brazil moved the ball with "Samba" style—dancing and spinning past the defenders. Italy was strong and organized, like a wall of blue. 

“Let’s go, Brazil! Let’s go, Philly!” Leo chanted, keeping the beat on the drum. *Thump-thump-CLAP!*
