# World Cup Series Book #7: Texas-Sized Soccer

**Target Age:** 5-9 years
**Theme:** Grandeur, precision, hospitality, big dreams.
**Characters:**
- **Emma:** A spirited 8-year-old from Arlington who loves everything "Texas-sized."
- **Grandpa Jim:** Emma’s grandfather, a former high school coach with a deep love for sports and Texas history.
- **Titan Tex:** A massive, gleaming stadium character with a curved silver roof and a giant "all-seeing" video-board eye.
- **Team Germany:** The "Die Mannschaft" team in their precise white and black kits.
