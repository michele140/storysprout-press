# World Cup Series Book #10: Seattle's Roar

**Target Age:** 5-9 years
**Theme:** Passion, nature, community, the power of a collective voice.
**Characters:**
- **Sam:** An 8-year-old from Seattle who loves coffee (decaf!), hiking, and has the loudest cheering voice in the Pacific Northwest.
- **Dad:** Sam’s dad, a ferry captain who loves the water and the Emerald City.
- **The Roaring Giant:** A rugged stadium character with a body made of green and blue glass, two soaring white arches for eyebrows, and a voice that sounds like a thousand fans cheering at once.
- **Team Netherlands:** The "Oranje" team in their bright orange kits.
