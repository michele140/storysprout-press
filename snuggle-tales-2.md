## BOOK 2: CANDYLAND CHAOS

**FORMAT:** Dork Diaries style — mix of text and black & white illustrations on every page
**TONE:** First-person, conversational, diary-like. Shifting perspectives.
**SETTING:** Candyland (Hawaii)

# [PAGE 2 - CHAPTER 1: LANDING IN CANDYLAND]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy the Panda is face-down in a pile of what looks like pink sand. Her floppy ears are covered in sprinkles. She’s looking up with one eye open.]**

Dear Diary,

I think my fluff is stuck. 

Actually, I *know* my fluff is stuck. My beautiful, black-and-white, Smudge Panda fur is currently glued to a beach made of... **POPPING CANDY**.

*CRACKLE! POP! SNAP!*

Every time I move a paw, the ground explodes with tiny little "pops." It’s very startling. It’s also very pink. 

I don't think we’re in JellyCat City anymore. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A tiny doodle of a panda head with "???" bubbles above it.]**

Remember how I said I wanted an adventure? Well, Pandy, you got one. A very, very sticky one.

---

# [PAGE 3]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty the Elephant is sitting nearby, trying to blow a bubble with her trunk. A giant pink bubble has formed and is bigger than her head.]**

"HONK! Pandy! Look! I’m a balloon!"

Dirty is clearly handling the transition better than I am. She didn't just land on the beach; she landed in a puddle of "Bubblegum Blue" ocean water. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Luna the Rabbit is zooming around in circles, leaving a trail of glittery dust behind her. She looks like she’s had way too much sugar.]**

Luna is... well, Luna is vibrating. 

"STARS! PANDY! THE TREES ARE MADE OF GUMMY BEARS! AND THE ROCKS ARE JAWBREAKERS! AND I THINK I JUST RAN OVER A LICORICE CRAB!" 

*BOING! BOING! BOING!*

Luna’s "Hoppy" legs have officially reached "Super-Sonic" speed.

---

# [PAGE 4]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy the Lamb is hugging a palm tree. The tree trunk is a giant chocolate bar (Kit-Kat style) and the leaves are green taffy.]**

Daisy is just happy to have something to hug. 

"It’s so soft, Pandy," she whispered. "And it smells like cookies." 

She’s right. The air doesn't smell like the Bamboo Forest or the Wild Meadow. It smells like a bakery exploded inside a fruit stand. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A map-style doodle of the immediate area: "CHOCOLATE COVE", "GUMMY GROVE", and "SYRUP SPRINGS".]**

I stood up (finally untangled my tail from a stubborn marshmallow root) and looked around. 

We were on an island. A beautiful, tropical, absolutely ridiculous island. 

---

# [PAGE 5]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy looking at the horizon. There’s a giant volcano in the distance made of dark chocolate with white-chocolate-snow on top. Lava made of glowing orange caramel is flowing down the side.]**

"Okay, team," I said, trying to sound like a Brave Leader (even though my legs were still wobbly). "We need a plan."

Dirty stopped blowing bubbles. Luna stopped vibrating for exactly three seconds. Daisy let go of the chocolate tree.

"Plan?" Luna asked. "Why do we need a plan when we have **CANDY VOLCANOES**?"

**[ILLUSTRATION: A doodle of the volcano with a "DANGER: DELICIOUS" sign.]**

"Because," I explained, "we don't know where we are. Or how to get home. Or if the licorice crabs are friendly."

---

# [PAGE 6]

**[ILLUSTRATION: A close-up of a licorice crab. It has eight legs made of black twists and two pincers made of red gummy hearts. It’s wearing a tiny grass skirt.]**

As if on cue, the licorice crab scuttled past my paw. It did a little hula dance and then pointed its gummy pincer toward the jungle. 

"See?" Dirty trumpeted. "He’s giving us directions! He says 'Go that way to find the snacks!'"

"I don't think he’s talking about snacks, Dirty," I said, looking at the map I’d brought from Book 1. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy looking at her old map. It’s blank. The gold letters have vanished and been replaced by a single word: "ALOHA".]**

*EEK!* My map is glitching! 

---

# [PAGE 7]

**[ILLUSTRATION: The four friends walking into the "Gummy Grove". The ground is made of soft green fruit-leather. Huge gummy bears are hanging from the trees like fruit.]**

We decided to follow the crab. Mostly because we didn't have any other ideas, and also because Luna was already halfway into the jungle. 

*Squish. Squish. Squish.*

The ground in the Gummy Grove is very bouncy. It’s like walking on a giant trampoline made of jam. 

"I could get used to this," Dirty said, using her trunk to pluck a giant orange gummy bear from a branch. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty with a giant gummy bear in her trunk. The bear looks surprised.]**

"Dirty! Don't eat the locals!" Daisy cried. 

---

# [PAGE 8]

**[ILLUSTRATION: A wide shot of the jungle. There are orchids made of rock candy and vines made of strawberry laces.]**

The jungle was even crazier than the beach. There were birds that looked like flying macaroons and butterflies with wings made of sugar-paper. 

It was beautiful. But it was also... LOUD. 

*GIGGLE-GIGGLE-SNORT!*

*Hee-hee-hee!*

The sounds weren't coming from the macaroon-birds. They were coming from behind a massive Pineapple-Upside-Down-Cake bush.

**[ILLUSTRATION: Four floppy ears (two peach, two purple) are peeking out from behind the cake bush.]**

---

# [PAGE 9]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy, Dirty, Luna, and Daisy are huddling together, looking at the cake bush.]**

"Who’s there?" I called out. My voice was a little shaky. "We are the Smudges! We are very soft and we come in peace!"

Luna hopped forward. "And we have a map that says ALOHA!"

The giggling stopped. 

Then, two heads popped up from behind the cake. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Two bunnies — one peach-colored and one purple — are staring at the Smudges. They both have giant, mischievous grins.]**

"ALOHA!" they shouted at the same time. 

---

# [PAGE 10]

**[ILLUSTRATION: The peach bunny (Apricot) does a backflip. The purple bunny (Lavender) does a cartwheel. They land perfectly in front of Pandy.]**

"Welcome to Candyland!" the peach one said. "I’m **Apricot**!"

"And I’m **Lavender**!" the purple one added. "We’re the Sunshine Sisters!"

They looked like Luna, but... *different*. They weren't just curious; they looked like they were made of pure energy and a little bit of trouble. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A doodle of Apricot with a "LEADER" crown and Lavender with a "GENIUS" lightbulb.]**

"Are you lost?" Apricot asked, her floppy peach ears twitching. "Or are you here for the Great Taffy Tug-of-War?"

---

# [PAGE 11]

"We're... lost," I admitted. 

"Great!" Lavender cheered. "Lost stuffies are the best. They’re always up for an adventure! And we have a HUGE one planned today."

"Wait," I said, feeling my panda-nerves kicking in. "What kind of adventure? Is it safe? Does it involve log bridges?"

Apricot laughed. It sounded like bells ringing. "Safe? Probably not! Fun? DEFINITELY! We’re going to the Caramel Crater!"

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy's face looking very worried. Dirty and Luna are looking very excited.]**

"CARAMEL CRATER!" Luna screamed. "DOES IT HAVE BOUNCY WALLS?"

---

# [PAGE 12]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Apricot and Lavender grabbing Luna and Dirty’s paws. They are leading them deeper into the candy jungle.]**

"It has the *bounciest* walls in all of Hawaii-land!" Apricot said. 

Before I could say "Wait for the panda!", the sisters were off. They moved through the candy vines like they were part of the jungle itself. 

Luna was right behind them, skipping so high I thought she might never come down. 

"Come on, Pandy! Daisy!" Dirty trumpeted. "The bunnies have the snacks!"

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy and Pandy trailing behind, looking at each other with "Here we go again" faces.]**

---

# [PAGE 13]

**[ILLUSTRATION: The group crossing a river of sparkling lemonade. Lavender is using a giant candy-cane as a walking stick.]**

I have to admit, Lavender and Apricot know this place well. They showed us how to avoid the "Sour Patch Pits" (very prickly) and where to find the "Sweet-Steam Vents" (great for fluffing fur).

"So," Lavender said, hopping backward so she could talk to me. "Where did you guys come from? You don't smell like sugar."

"We’re from the Bamboo Forest," I said. "And the Savannah. And the Meadow. We met in JellyCat City."

"JellyCat City?" Apricot tilted her head. "Is that near the Big Rock Candy Mountain?"

---

# [PAGE 14]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy trying to explain the map to the sisters. She’s holding the "ALOHA" map.]**

"We don't know," I said. "We fell into a pile of leaves and ended up here."

Lavender looked at our map. She tapped her chin with a purple paw. 

"Hmm. This looks like a **Shift-Map**," she whispered. "It changes based on where the magic is strongest. Right now, it wants you here!"

"But why?" Daisy asked. 

Apricot grinned and pointed through the trees. "Maybe because Hippy needs some new friends to splash with!"

**[ILLUSTRATION: A giant purple shadow is visible through the trees near a bubbling brown swamp.]**

---

# [PAGE 15]

**[ILLUSTRATION: A giant purple hippo (Hippy) is sitting in a pool of liquid chocolate (the swamp). He’s wearing a flower lei around his neck and is splashing happily.]**

"HIPPY!" Apricot and Lavender shouted. 

The giant purple hippo looked up. He had the biggest, friendliest eyes I’ve ever seen. 

"Friends?" Hippy asked. His voice was deep and slow, like honey dripping off a spoon. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Hippy waving a giant purple paw. Chocolate syrup is dripping everywhere.]**

"New friends!" Lavender confirmed. 

Hippy smiled. "Aloha, new friends. You’re just in time for the chocolate bath!"

---

# [PAGE 16]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty the Elephant is already chest-deep in the chocolate swamp next to Hippy. They are doing a "trunk-high-five."]**

"Dirty! No!" I cried. But it was too late. 

Dirty and Hippy were already best friends. They were both big, grey (or purple), and apparently shared a deep love for sitting in dessert. 

"It’s warm, Pandy!" Dirty called out. "It’s like a hug from a brownie!"

Luna was busy trying to see if she could hop *across* the chocolate without sinking. (Spoiler alert: She couldn't.)

**[ILLUSTRATION: Luna’s white tail is now a chocolate-covered-rabbit-tail.]**

---

# [PAGE 17]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy and Daisy sitting on the edge of the swamp, dipping their paws in tentatively. Lavender and Apricot are doing "cannonballs" into the chocolate.]**

"Is it always like this here?" Daisy asked Hippy. 

"Mostly," Hippy said, leaning his head back into the syrup. "We eat, we splash, we sleep. And sometimes, we have to run away from Winky’s plans."

"Winky?" I asked. "Who is Winky?"

As if on cue, a loud, fast-talking voice rang out from the chocolate-palm trees. 

"ALRIGHT! LISTEN UP, PEOPLE! THE MAYOR OF NYC IS IN THE HOUSE AND I HAVE THE PLAN OF THE CENTURY!"

---

# [PAGE 18]

**[ILLUSTRATION: A small, very pink pig (Winky) is standing on a rock. She’s wearing a tiny pair of sunglasses and a mini backpack with "NYC" on it. She’s holding a candy-cane like a microphone.]**

"Winky!" Apricot groaned (but she was still smiling). 

"Don't 'Winky' me, Apricot!" the pig said, talking at 100 miles per hour. "We have an island to run! We have candy-canes to harvest! We have... who are these guys?"

She pointed her candy-cane at us. Her sunglasses slid down her snout. 

"They’re the Smudges," Lavender explained. "They’re from... somewhere else."

**[ILLUSTRATION: Winky squinting at Pandy. Pandy is trying to look "Big City Cool."]**

---

# [PAGE 19]

"Somewhere else, huh?" Winky stepped closer. "Do they have subways? Do they have skyscrapers? Do they have pizzas the size of manhole covers?"

"We have bamboo," I said quietly. 

Winky blinked. "Bamboo? Is that a tech startup? Never heard of it. But listen, Pandy-Panda, you’re in Candyland now. And Winky is the boss of 'Getting Things Done.'"

**[ILLUSTRATION: Winky doing a sassy pose with one hoof on her hip.]**

"Anyway," she continued, "I need four volunteers for my 'Licorice Zip-Line' project. Who’s in?"

Luna, Dirty, and the bunny sisters all screamed: "MEEEEE!"

---

# [PAGE 20]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy looking at the reader with a "What have I gotten into?" expression. In the background, Winky is giving orders to a very confused Dirty.]**

So, Diary, that’s how our first day in Candyland ended. 

We have four new friends:
1. **Apricot** (The Brave Bunny)
2. **Lavender** (The Clever Bunny)
3. **Hippy** (The Chocolate Hippo)
4. **Winky** (The NYC Pig-Boss)

And we are currently helping Winky build a zip-line made of strawberry laces. 

I really hope my fluff is washable. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A tiny doodle of a "To-Do" list: 1. Don't fall. 2. Don't eat the zip-line. 3. Find more bamboo.]**

Goodnight, Diary. I think tomorrow is going to be very... chaotic.

# [PAGE 21 - CHAPTER 2: APRICOT'S STORY]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Full-page title illustration. APRICOT THE BUNNY is surfing on a giant waffle-cone surfboard. The waves are made of blue-raspberry soda. She’s doing a "hang ten" with her front paws.]**

Dear Diary,

(Wait, is that how you start these things? Pandy told me I should write down my thoughts so I don't forget the fun stuff. But I NEVER forget the fun stuff!)

My name is Apricot. I’m a Hawaii bunny. I’m peach-colored, I’m fast, and I’m 100% more adventurous than that white rabbit, Luna. (Don't tell her I said that. Actually, do tell her. We love a good race!)

Today was **EPIC**. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Apricot doing a "Shaka" sign with her paw. 🤙]**

Not only did we have perfect soda-waves, but four new stuffies literally fell out of the sky!

---

# [PAGE 22]

Lavender and I were just minding our own business, hiding behind the Pineapple-Cake bushes (we were planning a surprise "Taffy-Attack" on Hippy), when we heard a giant **THUMP**.

"Did a marshmallow meteor hit the island?" Lavender asked. 

"Let's go find out!" I shouted. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Apricot and Lavender zooming through the bushes, their ears flapping like capes.]**

We found them on Popping-Candy Beach. They looked so... *clean*. 

Well, except for the elephant. He was already trying to eat the sand. (Relatable. Popping candy is delicious.)

---

# [PAGE 23]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Apricot looking at Pandy. Pandy looks like she’s trying to be very serious and "grown-up."]**

The panda, Pandy, is the funny one. She acts like she’s scared of everything, but I saw her rescue a ladybug from a syrup-puddle. That’s a "Brave Heart" move right there!

And Luna! My new best friend! She can hop almost as high as I can. Almost. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A doodle of a "Hop-O-Meter". Apricot is at the top, Luna is just a tiny bit lower.]**

"Lavender!" I whispered while we were leading them to the swamp. "We have to show them the Caramel Crater. It’s the best place on the island!"

"But Apricot," Lavender said, her purple ears drooping. "Winky told us to stay away from the crater. She said the caramel is 'unstable'."

---

# [PAGE 24]

"Winky says everything is 'unstable' if she didn't build it herself!" I laughed. 

Lavender grinned. She’s the smart one, but she can't resist a good risk. 

"You're right," Lavender said. "Let's take the Smudges to the crater! It’ll be a 'Welcome to the Island' party!"

**[ILLUSTRATION: Lavender and Apricot high-fiving. A doodle of a "Danger" sign with a smiley face over it.]**

Of course, we had to stop by Winky’s first. Winky is a pig with a mission. She moved here from a place called "The Big Apple." I think that’s a land made of giant fruit? Sounds amazing.

---

# [PAGE 25]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Winky trying to explain a blueprint to a very distracted Dirty.]**

Winky is always trying to "improve" things. 

"Apricot! Lavender!" Winky shouted when she saw us. "I need more power! This zip-line needs a motor! Get me some Soda-Springs batteries, stat!"

Winky is bossy, but she makes us laugh. And she has the best sunglasses on the island. 

"Can't now, Winks!" I shouted back. "We’re on a 'New Friend Orientation Tour'!"

**[ILLUSTRATION: Apricot sticking her tongue out playfully at Winky.]**

"Orientation? Is there a fee for that? I should charge a fee!" Winky yelled after us. 

Typical Winky.

---

# [PAGE 26]

**[ILLUSTRATION: The whole group (8 friends) standing at the edge of a giant, glowing orange crater. The smell of burnt sugar is in the air.]**

We finally made it. The Caramel Crater. 

It’s a giant bowl in the middle of the island filled with thick, gooey, bubbling caramel. And the best part? It’s **HOT**. Like a giant hot tub made of candy.

"WHOA," Luna said. 

"Is it... safe?" Pandy asked (classic Pandy). 

"It’s perfectly safe!" I said, stepping onto a floating marshmallow-raft. "As long as you don't stay in too long. Otherwise, you turn into a Praline."

**[ILLUSTRATION: A doodle of a "Stuffy-Praline" — a panda covered in hard candy.]**

---

# [PAGE 27]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Hippy and Dirty are already in the caramel. They look like they are in heaven.]**

"This is better than mud!" Hippy rumbled. 

"It’s like a warm bath but you can eat the bubbles!" Dirty added. 

I looked at Lavender. She was already setting up a game of "Crater-Ball" using a giant gummy grape. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Lavender holding the gummy grape. She’s wearing a headband made of licorice.]**

"Alright, Smudges!" Lavender shouted. "Local Bunnies vs. New Friends! Let the games begin!"

It was the most fun we’d had all day. Until Winky showed up. 

---

# [PAGE 28]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Winky is standing on the rim of the crater, looking furious. She’s holding a megaphone.]**

"WHAT DID I TELL YOU ABOUT THE CRATER?" Winky’s voice echoed through the jungle. 

"Uh-oh," Lavender whispered. 

"GET OUT OF THERE! NOW! THE CARAMEL IS OVER-HEATING! WE HAVE A LAVA-LERT! A LAVA-LERT!" 

*GLUB. GLUB. GLUB.*

Suddenly, the caramel started to bubble really fast. Like, *really* fast. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Giant caramel bubbles are popping and spraying orange goo everywhere.]**

---

# [PAGE 29]

**[ILLUSTRATION: The friends scrambling to get out of the crater. Pandy is pulling Daisy up a marshmallow-bank. Lavender is helping Winky.]**

"MOVE! MOVE! MOVE!" Winky screamed. 

We all scrambled out just as a giant fountain of caramel shot fifty feet into the air. 

*SPLUTTER!*

"My fur!" Luna cried. "I’m an orange rabbit now!"

"You look like a carrot, Luna!" Lavender joked, even though we were all running for our lives. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: The group running through the candy jungle, pursued by a slow-moving wave of caramel lava.]**

"To the beach!" I yelled. "The soda-waves will wash it off!"

---

# [PAGE 30]

**[ILLUSTRATION: The 8 friends jumping into the blue-raspberry ocean. Splash! Blue water is flying everywhere.]**

*SHHHHHHHH!*

The cold soda-water felt amazing. The sticky caramel washed away, and we all bobbed in the waves like eight little corks. 

"That," Pandy said, coughing up a bit of blue raspberry foam, "was... intense."

"Welcome to Candyland, Pandy!" I laughed. 

Winky paddled over on her NYC backpack. "That’s it. New rule. No crater without a permit. And the permit costs three peanuts."

**[ILLUSTRATION: Winky looking very serious while floating in the blue water.]**

---

# [PAGE 31]

**[ILLUSTRATION: The group sitting on the beach at sunset. They are all blue-tinted from the water. They look exhausted but happy.]**

We spent the rest of the evening drying off in the warm Hawaiian breeze. 

I’ve lived on this island my whole life, but I’ve never seen it so full of life. Having the Smudges here is going to change everything. 

I think we’re going to be great friends. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Apricot and Pandy sharing a piece of pineapple-cake. A doodle of a heart with "SISTERS" written in it.]**

Goodnight, Diary. (Pandy, you were right, this writing thing is kind of cool!)

# [PAGE 32 - CHAPTER 3: HIPPY'S STORY]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Full-page title illustration. HIPPY THE HIPPO is lying on his back in the "Chocolate Swamp". He’s using his big belly as a drum and playing a rhythm with his paws. A group of macaroon-birds are chirping along.]**

Dear Diary,

(Apricot said I should do this. I told her I’m too slow at writing, but she gave me a giant chocolate-dipped banana to start. So here I am.)

My name is Hippy. I am a Smudge Hippo. I am purple, I am round, and I am very, very relaxed. 

People think hippos are scary. But I’m not scary. I’m just... hungry. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A doodle of Hippy with a thought-bubble showing a pizza.]**

Today was the most active day I’ve had in three years. Usually, my biggest decision is: "Left side of the swamp or right side of the swamp?"

Today, I had to save an elephant.

---

# [PAGE 33]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty the Elephant is stuck in a thick patch of "Taffy-Tar" at the edge of the swamp. He’s looking very embarrassed.]**

I was just finishing my morning chocolate-nap when I heard a very loud *HONK*.

It wasn't a bird. It was a grey stuffy with a trunk. 

"HEEEELP!" the grey one trumpeted. "I tried to pick a taffy-flower and now I AM the taffy-flower!"

He was stuck. Really stuck. The Taffy-Tar is no joke. It’s like super-glue but it smells like strawberries. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty looking like a grey statue with strawberry-colored feet.]**

---

# [PAGE 34]

I waddled over. It took me a while (I’m a hippo, not a racecar). 

"Don't worry, friend," I said. "Hippy is here. I have a 'Belly-Barge' move that will get you right out."

"A what?" the elephant asked. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Hippy doing a "Belly-Barge" — sliding his big purple tummy into the taffy-tar to break it up.]**

*CRUNCH. SQUISH. POP!*

Dirty flew out of the tar and landed right in the chocolate swamp. 

*SPLASH!*

"Chocolate!" Dirty cheered. "My favorite food and my favorite bath!"

---

# [PAGE 35]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Hippy and Dirty sitting side-by-side in the swamp. They are both covered in chocolate and looking very content.]**

"I’m Dirty," the elephant said. 

"I can see why," I said. 

We talked for a long time. Dirty told me about the "Sunny Savannah." It sounds very dry. I don't think I’d like it there. Not enough chocolate rivers. 

"You should meet my friends," Dirty said. "There’s a panda who is always worried, a rabbit who never stops moving, and a lamb who is basically a cloud."

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty drawing Pandy, Luna, and Daisy in the chocolate with his trunk.]**

---

# [PAGE 36]

Just then, the "Sunshine Sisters" arrived with the rest of the gang. 

Apricot and Lavender are always moving. They make my head spin. But they brought the others, just like Dirty said. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy and Daisy standing on the shore, looking at the chocolate swamp with big eyes.]**

Pandy looked at my swamp like it was a giant mud-puddle. 

"Hi, Pandy," I said. "Want a chocolate-banana?"

I pulled one out from behind my ear. (I always keep a spare). 

Pandy smiled. It was a nice smile. "Thank you, Hippy. I think I’m going to like it here."

---

# [PAGE 37]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Winky the Pig is trying to measure Hippy's back with a tape-measure. Hippy is looking very confused.]**

Then there’s Winky. Winky is... a lot. 

"Hippy! Buddy! Pal!" Winky said, talking so fast I almost missed the words. "I have a business proposition for you. How would you like to be the official 'Candyland Ferry'?"

"A ferry?" I asked. 

"Yeah! You’re big, you float, you’re purple! You’re perfect! We’ll put a little bench on your back, charge two gumdrops a ride, and we’ll be millionaires by Tuesday!"

**[ILLUSTRATION: A doodle of Winky’s "Hippy-Ferry" design — Hippy with a tiny bus on his back.]**

---

# [PAGE 38]

"I don't know, Winky," I said. "That sounds like a lot of work."

"Work? It’s not work! It’s 'Passive Income'!" Winky shouted. 

Dirty looked interested. "Can I be the first mate? I can blow the horn!" 

*HONKKKK!*

"Sold!" Winky high-fived Dirty. 

Pandy just sighed and went back to writing in her diary. I think Pandy and I are going to get along just fine. We both appreciate a good sit. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Hippy and Pandy sitting on a log together. Pandy is writing, Hippy is eating a banana.]**

---

# [PAGE 39]

**[ILLUSTRATION: The 8 friends helping Winky harvest "Licorice Logs" from the forest. It looks like a lot of work.]**

Winky’s plans always involve moving things. 

"Come on, team! Lift with your legs, not your fluff!" Winky barked. 

Even though it was hard work, it was fun being together. Pandy and Daisy were the 'Licorice Wrappers.' Luna and the bunnies were the 'Scouts.' Hippy and Dirty were the 'Heavy Lifters.' 

And Winky was the 'Chief Visionary Officer.' (That means she sat on a rock and pointed.)

**[ILLUSTRATION: A doodle of Winky pointing her candy-cane at a pile of licorice.]**

---

# [PAGE 40]

**[ILLUSTRATION: The group sitting around a campfire made of "Glow-Grains" (glowing cereal). They are roasting marshmallows.]**

At the end of the day, we were all tired. Winky even stopped talking for five minutes. 

"This is nice," Daisy said, leaning her head on Hippy’s shoulder. 

"It’s better than nice," I said. "It’s Candyland."

I looked at the stars. They looked like sugar-sprinkles tonight. I hope the Smudges stay for a long time. The swamp was a bit lonely before they got here. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A doodle of the 8 friends' silhouettes against the glowing fire.]**

Goodnight, Diary. (Tell Apricot I finished the banana!)

# [PAGE 41 - CHAPTER 4: WINKY'S STORY]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Full-page title illustration. WINKY THE PIG is standing on top of a giant "Candy-Crush" skyscraper (a tall stack of sugar cubes). She’s looking out over the island through a pair of binoculars made of toilet-paper rolls. She looks like a mogul.]**

Dear Diary,
