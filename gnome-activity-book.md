# Pip the Garden Gnome Activity Book (Outline)

**Target Audience:** Ages 2-6
**Theme:** Gardens, Gnomes, Kindness, and Magic

---

## Page-by-Page Outline (24 Pages)

### Page 1: Cover Page
*   **Activity:** Title and Illustration
*   **Description:** "Pip the Garden Gnome's Magical Surprise Activity Book!" Pip holding a tiny blue flower and smiling.

### Page 2: This Book Belongs To...
*   **Activity:** Name Plate
*   **Description:** A frame made of colorful flowers and leaves for the child to write/trace their name.

### Page 3: Coloring Page - Pip the Gnome
*   **Scene:** Pip standing under a giant rhubarb leaf.
*   **Text:** "Pip is a tiny helper. Color his red pointy hat!"

### Page 4: Tracing Activity - G-N-O-M-E
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the word "GNOME".
*   **Text:** "Can you trace the word? Pip is a gnome."

### Page 5: Coloring Page - Pip's Gourd House
*   **Scene:** The close-up of the hollowed-out gourd house with a little door.
*   **Text:** "Pip lives in a cozy gourd house. Color his tiny home."

### Page 6: Tracing Activity - Leaf Veins
*   **Activity:** Shape Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed outlines of hosta and rhubarb leaves.
*   **Text:** "The garden has big, green leaves. Trace the shapes!"

### Page 7: Coloring Page - Mr. McGregor
*   **Scene:** The kindly gardener sitting on his bench.
*   **Text:** "Mr. McGregor loves his garden. Color his big straw hat."

### Page 8: Simple Maze - Find the Trowel
*   **Activity:** Maze.
*   **Description:** Help Pip find the trowel hidden under a pile of leaves.
*   **Text:** "Where is the trowel? Help Pip find the missing tool!"

### Page 9: Coloring Page - Bella the Butterfly
*   **Scene:** Bella landing on a daisy and talking to Pip.
*   **Text:** "Bella is Pip's best friend. Color her beautiful yellow wings."

### Page 10: Tracing Activity - Butterfly Flight
*   **Activity:** Line Tracing.
*   **Description:** Loops and swirls representing a butterfly's path.
*   **Text:** "Bella loves to fly! Trace her flight path."

### Page 11: Coloring Page - Tiptoe the Mouse
*   **Scene:** Tiptoe carrying a small item on his back.
*   **Text:** "Tiptoe is a fast helper. Color the tiny field mouse."

### Page 12: Connect-the-Dots - Garden Trowel
*   **Activity:** Connect-the-dots (Numbers 1-10).
*   **Description:** An outline of a garden trowel.
*   **Text:** "What tool did Mr. McGregor lose? Connect the dots!"

### Page 13: Coloring Page - Filling the Acorn Cap
*   **Scene:** Pip catching water drops in a large acorn cap.
*   **Text:** "Pip is using an acorn cap! Color the sparkling water drops."

### Page 14: Counting Activity - Red Roses
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** A picture of a rosebush with several roses.
*   **Text:** "How many roses are blooming? Count them: [ ]"

### Page 15: Coloring Page - Watering the Roses
*   **Scene:** Pip climbing a branch to pour water on the soil.
*   **Text:** "Drip, drop, drip! Pip is giving the roses a drink."

### Page 16: Tracing Activity - Water Drops
*   **Activity:** Shape Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed raindrop shapes.
*   **Text:** "Trace the water drops for the thirsty plants."

### Page 17: Coloring Page - The Happy Gardener
*   **Scene:** Mr. McGregor smelling a rose and looking happy.
*   **Text:** "A magical surprise! Color the beautiful red rose."

### Page 18: Spot the Difference - Garden Bench
*   **Activity:** Spot 3 Differences.
*   **Description:** Two pictures of the garden bench with seeds on it.
*   **Differences:** (1) A packet of seeds, (2) A small snail, (3) The gardener's glasses.

### Page 19: Coloring Page - Moonlight Garden
*   **Scene:** The garden glowing under the silver moon.
*   **Text:** "The garden looks magical at night. Color the big moon."

### Page 20: Tracing Activity - Grass Blades
*   **Activity:** Line Tracing.
*   **Description:** Various lengths of vertical/slanted dashed lines.
*   **Text:** "The grass is soft and tall. Trace the lines!"

### Page 21: Coloring Page - Bedtime for Gnomes
*   **Scene:** Pip hanging his hat on a peg inside his gourd house.
*   **Text:** "Sweet dreams, Pip. Color the cozy bedroom."

### Page 22: Drawing Activity - My Secret Gift
*   **Activity:** Creative Drawing.
*   **Description:** A large blank area with a "gift wrap" border.
*   **Text:** "What would YOU give as a surprise? Draw a magical gift here!"

### Page 23: Matching Activity - Helper Match
*   **Activity:** Matching.
*   **Description:** Match the helper to their job (Pip -> Watering, Tiptoe -> Carrying, Bella -> Spotting).

### Page 24: Secret Garden Helper Certificate
*   **Activity:** Award Page.
*   **Description:** "Congratulations! [Name] is a Certified Secret Garden Helper!" Decorated with gnomes and ladybugs.
