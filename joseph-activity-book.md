# Activity Book Outline: Joseph's Colorful Coat (Bible Adventures)

**Series:** Bible Adventures (Series 2)
**Book Title:** Joseph's Colorful Coat
**Target Age:** 2-6 years
**Theme:** Family, dreams, God's big plan, forgiveness.
**Total Pages:** 24

---

## Page-by-Page Outline

**Page 1: Title Page**
- **Activity:** "This Book Belongs To" with Joseph wearing a small version of his coat.
- **Visual:** A frame for the child's name.

**Page 2: Introduction / Story Recap**
- **Activity:** "Color the Dreamer."
- **Visual:** Joseph sleeping and dreaming of bright stars.

**Page 3: Coloring Page - Joseph's Special Coat**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** A large, detailed drawing of Joseph in his long coat. The coat has many different sections for different colors.

**Page 4: Tracing Page - Color Words**
- **Activity:** Trace the words: RED, BLUE, YELLOW, GREEN.
- **Visual:** Each word next to a small splat of that color.

**Page 5: Pattern Activity - Finish the Coat**
- **Activity:** "Color the stripes in a pattern: Red, Blue, Red, Blue..."
- **Visual:** A close-up of the coat sleeves with dotted lines.

**Page 6: Coloring Page - Joseph and His Father**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** Jacob giving the coat to Joseph. Both look very happy.

**Page 7: Maze - Find the Brothers**
- **Activity:** Maze.
- **Visual:** Help Joseph find his brothers in the field with their sheep.

**Page 8: Tracing Page - J is for Joseph**
- **Activity:** Trace the letter 'J' and the word "JOSEPH."
- **Visual:** Large block letters.

**Page 9: Connect-the-Dots - The Big Star**
- **Activity:** Connect dots 1-12 to make a star.
- **Visual:** A large star from Joseph's dream.

**Page 10: Coloring Page - The Wheat Dream**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** Bundles of wheat bowing down (make them look like friendly characters).

**Page 11: Counting Activity - The Brothers**
- **Activity:** "Joseph has many brothers. Can you count them?"
- **Visual:** A group of brothers (keep them simple and friendly).

**Page 12: Drawing Activity - Your Own Dream**
- **Activity:** "Joseph had a big dream. What do you dream about?"
- **Visual:** A large thought bubble to draw inside.

**Page 13: Coloring Page - Traveling to Egypt**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** Joseph traveling on a camel. The desert has big pyramids in the background.

**Page 14: Matching Activity - Shadows**
- **Activity:** Match the brother to his shadow.
- **Visual:** Three brothers and three silhouettes.

**Page 15: Coloring Page - The Egyptian Palace**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** A large, beautiful palace with palm trees.

**Page 16: Letter Tracing - C is for Coat**
- **Activity:** Trace the letter 'C' and the word "COAT."
- **Visual:** Large dotted letters.

**Page 17: Coloring Page - Helping Others**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** Joseph giving bags of grain to people. He is a kind leader.

**Page 18: Tracing Page - Rain and Sun**
- **Activity:** Trace the sun and the raindrops.
- **Visual:** Dotted lines for sunbeams and rain.

**Page 19: Coloring Page - Family Reunion**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** Joseph hugging his brothers. Everyone is smiling and happy.

**Page 20: Pattern Activity - Colorful Tassels**
- **Activity:** "Color the tassels on the coat: Purple, Orange, Purple, Orange..."
- **Visual:** A row of tassels at the bottom of a coat.

**Page 21: Drawing Activity - Design a Coat**
- **Activity:** "Design a beautiful coat for yourself! Use all your favorite colors."
- **Visual:** A large blank coat outline.

**Page 22: Coloring Page - God's Plan**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** A large sun with the words "God has a big plan for me."

**Page 23: Memory Verse Page**
- **Activity:** Color the words "God is with you everywhere."
- **Visual:** A decorative border with Joseph and a camel.

**Page 24: Certificate of Dreaming Big**
- **Activity:** Cut out and keep.
- **Visual:** A certificate that says: "This certifies that [Name] is a DAZZLING DREAMER! God has a special plan for me!"
- **Illustrations:** Stars, a rainbow, and a colorful coat.

---

## Visual Notes for Illustrator
- **Coat Colors:** Encourage the use of a rainbow palette.
- **Characters:** Joseph should always look kind and hopeful, even when he is traveling.
- **Setting:** Transition from the simple tents of Canaan to the ornate, colorful buildings of Egypt.
- **Ages:** Keep all activities very simple for the 2-6 age group.
