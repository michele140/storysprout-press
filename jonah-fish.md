# Jonah and the Big Fish

## Character Descriptions

*   **Jonah**: A friendly man with a kind face and a short beard. He wears a simple blue and white robe and a headcloth.
*   **The Big Fish**: A very large, friendly-looking blue fish. It has a big, wide smile and large, gentle eyes. It looks like a happy ocean giant.
*   **The Sailors**: A group of kind men with colorful shirts and hats who work on a wooden boat.
*   **The Captain**: A sturdy man with a grey beard and a captain's hat, looking very brave.

---

## Story Pages

### Page 1: Title Page
**Text:** Jonah and the Big Fish
**Scene Description:** Jonah is standing on a sunny beach, waving. In the background, the Big Fish is peeking its head out of the water with a friendly smile.

### Page 2
**Text:** Long ago, there lived a man named Jonah. Jonah loved God, but sometimes he found it hard to listen.
**Scene Description:** Jonah is sitting under a leafy tree, looking thoughtful. The sun is shining brightly over a small town.

### Page 3
**Text:** One day, God said, “Jonah, please go to the city of Nineveh. Tell the people there that I love them.”
**Scene Description:** A soft, warm light shines down on Jonah. Jonah looks a little bit worried and is shaking his head.

### Page 4
**Text:** But Jonah did not want to go to Nineveh. He decided to run away instead!
**Scene Description:** Jonah is packing a small bag in a hurry. He is looking over his shoulder, as if he is trying to hide.

### Page 5
**Text:** Jonah went down to the big blue sea. He found a sturdy wooden boat.
**Scene Description:** A wide shot of a busy harbor. Jonah is walking toward a large boat with white sails.

### Page 6
**Text:** “Where are you going?” the Captain asked. “Far away!” Jonah said. He hopped onto the boat.
**Scene Description:** Jonah is talking to the Captain on the deck of the boat. The sailors are pulling on ropes to get ready.

### Page 7
**Text:** The boat sailed out into the deep water. Jonah went downstairs to take a long nap.
**Scene Description:** The boat is sailing peacefully on the ocean. A small window shows Jonah curled up on some soft blankets, fast asleep.

### Page 8
**Text:** Suddenly, the sky turned grey. *Whoosh!* The wind began to blow. *Splash!* The waves grew big.
**Scene Description:** The ocean is now wavy and dark blue. Rain is falling, and the boat is tipping to one side. The sailors look very busy.

### Page 9
**Text:** The sailors were afraid. “The sea is very grumpy today!” they cried.
**Scene Description:** The sailors are holding onto the side of the boat as a large wave splashes over the deck.

### Page 10
**Text:** Jonah woke up and came upstairs. He knew why the sea was grumpy. “I am running away from God,” he said quietly.
**Scene Description:** Jonah is standing on the deck, looking sad. The Captain and sailors are listening to him with wide eyes.

### Page 11
**Text:** “If you throw me into the water, the sea will be calm again,” Jonah told them.
**Scene Description:** Jonah is pointing toward the water. The sailors look unsure—they don't want to throw Jonah into the sea.

### Page 12
**Text:** The sailors tried to row to land, but the waves were too big. Finally, they gently lifted Jonah and… *Splash!*
**Scene Description:** Two sailors are holding Jonah’s arms and legs as he drops into the blue water. The water is full of bubbles.

### Page 13
**Text:** As soon as Jonah hit the water, the wind stopped. The sea became smooth and quiet again.
**Scene Description:** The boat is now sitting on calm, flat water. The sun is starting to peek through the clouds. The sailors look relieved.

### Page 14
**Text:** But Jonah was under the water! *Gurgle, gurgle.* Just then, a very Big Fish swam by.
**Scene Description:** Jonah is floating underwater, surrounded by little bubbles. The giant, friendly blue fish is swimming toward him with its mouth open wide.

### Page 15
**Text:** *Gulp!* The Big Fish swallowed Jonah whole!
**Scene Description:** A funny shot of Jonah disappearing into the fish’s big mouth. The fish looks like it’s taking a big, happy bite of a snack.

### Page 16
**Text:** Inside the Big Fish, it wasn't scary at all. It was warm and dark, like a cozy cave.
**Scene Description:** Jonah is sitting inside the fish’s belly. It looks like a room with soft, pink walls. There are some glowing sea plants making a gentle light.

### Page 17
**Text:** Jonah sat down on a soft spot. He had a lot of time to think.
**Scene Description:** Jonah is sitting with his chin in his hand, looking thoughtful. He looks much calmer now.

### Page 18
**Text:** “I’m sorry, God,” Jonah prayed. “I should have listened to you. Please give me another chance.”
**Scene Description:** Jonah is kneeling with his hands together, looking up. A soft glow fills the "room" inside the fish.

### Page 19
**Text:** Jonah stayed inside the Big Fish for three whole days. He sang songs and talked to God.
**Scene Description:** Jonah is smiling and clapping his hands. The fish is swimming through the deep blue ocean, looking very happy too.

### Page 20
**Text:** God heard Jonah’s prayer. He spoke to the Big Fish. “It’s time to let Jonah go,” God said.
**Scene Description:** The Big Fish is swimming toward a bright, sandy beach. The water is light blue and clear.

### Page 21
**Text:** The Big Fish swam close to the shore. *Wiggle, wiggle, burp!* Jonah flew out onto the dry sand.
**Scene Description:** The Big Fish has its mouth open near the sand. Jonah is "popping" out, landing safely on the beach.

### Page 22
**Text:** “Thank you, Big Fish!” Jonah called out. The fish wiggled its tail and swam back into the sea.
**Scene Description:** Jonah is standing on the beach, waving goodbye. The Big Fish is splashing its tail as it swims away.

### Page 23
**Text:** This time, Jonah listened! He walked all the way to Nineveh and told everyone about God’s love.
**Scene Description:** Jonah is standing in a town square, surrounded by many happy people. He is smiling and gesturing with his hands.

### Page 24
**Text:** Jonah was happy because he listened to God. And God was happy because Jonah was his friend.
**Scene Description:** A beautiful sunset scene. Jonah is walking home, and a colorful rainbow (just like Noah's!) is in the sky.

**The End.**
