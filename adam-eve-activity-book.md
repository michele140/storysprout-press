# Activity Book Outline: Adam and Eve's Beautiful Garden (Bible Adventures)

**Series:** Bible Adventures (Series 2)
**Book Title:** Adam and Eve's Beautiful Garden
**Target Age:** 2-6 years
**Theme:** Creation, God's beautiful world, animals, caring for nature.
**Total Pages:** 24

---

## Page-by-Page Outline

**Page 1: Title Page**
- **Activity:** "This Book Belongs To" with a cute bird and a flower.
- **Visual:** A frame made of vines and leaves.

**Page 2: Introduction / Story Recap**
- **Activity:** "Color the Garden."
- **Visual:** A lush garden scene with a big waterfall and a rainbow.

**Page 3: Coloring Page - Adam**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** Adam sitting in the grass, surrounded by friendly rabbits.

**Page 4: Tracing Page - G is for Garden**
- **Activity:** Trace the letter 'G' and the word "GARDEN."
- **Visual:** Large block letters with flower motifs.

**Page 5: Coloring Page - Eve**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** Eve picking a beautiful, large flower with a butterfly on it.

**Page 6: Letter Tracing - E is for Eve**
- **Activity:** Trace the letter 'E' and the word "EVE."
- **Visual:** Large dotted letters.

**Page 7: Maze - Find the Tall Giraffe**
- **Activity:** Maze.
- **Visual:** Help Adam walk through the garden to find a tall, smiling giraffe.

**Page 8: Coloring Page - Naming the Animals**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** Adam pointing to a lion, and the lion looks like a big, fluffy cat.

**Page 9: Connect-the-Dots - The Butterfly**
- **Activity:** Connect dots 1-10 to reveal a butterfly.
- **Visual:** A simple butterfly shape.

**Page 10: Tracing Page - Leaf Shapes**
- **Activity:** Trace different types of leaves (round, pointy, heart-shaped).
- **Visual:** Dotted leaf outlines.

**Page 11: Coloring Page - The Beautiful River**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** A sparkling river with fish jumping out (they are smiling).

**Page 12: Counting Activity - Friendly Fish**
- **Activity:** "How many fish are in the river? Count them!"
- **Visual:** Seven simple fish in the water.

**Page 13: Drawing Activity - Your Favorite Animal**
- **Activity:** "Adam named all the animals. Draw your favorite animal in the garden!"
- **Visual:** A large blank space with a border of animal footprints.

**Page 14: Coloring Page - The Night Sky**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** Adam and Eve looking at a giant moon and lots of stars.

**Page 15: Matching Activity - Animal Pairs**
- **Activity:** Match the animal to its name (Bird, Cat, Elephant).
- **Visual:** Simple drawings and words to connect.

**Page 16: Coloring Page - The Huge Elephant**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** An elephant spraying water from its trunk to water the flowers.

**Page 17: Tracing Page - Wavy Water**
- **Activity:** Trace the wavy lines of the river.
- **Visual:** Long, wavy dotted lines.

**Page 18: Coloring Page - Sleeping Animals**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** A little lamb and a lion sleeping together in the soft grass.

**Page 19: Counting Activity - Apples on the Tree**
- **Activity:** Count the big, red apples on the tree (1, 2, 3, 4, 5).
- **Visual:** A large, leafy tree with five clear apples.

**Page 20: Drawing Activity - Design a Flower**
- **Activity:** "God made so many beautiful flowers. Draw a new one!"
- **Visual:** A blank stem and leaves for the child to add a flower head.

**Page 21: Coloring Page - Adam and Eve Happy**
- **Activity:** Coloring.
- **Visual:** Adam and Eve holding hands and smiling at the viewer.

**Page 22: Letter Tracing - A is for Adam**
- **Activity:** Trace the letter 'A' and the word "ADAM."
- **Visual:** Large block letters.

**Page 23: Memory Verse Page**
- **Activity:** Color the words "God saw that it was good."
- **Visual:** A decorative border of vines and fruit.

**Page 24: Certificate of Nature Friend**
- **Activity:** Cut out and keep.
- **Visual:** A certificate that says: "This certifies that [Name] is a FRIEND OF NATURE! I love God's beautiful world!"
- **Illustrations:** Sun, flowers, and a smiling lion.

---

## Visual Notes for Illustrator
- **Tone:** Pure joy and peace. No scary elements.
- **Characters:** Adam and Eve should look young and happy, wearing simple, modest clothing made of leaves/natural fibers.
- **Environment:** Use lots of green, blue, and bright floral colors.
- **Ages:** All animals should look "cuddly" and friendly.
