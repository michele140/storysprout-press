# Pip the Garden Gnome's Surprise

## Character Descriptions

*   **Pip**: A tiny garden gnome about the size of a large strawberry. He has a very pointy red hat that sometimes flops over his eyes, a snowy white beard that reaches his belt, and a friendly blue tunic. His cheeks are as rosy as ripe tomatoes.
*   **Bella the Butterfly**: A bright yellow-and-black swallowtail butterfly. She is Pip's best friend and provides him with a "bird's-eye view" of the garden.
*   **Mr. McGregor**: A kindly old man with spectacles and a big straw hat. He loves his garden but is a bit forgetful and sometimes loses his tools.
*   **Tiptoe the Mouse**: A tiny, light-brown field mouse with big ears and a long, pink tail. He is very fast and helps Pip carry small items.
