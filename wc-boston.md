# World Cup Series Book #11: Boston's Victory

**Target Age:** 5-9 years
**Theme:** History, bravery, legacy, victory through teamwork.
**Characters:**
- **Nora:** A thoughtful 9-year-old from Boston who loves history, sailing, and soccer.
- **Grandma Elin:** Nora’s grandmother, a retired historian who loves telling stories about Boston’s brave past.
- **The Lighthouse Giant:** A stately stadium character with a body of red brick and silver steel, and a towering lighthouse for a head that shines a guiding light for all fans.
- **Team Italy:** The "Azzurri" team in their classic blue and white kits.
