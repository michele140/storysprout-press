# Moses and the Amazing Sea

## Character Descriptions

*   **Moses**: A kind and humble leader with a long, snowy-white beard and wise, gentle eyes. He wears a simple tan robe and a striped headcloth. He always carries a tall wooden staff that helps him walk.
*   **Miriam**: Moses' older sister. She has a bright smile and a very kind heart. She wears a colorful dress and often carries a small tambourine for singing and dancing.
*   **The Israelites**: A large, happy group of families. There are grandmas, grandpas, moms, dads, and lots of children. They carry bundles of food and clothes, and some have fluffy sheep and goats with them.
*   **The Red Sea**: A giant, beautiful body of deep blue water. When it parts, it looks like two tall, shimmering curtains made of crystal-clear water.

## Story Pages

### Page 1: Title Page
**Text:** Moses and the Amazing Sea
**Scene Description:** Moses is standing on a high rock, holding his staff up toward a giant path opening through the deep blue sea. The sun is shining brightly on the water.

### Page 2
**Text:** Long ago, a kind man named Moses had a very big job. God asked him to lead a large group of families to a new, beautiful home.
**Scene Description:** Moses is standing in front of a group of people (the Israelites), talking to them with a kind smile. They are all getting ready for a long walk.

### Page 3
**Text:** Moses’ sister, Miriam, helped him. “Don't worry,” she told the people. “God is going to take care of us!”
**Scene Description:** Miriam is helping a small child tie their sandal. She looks brave and cheerful.

### Page 4
**Text:** The families began their journey. They walked across the warm, sandy desert.
**Scene Description:** A wide shot of the long line of people walking through a sandy desert with palm trees in the distance.

### Page 5
**Text:** *Crunch, crunch, crunch.* Their sandals made a soft sound on the sand. The sun was bright, but they were happy to be together.
**Scene Description:** A close-up of a family walking. A little boy is riding on his father's shoulders, and a little girl is holding her mother's hand.

### Page 6
**Text:** After walking for many days, they reached the edge of a giant, blue sea called the Red Sea.
**Scene Description:** The people have reached the shore. The water is big and deep, stretching as far as they can see.

### Page 7
**Text:** The people stopped. “The water is too big!” they said. “How will we ever get across?”
**Scene Description:** A group of people looking worriedly at the large waves. Moses is standing by the water, looking thoughtful.

### Page 8
**Text:** Suddenly, they heard a sound in the distance. *Thump, thump, thump.* Pharaoh’s army was coming!
**Scene Description:** Far off in the distance, behind the hills, you can see the dust from an army. They aren't close, but the people are looking back.

### Page 9
**Text:** The people were afraid. But Moses stood tall. “Do not be afraid,” he said in a calm voice. “Watch and see what God will do!”
**Scene Description:** Moses is standing bravely with his staff. He has a peaceful expression that makes the people feel better.

### Page 10
**Text:** Moses walked right up to the edge of the water. He took a deep breath and prayed.
**Scene Description:** A close-up of Moses' face as he looks out over the sea. The wind is starting to blow his beard.

### Page 11
**Text:** Then, Moses stretched out his wooden staff over the big, blue waves.
**Scene Description:** Moses is holding his staff high over the water. A soft light is beginning to glow from the staff.

### Page 12
**Text:** *Whoosh!* A strong, gentle wind began to blow. It blew and it blew all through the night.
**Scene Description:** A beautiful night scene. The moon is out, and you can see the wind blowing the surface of the water into big ripples.

### Page 13
**Text:** Then, something amazing happened. The water began to move! It pushed back further and further.
**Scene Description:** The water is starting to pull apart in the middle, revealing a path of dry land underneath.

### Page 14
**Text:** The Red Sea parted! On the left and on the right, the water stood up like tall, shimmering curtains.
**Scene Description:** A giant path has opened through the sea. The walls of water are tall and clear, and you can even see little fish swimming inside them!

### Page 15
**Text:** In the middle of the sea, there was a wide, dry path. “Look!” Miriam cried. “We can walk right through!”
**Scene Description:** Miriam is pointing at the path, looking very excited. The people are gathering their things.

### Page 16
**Text:** Moses led the way. One by one, the families began to walk onto the dry ground between the walls of water.
**Scene Description:** Moses is walking onto the path. The ground is dry and dusty, not muddy at all.

### Page 17
**Text:** The children looked up at the water walls. “Wow!” they whispered. The water looked like a giant, blue window.
**Scene Description:** Two children are standing on the path, looking up in awe at the wall of water. A friendly dolphin is visible through the water wall.

### Page 18
**Text:** The Israelites walked and walked. They felt very safe because they knew God was protecting them.
**Scene Description:** A wide shot from inside the parted sea. The line of people is halfway across the path, with the tall water curtains on both sides.

### Page 19
**Text:** Finally, the very last person stepped onto the other side of the sea. They were safe!
**Scene Description:** The families are arriving on the new shore, hugging each other and looking back at the path.

### Page 20
**Text:** Moses stretched out his staff once more. *Splash!* The walls of water began to lean back down.
**Scene Description:** Moses is on the new shore, holding his staff out. The walls of water are gently falling back into the sea.

### Page 21
**Text:** Soon, the Red Sea was just a big, blue body of water again. Pharaoh’s army couldn't follow them anymore.
**Scene Description:** The sea is smooth and calm again. The army is far away on the other side, unable to cross.

### Page 22
**Text:** Miriam grabbed her tambourine. *Jingle, jingle, shake!* “Let's celebrate!” she cried.
**Scene Description:** Miriam is dancing and playing her tambourine. Other people are starting to clap and join in.

### Page 23
**Text:** Everyone began to sing and dance. They were so happy to be free and safe in their new home.
**Scene Description:** A big, joyful celebration on the beach. People are dancing, and children are playing in the sand.

### Page 24
**Text:** Moses smiled as he watched his friends. He knew that with God's help, even the biggest sea could be an amazing path.
**Scene Description:** Moses is sitting on a rock, watching the happy crowd. He looks very peaceful and satisfied.

**The End.**
