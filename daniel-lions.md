# Daniel and the Friendly Lions

## Character Descriptions

*   **Daniel**: A kind man with a warm smile and a twinkle in his eye. He wears a bright, colorful coat and a simple headcloth.
*   **King Darius**: A tall king with a big grey beard and a shiny gold crown. He wears a royal purple robe and has a friendly face.
*   **The Lions**: A group of big, orange, fluffy lions. They have soft manes and big paws. They look more like sleepy, cuddly kittens than wild animals.
*   **The Angel**: A bright, gentle being made of soft white light. The angel has a peaceful expression and a warm glow.

---

## Story Pages

### Page 1: Title Page
**Text:** Daniel and the Friendly Lions
**Scene Description:** Daniel is standing in front of a big, happy lion. The lion is sitting like a house cat, and Daniel is patting its head.

### Page 2
**Text:** Long ago, there was a kind man named Daniel. Daniel worked hard and he always did his best.
**Scene Description:** Daniel is in a beautiful palace room, organized and working with some scrolls. He looks happy and focused.

### Page 3
**Text:** King Darius liked Daniel very much. “You are a good helper, Daniel,” the King said with a smile.
**Scene Description:** King Darius is sitting on his throne, patting Daniel on the shoulder. They both look like good friends.

### Page 4
**Text:** But some other men in the palace were jealous. They didn't want the King to like Daniel so much.
**Scene Description:** Three men are whispering behind a pillar, looking grumpy and pointing at Daniel.

### Page 5
**Text:** The men made a trick rule. “Everyone must pray only to the King for thirty days!” they told the King.
**Scene Description:** The jealous men are handing a long scroll (the rule) to King Darius. The King looks a bit confused but is signing it.

### Page 6
**Text:** Daniel heard the rule. But Daniel loved to talk to God. He prayed every morning, every noon, and every night.
**Scene Description:** Daniel is by a window in his house, looking up at the sky with a peaceful smile. The sun is shining in.

### Page 7
**Text:** The jealous men watched Daniel. “Look!” they cried. “Daniel is still praying to God! He broke the rule!”
**Scene Description:** The men are peeking through Daniel’s window, looking very pleased with themselves.

### Page 8
**Text:** The men went to the King. “Daniel broke the rule,” they said. “He must go into the lions' pit.”
**Scene Description:** The men are talking to King Darius. The King looks very sad and worried, holding his head in his hands.

### Page 9
**Text:** King Darius didn't want to hurt Daniel. But he had already made the rule. He had to follow it.
**Scene Description:** King Darius is looking at Daniel with a sad face. Daniel looks calm and brave.

### Page 10
**Text:** The soldiers took Daniel to a big, deep pit. Inside the pit lived a group of big, fluffy lions.
**Scene Description:** A wide shot of a circular stone pit. At the bottom, you can see the orange manes of several lions.

### Page 11
**Text:** *Roar!* went the lions. But they didn't sound scary. They sounded like they were saying hello!
**Scene Description:** A close-up of a lion with its mouth open, but its eyes are big and friendly, and its tail is wagging.

### Page 12
**Text:** Daniel stepped into the pit. The lions didn't bite. They just sniffed Daniel’s colorful coat.
**Scene Description:** Daniel is at the bottom of the pit. Three lions are gathered around him, looking curious and gentle.

### Page 13
**Text:** One lion began to purr. *Purr, purr, purr.* It sounded like a little motor. 
**Scene Description:** Daniel is sitting on the floor of the pit, and a big lion has its head in his lap. Daniel is scratching behind its ears.

### Page 14
**Text:** Another lion wanted to play! It rolled onto its back and showed its fluffy tummy.
**Scene Description:** A big lion is rolling on the ground next to Daniel, looking very silly and happy.

### Page 15
**Text:** Suddenly, the pit became very bright. A gentle Angel of light appeared in the middle of the lions.
**Scene Description:** A beautiful, glowing Angel stands next to Daniel. The whole pit is filled with a soft, warm light.

### Page 16
**Text:** The Angel smiled at Daniel. “Do not be afraid,” the Angel whispered. “God is watching over you.”
**Scene Description:** A close-up of the Angel and Daniel. The Angel has a hand on Daniel’s shoulder. Daniel looks very peaceful.

### Page 17
**Text:** The Angel patted the lions on their heads. One by one, the lions began to yawn. *Yaaaawn!*
**Scene Description:** The lions are all yawning widely, showing their big tongues. They look very, very sleepy.

### Page 18
**Text:** The lions curled up into big, orange balls of fluff. Soon, they were all fast asleep.
**Scene Description:** Daniel is leaning against a sleeping lion like it’s a big pillow. All the other lions are sleeping around them.

### Page 19
**Text:** Up in the palace, King Darius couldn't sleep. He was too worried about his friend Daniel.
**Scene Description:** King Darius is pacing back and forth in his bedroom. The moon is visible through the window.

### Page 20
**Text:** As soon as the sun came up, the King ran to the pit. “Daniel! Daniel! Are you okay?” he called out.
**Scene Description:** King Darius is peeking over the edge of the pit. The morning sun is making everything look golden.

### Page 21
**Text:** Daniel looked up and waved. “I am safe, King Darius! God sent an Angel to stay with me.”
**Scene Description:** Daniel is standing up in the pit, surrounded by sleeping lions, waving a big hello to the King.

### Page 22
**Text:** King Darius was so happy! He told the soldiers to help Daniel out of the pit right away.
**Scene Description:** Soldiers are lowering a rope, and the King is cheering. Daniel is getting ready to climb up.

### Page 23
**Text:** The King told everyone the good news. “God is very strong! He protects his friends,” the King said.
**Scene Description:** King Darius and Daniel are standing together on a balcony, talking to a big crowd of happy people.

### Page 24
**Text:** Daniel went back to his work with a happy heart. He knew that God would always be his best friend.
**Scene Description:** Daniel is back in his palace room, but now a little lion cub is sitting by his feet, and they are both smiling.

**The End.**
