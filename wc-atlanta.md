# World Cup Series Book #5: Atlanta's Amazing Stadium

**Target Age:** 5-9 years
**Theme:** Innovation, precision, excitement, Southern hospitality.
**Characters:**
- **Zoe:** A tech-savvy 9-year-old from Atlanta who loves drones, gadgets, and soccer.
- **Dad:** Zoe’s dad, an engineer who loves explaining how things work.
- **The Benz:** A futuristic, high-tech stadium character with a retractable roof that looks like a flower and a giant halo video board for a crown.
- **Team France:** The "Les Bleus" team in their elegant blue and white kits.
