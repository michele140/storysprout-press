# Little Cloud's Big Activity Book! (Outline)

**Target Audience:** Ages 2-6
**Theme:** Based on "The Little Cloud's Big Adventure"

---

## Page-by-Page Outline

### Page 1: Cover Page
*   **Title:** Little Cloud's Big Activity Book!
*   **Illustration:** Cirrus waving with a big smile.

### Page 2: This Book Belongs To...
*   **Activity:** A space for the child to write/trace their name inside a fluffy cloud frame.

### Page 3: Coloring Page - Meet Cirrus
*   **Scene:** Cirrus floating alone in a bright sky.
*   **Text:** Color Cirrus! He is a happy, fluffy cloud.

### Page 4: Tracing Activity - Cloud Words
*   **Activity:** Large, dashed letters for the child to trace the word "CLOUD".
*   **Illustration:** A small cloud icon next to the word.

### Page 5: Maze - Find the Flowers
*   **Activity:** A simple maze where Cirrus needs to get to the drooping flowers.
*   **Text:** Help Cirrus find the thirsty garden!

### Page 6: Coloring Page - Hello, Sunny!
*   **Scene:** Cirrus meeting Sunny the Sun.
*   **Text:** Sunny is bright and warm. Use your favorite yellow crayon!

### Page 7: Connect-the-Dots - The Big Sun
*   **Activity:** Connect dots numbered 1 to 10 to complete Sunny's circle.
*   **Text:** Connect the dots to finish the Sun!

### Page 8: Spot the Difference - Windy Day
*   **Activity:** Two similar pictures of Cirrus and Gusty the Breeze.
*   **Differences (5):** A missing leaf, a different swirl pattern, Cirrus's eyes closed in one, a bird missing, a hill color change.

### Page 9: Tracing Activity - Windy Swirls
*   **Activity:** Trace the swirling lines that represent Gusty.
*   **Text:** Gusty is blowing! Trace the swirls.

### Page 10: Coloring Page - Shade for Friends
*   **Scene:** Cirrus blocking the sun over the playground.
*   **Text:** Cirrus is making a cool shadow. Color the happy children!

### Page 11: Counting Activity - Playground Fun
*   **Activity:** Count the children, the swings, and the birds in the playground scene.
*   **Text:** How many friends are in the shade? Write the number: [ ]

### Page 12: Coloring Page - Best Friends
*   **Scene:** Cirrus and Drippy floating together.
*   **Text:** Color Cirrus and his friend Drippy.

### Page 13: Connect-the-Dots - Raindrop
*   **Activity:** Connect dots 1 to 15 to draw a large raindrop.
*   **Text:** What falls from the clouds? Connect the dots!

### Page 14: Coloring Page - Cloud Huddle
*   **Scene:** Many clouds gathering together to make a big rain cloud.
*   **Text:** All the clouds are working together!

### Page 15: Tracing Activity - Falling Rain
*   **Activity:** Trace vertical dashed lines falling from the clouds to the ground.
*   **Text:** Trace the rain! *Pitter-patter, pitter-patter.*

### Page 16: Coloring Page - Happy Farmer
*   **Scene:** The farmer dancing in the rain in his field.
*   **Text:** The farmer is so happy! The plants are drinking the water.

### Page 17: Spot the Difference - The Green Field
*   **Activity:** Two pictures of the lush green field after the rain.
*   **Differences (5):** A flower missing, a bug on a leaf, the farmer's hat color, a fence post missing, a cloud shape.

### Page 18: Coloring Page - The Beautiful Rainbow
*   **Scene:** A large rainbow across the sky with Cirrus at the end.
*   **Text:** After the rain comes the rainbow!

### Page 19: Color by Number - Small Rainbow
*   **Activity:** A simple rainbow with sections numbered 1 (Red), 2 (Yellow), 3 (Blue).
*   **Text:** Use the code to color the rainbow!

### Page 20: Coloring Page - Sunset Glow
*   **Scene:** Cirrus colored by the sunset (pink and orange).
*   **Text:** Cirrus is getting ready for bed. Color him with sunset colors.

### Page 21: Matching Activity - Who is Who?
*   **Activity:** Match images of Cirrus, Sunny, Gusty (swirls), and Drippy to their names.
*   **Text:** Draw a line to match the friend to their name!

### Page 22: Drawing Activity - My Cloud
*   **Activity:** A large blank cloud shape for the child to draw a face on.
*   **Text:** If you were a cloud, what would you look like? Draw your face!

### Page 23: Coloring Page - Cloud Party
*   **Scene:** All characters (Cirrus, Sunny, Gusty, Drippy) together in the sky.
*   **Text:** We are all special!

### Page 24: Cloud Explorer Certificate
*   **Activity:** A decorative page that says "Certificate of Achievement: [Name] is a Great Cloud Explorer!"
*   **Text:** You finished the book! Great job!
