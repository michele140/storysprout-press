# World Cup Series Book #9: Bay Area Blast

**Target Age:** 5-9 years
**Theme:** Technology, sustainability, nature, digital connection.
**Characters:**
- **Kai:** A 9-year-old from San Jose who loves coding, hiking, and soccer.
- **Mom:** Kai’s mom, a software engineer who loves the outdoors.
- **The Silicon Giant:** A high-tech stadium character with a roof covered in real grass and a body that pulses with light like a computer circuit.
- **Team Portugal:** The "Seleção" team in their vibrant red and green kits.
