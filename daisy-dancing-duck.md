# Daisy the Dancing Duck

## Character Descriptions

*   **Daisy**: A bright yellow, fluffy duckling with big, sparkling eyes. she wears a tiny pink bow on one of the three feathers on her head. She is energetic and graceful.
*   **Puddles**: A friendly, slightly plump brown duck with a very orange beak. He is loyal and kind, though a bit shy.
*   **Oliver the Owl**: A wise-looking, large brown owl with tufted ears and big yellow glasses perched on his beak. He lives in a hollow oak tree.
*   **The Frog Choir**: A group of five green frogs of various sizes, all wearing tiny bowties. They sit on lily pads and look very serious about their music.

## Story Pages

### Page 1: Title Page
**Text:** Daisy the Dancing Duck
**Scene Description:** Daisy is doing a graceful pose on one leg near the edge of a sparkling pond. Flowers are blooming around her.

### Page 2
**Text:** Down at the Willow Pond, the morning started just like any other.
**Scene Description:** A beautiful sunrise over a pond surrounded by weeping willow trees. A group of ducks is gathered.

### Page 3
**Text:** All the ducks lined up for their morning walk. *Waddle, waddle, waddle.*
**Scene Description:** A line of ducks walking in a straight line. They look very orderly and serious.

### Page 4
**Text:** But Daisy didn't want to waddle. Daisy had a secret. In her heart, she wanted to dance!
**Scene Description:** Daisy is at the back of the line, looking like she's about to burst with energy. Her feet are slightly off the ground.

### Page 5
**Text:** While the others went *left, right, left*, Daisy went *tap, tap, twirl!*
**Scene Description:** Daisy doing a little spin out of the line, while the other ducks continue straight ahead, looking surprised.

### Page 6
**Text:** "Daisy!" the big ducks quacked. "Ducks do not twirl. Ducks waddle!"
**Scene Description:** A group of older, grumpy-looking ducks staring at Daisy. Daisy looks a bit embarrassed, pulling her wings in.

### Page 7
**Text:** Daisy tried to be a good duck. She tried to waddle just like everyone else.
**Scene Description:** Daisy walking very stiffly in the line. She looks unhappy and bored.

### Page 8
**Text:** But it was very hard. Her feet felt like they were full of bouncy springs!
**Scene Description:** Daisy's legs look like they are vibrating. She's trying hard to keep them still, but she's failing.

### Page 9
**Text:** "I'm just not a good waddler," Daisy sighed. She sat under the old oak tree, feeling very droopy.
**Scene Description:** Daisy sitting alone in the shade. Her tiny bow is slightly crooked.

### Page 10
**Text:** "Whoo-whoo is looking so sad?" asked a voice from above. It was Oliver the Owl.
**Scene Description:** Oliver the Owl looking down from a branch, his glasses reflecting the sunlight.

### Page 11
**Text:** "I want to dance, Oliver," Daisy said. "But ducks are supposed to waddle."
**Scene Description:** Daisy looking up at Oliver. Puddles the duck is waddling toward them in the background.

### Page 12
**Text:** Oliver adjusted his glasses. "The pond is big enough for many rhythms, Daisy. Why hide your spark?"
**Scene Description:** Close up of Oliver's wise face. He looks very encouraging.

### Page 13
**Text:** Puddles arrived and sat next to Daisy. "I like your dancing," he whispered. "It makes me want to wiggle!"
**Scene Description:** Puddles sitting by Daisy, giving her a friendly nudge with his beak.

### Page 14
**Text:** Just then, they heard a sound from the reeds. *Ribbit-hop! Ribbit-rock!*
**Scene Description:** The Frog Choir appearing from the tall green grass, carrying tiny instruments like a grass-stalk flute and a nut-shell drum.

### Page 15
**Text:** "We need a star for our show!" the lead frog croaked. "Will you dance for us, Daisy?"
**Scene Description:** The frogs looking at Daisy expectantly. One frog is holding out a tiny flower as an invitation.

### Page 16
**Text:** Daisy looked at Oliver. She looked at Puddles. Then, she stood up and took a deep breath.
**Scene Description:** Daisy standing tall, looking determined. Puddles is cheering in the background.

### Page 17
**Text:** The Frog Choir began to play a groovy, lily-pad beat.
**Scene Description:** The frogs playing their instruments with great enthusiasm. Musical notes are floating in the air.

### Page 18
**Text:** Daisy started slow. *Shuffle, shuffle, flap!*
**Scene Description:** Daisy starting to move her wings and feet to the beat. She has a small smile starting.

### Page 19
**Text:** Then she went fast! *Spin, spin, leap!* She was dancing like a golden sunbeam.
**Scene Description:** Daisy in a blur of motion, feathers fluffed, looking absolutely joyful and graceful.

### Page 20
**Text:** Puddles couldn't help it. He started to wiggle his tail. *Shake, shake, shake!*
**Scene Description:** Puddles dancing in his own way—lots of tail-shaking and happy quacking.

### Page 21
**Text:** The other ducks came to see what the noise was about. They had never seen anything like it!
**Scene Description:** A crowd of ducks gathered around the "stage." Some look shocked, but many look interested.

### Page 22
**Text:** "Look at Daisy!" cried a duckling. "She's beautiful!"
**Scene Description:** A small duckling pointing at Daisy with excitement.

### Page 23
**Text:** Soon, the whole pond was moving. Some waddled to the beat, and some found a brand new dance.
**Scene Description:** A big party scene! Ducks, frogs, and even a squirrel are all dancing together in different styles.

### Page 24
**Text:** Daisy led the way, dancing to her own rhythm. She was a duck, and she was a dancer, too!
**Scene Description:** Daisy in the center of a happy parade, leading everyone around the pond. Her bow is straight and her eyes are bright.

**The End.**
