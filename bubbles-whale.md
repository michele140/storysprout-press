# Bubbles the Helpful Whale

## Character Descriptions

*   **Bubbles**: A young blue whale calf with a round, cheerful face and big, bright eyes. She has a special talent: when she breathes out through her blowhole, she creates sparkling, heart-shaped bubbles.
*   **Finley**: A small, energetic orange-and-white striped clownfish. He is Bubbles' best friend and always swims with a quick, darting motion.
*   **Shelly**: A wise old sea turtle with a beautiful, mossy-green shell that has tiny glowing barnacles. She moves slowly and speaks with a calm, gravelly voice.
*   **Captain Kelp**: A tall, vibrant green piece of giant kelp that stands upright. He wears a tiny sailor's hat and uses his long fronds like arms to direct the "traffic" of fish in the reef.
