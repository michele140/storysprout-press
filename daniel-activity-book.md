# Daniel and the Friendly Lions Activity Book (Outline)

**Target Audience:** Ages 2-6
**Theme:** Bible Series - Bravery, Prayer, and Kindness

---

## Page-by-Page Outline (24 Pages)

### Page 1: Cover Page
*   **Activity:** Title and Illustration
*   **Description:** "Daniel and the Friendly Lions: A Bible Adventures Activity Book!" A large, happy illustration of Daniel hugging a big, fluffy lion.

### Page 2: This Book Belongs To...
*   **Activity:** Name Plate
*   **Description:** A frame decorated with lion paws and stars.
*   **Text:** "This Brave Believer is: [Name]"

### Page 3: Coloring Page - Helpful Daniel
*   **Scene:** Daniel working happily with scrolls in the palace.
*   **Text:** "Daniel is a very good helper. Color his bright coat!"

### Page 4: Tracing Activity - L-I-O-N
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the word "LION".
*   **Text:** "Can you trace the word LION?"

### Page 5: Coloring Page - King Darius
*   **Scene:** King Darius sitting on his throne with a big smile.
*   **Text:** "King Darius is Daniel's friend. Color his shiny gold crown!"

### Page 6: Tracing Activity - Lion Shapes
*   **Activity:** Shape Tracing.
*   **Description:** Dashed outlines of sitting and standing lions.
*   **Text:** "Trace the shapes of the friendly lions!"

### Page 7: Coloring Page - Time to Pray
*   **Scene:** Daniel by the window, looking at the sun and praying.
*   **Text:** "Daniel loves to talk to God. Color the warm sun in the window."

### Page 8: Simple Maze - Help Daniel find the Palace
*   **Activity:** Maze.
*   **Description:** A simple path through some palm trees to reach the palace gates.
*   **Text:** "Daniel is coming home! Can you help him find the palace?"

### Page 9: Coloring Page - The Big Pit
*   **Scene:** A deep stone pit with some soft hay at the bottom.
*   **Text:** "Down in the pit, it is quiet. Color the stone walls."

### Page 10: Counting Activity - Fluffy Lions
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** A group of 5 friendly-looking lions.
*   **Text:** "How many friendly lions are in the pit? Count them: [ ]"

### Page 11: Coloring Page - The Happy Lion Cub
*   **Scene:** Daniel playing with a small, fluffy lion cub.
*   **Text:** "Even the little cubs are friendly! Color the lion cub's soft fur."

### Page 12: Tracing Activity - D-A-N-I-E-L
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the name "DANIEL".
*   **Text:** "Can you trace the letters in Daniel's name?"

### Page 13: Coloring Page - Playtime!
*   **Scene:** A big lion rolling on its back while Daniel scratches its tummy.
*   **Text:** "This lion wants to play! Color his fluffy tummy."

### Page 14: Connect-the-Dots - Lion Face
*   **Activity:** Connect-the-dots (Numbers 1-10).
*   **Description:** An outline of a friendly lion's face with ears and whiskers.
*   **Text:** "Connect the dots 1-10 to see a happy lion!"

### Page 15: Coloring Page - The Bright Angel
*   **Scene:** A glowing Angel appearing in the pit next to Daniel.
*   **Text:** "A bright Angel came to visit! Color the Angel's soft white light."

### Page 16: Counting Activity - Bright Angels
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** Three glowing angels flying in the sky.
*   **Text:** "How many angels are watching over Daniel? Count them: [ ]"

### Page 17: Coloring Page - Sleepy Time
*   **Scene:** The lions yawning and getting ready for a nap.
*   **Text:** "The lions are getting sleepy. Color their big yawns!"

### Page 18: Spot the Difference - The Sleeping Lions
*   **Activity:** Spot 3 Differences.
*   **Description:** Two pictures of Daniel leaning against a sleeping lion.
*   **Differences:** (1) The number of stars in the sky, (2) A small mouse peeking from a corner, (3) The color of Daniel's headcloth.

### Page 19: Coloring Page - Safe and Sound
*   **Scene:** Daniel waving to the King from the pit as the sun rises.
*   **Text:** "Good morning! Daniel is safe. Color the golden sunrise."

### Page 20: Matching Activity - Daniel's Story
*   **Activity:** Sequence Matching.
*   **Description:** Match the picture to the step in the story.
*   **Steps:** 1. Daniel prays. 2. Daniel goes to the pit. 3. The Angel visits. 4. Daniel is safe!
*   **Text:** "Draw a line to match what happened first, second, third, and fourth!"

### Page 21: Coloring Page - The Happy King
*   **Scene:** King Darius hugging Daniel and cheering.
*   **Text:** "The King is so happy! Everyone is celebrating."

### Page 22: Counting Activity - Royal Crowns
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** A row of different crowns.
*   **Text:** "How many crowns can you find? Count them: [ ]"

### Page 23: Drawing Activity - Design a Friendly Lion
*   **Activity:** Creative Drawing.
*   **Description:** A large empty frame.
*   **Text:** "If you had a pet lion, what would it look like? Design your own friendly lion here!"

### Page 24: Brave Believer Certificate
*   **Activity:** Award Page.
*   **Description:** "Congratulations! [Name] is a Brave Believer!" Decorated with lions, stars, and a big ribbon.
