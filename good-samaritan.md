# The Good Samaritan

## Character Descriptions

*   **The Traveler**: A friendly man with a kind face and a walking stick. He wears a simple blue tunic and a woven sun hat.
*   **The Good Samaritan**: A very kind man with a warm smile. He wears a bright orange robe and has a gentle way of moving.
*   **The Little Donkey**: A small, sturdy grey donkey with big, fuzzy ears. He is very patient and helpful.
*   **The Innkeeper**: A cheerful, round man with a big apron and a bunch of keys. He has a welcoming smile.

---

## Story Pages

### Page 1: Title Page
**Text:** The Good Samaritan
**Scene Description:** The Good Samaritan is walking alongside his little donkey. The Traveler is sitting on the donkey’s back, waving. They are on a sunny, winding road.

### Page 2
**Text:** Once there was a traveler named Thomas. Thomas loved to go on long walks to see new places.
**Scene Description:** Thomas the Traveler is starting his journey. He is waving goodbye to a small house with a happy heart.

### Page 3
**Text:** One day, Thomas went on a very long walk. The road was dusty and full of big, grey rocks.
**Scene Description:** A wide shot of Thomas walking on a winding road that goes through some hills. The sun is high in the sky.

### Page 4
**Text:** Thomas walked and walked. *Tramp, tramp, tramp.* The sun was very hot, and his legs began to feel heavy.
**Scene Description:** A close-up of Thomas’s feet in sandals walking on the dusty ground. He looks a little bit tired.

### Page 5
**Text:** Oh no! Thomas tripped over a big rock. *Ouch!* He sat down on the side of the road.
**Scene Description:** Thomas is sitting on a patch of grass by the road, looking at a small scrape on his knee. He looks sad.

### Page 6
**Text:** Thomas was very tired. He needed someone to help him get to the next town.
**Scene Description:** Thomas is sitting with his back against a large rock, looking down the long, empty road.

### Page 7
**Text:** Soon, a man came walking by. Thomas waved his hand. “Hello! Can you help me?” he asked.
**Scene Description:** A man in a fancy purple robe is walking down the road. Thomas is waving from his spot by the rock.

### Page 8
**Text:** But the man did not stop. He was in a big hurry. He walked right past Thomas.
**Scene Description:** The man in the purple robe is walking away quickly, looking at a scroll in his hand. Thomas looks disappointed.

### Page 9
**Text:** Then, another man came by. He saw Thomas sitting there, but he walked on the other side of the road.
**Scene Description:** A second man is walking past, staying as far away from Thomas as possible. Thomas is holding out his hand for help.

### Page 10
**Text:** Thomas felt very lonely. “Will anyone stop to help me?” he wondered.
**Scene Description:** A close-up of Thomas’s face. He looks very sad. A small bird is sitting on a nearby branch, looking at him.

### Page 11
**Text:** Suddenly, he heard a new sound. *Clip-clop, clip-clop.* A little donkey was coming down the road.
**Scene Description:** A grey donkey is walking toward Thomas. A man in an orange robe is leading the donkey.

### Page 12
**Text:** It was a man from a place called Samaria. People called him the Good Samaritan because he had a big heart.
**Scene Description:** The Good Samaritan stops in front of Thomas. He has a very kind and concerned look on his face.

### Page 13
**Text:** The Good Samaritan knelt down. “Don’t worry, friend,” he said. “I will help you.”
**Scene Description:** The Good Samaritan is kneeling next to Thomas. He is opening a small bag with some soft bandages and oil.

### Page 14
**Text:** He gently cleaned Thomas’s scrape and put on a soft white bandage. Thomas began to feel much better.
**Scene Description:** A close-up of the Good Samaritan’s hands gently wrapping a bandage around Thomas’s knee.

### Page 15
**Text:** Then, the Good Samaritan helped Thomas stand up. “You can ride on my donkey,” he said.
**Scene Description:** The Good Samaritan is supporting Thomas as he stands up. The Little Donkey is standing patiently nearby.

### Page 16
**Text:** He lifted Thomas up onto the donkey’s back. The donkey’s fur was very soft and fuzzy.
**Scene Description:** Thomas is sitting on the donkey. He is patting the donkey’s neck and smiling for the first time in a while.

### Page 17
**Text:** They traveled together down the long road. The Good Samaritan walked beside them, holding the donkey’s rope.
**Scene Description:** A beautiful sunset scene. The three of them are silhouettes walking toward a town with glowing lights.

### Page 18
**Text:** They reached a cozy inn. An inn is like a big house where travelers can sleep in warm beds.
**Scene Description:** They are standing in front of a welcoming stone building with a wooden sign that says "INN."

### Page 19
**Text:** The Innkeeper came to the door. “Welcome! Welcome!” he said. “We have plenty of room.”
**Scene Description:** The cheerful Innkeeper is holding the door open. There is a warm glow coming from inside the inn.

### Page 20
**Text:** The Good Samaritan helped Thomas into a soft, comfy bed. He gave him a cool drink of water.
**Scene Description:** Thomas is tucked into a bed with a colorful quilt. The Good Samaritan is handing him a cup.

### Page 21
**Text:** The next morning, the Good Samaritan spoke to the Innkeeper. He gave him some shiny coins.
**Scene Description:** The Good Samaritan is at the front desk, handing two silver coins to the Innkeeper.

### Page 22
**Text:** “Please take good care of my friend,” the Good Samaritan said. “I want him to be strong and happy.”
**Scene Description:** The Innkeeper is nodding and smiling. In the background, you can see Thomas sitting up in bed, looking much better.

### Page 23
**Text:** Thomas thanked the Good Samaritan for being such a good neighbor. “You are very kind,” he said.
**Scene Description:** The Good Samaritan is waving goodbye to Thomas from the doorway. The Little Donkey is waiting outside.

### Page 24
**Text:** The Good Samaritan smiled. “Always be kind and help others,” he said. And Thomas promised he would.
**Scene Description:** Thomas is standing on the porch of the inn, waving. He looks happy and healthy. The sun is shining brightly.

**The End.**
