# Bubbles the Helpful Whale Activity Book (Outline)

**Target Audience:** Ages 2-6
**Theme:** Ocean, Teamwork, Helping, and Marine Life

---

## Page-by-Page Outline (24 Pages)

### Page 1: Cover Page
*   **Activity:** Title and Illustration
*   **Description:** "Bubbles the Helpful Whale's Ocean Teamwork Activity Book!" Bubbles blowing a giant heart-shaped bubble.

### Page 2: This Book Belongs To...
*   **Activity:** Name Plate
*   **Description:** A box surrounded by bubbles and sea stars for the child to write/trace their name.

### Page 3: Coloring Page - Bubbles in the Reef
*   **Scene:** Bubbles swimming happily in the Coral Kingdom.
*   **Text:** "Bubbles has a big heart! Color the beautiful coral reef."

### Page 4: Tracing Activity - W-H-A-L-E
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the word "WHALE".
*   **Text:** "Bubbles is a whale! Can you trace the word?"

### Page 5: Coloring Page - Heart Bubbles
*   **Scene:** Bubbles blowing her special heart-shaped bubbles.
*   **Text:** "Pop! Pop! Pop! Color the heart-shaped bubbles pink and red."

### Page 6: Tracing Activity - Ocean Waves
*   **Activity:** Line Tracing.
*   **Description:** Wavy dashed lines representing ocean currents.
*   **Text:** "The water is moving! Trace the ocean waves."

### Page 7: Coloring Page - Meet Finley
*   **Scene:** Finley the Clownfish in his messy anemone.
*   **Text:** "Finley is Bubbles' best friend. Color his orange and white stripes!"

### Page 8: Simple Maze - Help Bubbles Find Finley
*   **Activity:** Maze.
*   **Description:** A simple underwater maze through some kelp to get to Finley.
*   **Text:** "Finley needs help! Can Bubbles find him?"

### Page 9: Coloring Page - Captain Kelp
*   **Scene:** Captain Kelp directing traffic with his fronds.
*   **Text:** "Captain Kelp is the reef director. Color his green fronds!"

### Page 10: Tracing Activity - Straight Fronds
*   **Activity:** Line Tracing.
*   **Description:** Vertical dashed lines representing tall kelp.
*   **Text:** "Kelp grows tall! Trace the lines from bottom to top."

### Page 11: Coloring Page - Shelly the Turtle
*   **Scene:** Shelly the Sea Turtle trying to move a heavy rock.
*   **Text:** "Shelly is very wise. Color her beautiful mossy-green shell."

### Page 12: Connect-the-Dots - Sea Star
*   **Activity:** Connect-the-dots (Numbers 1-10).
*   **Description:** An outline of a 5-pointed sea star.
*   **Text:** "What lives on the ocean floor? Connect the dots!"

### Page 13: Coloring Page - Tired Bubbles
*   **Scene:** Bubbles looking very sleepy and tired after helping everyone.
*   **Text:** "Helping can be hard work. Bubbles needs a rest!"

### Page 14: Counting Activity - Sea Grapes
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** A picture of Shelly's sea grapes in a bunch.
*   **Text:** "Count the sea grapes: [ ]"

### Page 15: Coloring Page - Teamwork!
*   **Scene:** Finley, Shelly, and Captain Kelp working together to help Bubbles.
*   **Text:** "Friends work better together! Color the happy team."

### Page 16: Tracing Activity - Bubble Trail
*   **Activity:** Line Tracing.
*   **Description:** A trail of small circles (bubbles) following a path.
*   **Text:** "Follow the hearts! Trace the bubble trail."

### Page 17: Coloring Page - Finley to the Rescue
*   **Scene:** Small Finley darting into the dark crevice to find the ball.
*   **Text:** "Finley is small and fast! He found the crabs' ball."

### Page 18: Spot the Difference - Coral Party
*   **Activity:** Spot 3 Differences.
*   **Description:** Two pictures of the baby crabs dancing with their ball.
*   **Differences:** (1) Color of the ball, (2) Number of crabs, (3) A sea shell.

### Page 19: Coloring Page - The Big Heart Bubble
*   **Scene:** Bubbles blowing one giant heart bubble around all her friends.
*   **Text:** "Teamwork makes the dream work! We love our reef."

### Page 20: Tracing Activity - Crab Walks
*   **Activity:** Line Tracing.
*   **Description:** Side-to-side zig-zag lines representing crab movement.
*   **Text:** "Crabs walk sideways! Trace the zig-zag lines."

### Page 21: Coloring Page - Underwater Sunset
*   **Scene:** The ocean water turning pink as the sun sets.
*   **Text:** "The day is ending. Color the pretty pink water."

### Page 22: Drawing Activity - My Favorite Sea Creature
*   **Activity:** Creative Drawing.
*   **Description:** A large blank area with "bubbles" at the top.
*   **Text:** "Who else lives in the ocean? Draw your favorite sea animal here!"

### Page 23: Matching Activity - Ocean Roles
*   **Activity:** Matching.
*   **Description:** Match character to their helpful act (Bubbles -> Heart bubbles, Finley -> Finding small things, Shelly -> Moving rocks).

### Page 24: Deep Sea Helper Certificate
*   **Activity:** Award Page.
*   **Description:** "Congratulations! [Name] is a Helpful Deep Sea Team Player!" Decorated with shells and fish.
