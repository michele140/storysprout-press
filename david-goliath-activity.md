# David and the Giant Activity Book (Outline)

**Target Audience:** Ages 2-6
**Theme:** Bible Series - Bravery, Faith, and Trusting God

---

## Page-by-Page Outline (24 Pages)

### Page 1: Cover Page
*   **Activity:** Title and Illustration
*   **Description:** "David and the Giant: A Bible Adventures Activity Book!" A large, happy illustration of David holding his staff with Woolly the lamb at his side, and Goliath's large (but friendly) face peeking from the top corner.

### Page 2: This Book Belongs To...
*   **Activity:** Name Plate
*   **Description:** A frame shaped like a shepherd's pouch.
*   **Text:** "This Brave Shepherd is: [Name]"

### Page 3: Coloring Page - David the Shepherd
*   **Scene:** David sitting on a hill with his sheep and Woolly.
*   **Text:** "David takes care of his fluffy sheep. Color the green hills!"

### Page 4: Tracing Activity - S-H-E-E-P
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the word "SHEEP".
*   **Text:** "David is a shepherd. Can you trace the letters?"

### Page 5: Coloring Page - Woolly the Lamb
*   **Scene:** A close-up of Woolly the lamb hopping through some flowers.
*   **Text:** "Woolly is David's best friend. Color the happy lamb!"

### Page 6: Tracing Activity - Rolling Hills
*   **Activity:** Shape Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed curvy lines representing the hills.
*   **Text:** "Help David find his sheep! Trace the hills."

### Page 7: Coloring Page - The Army Camp
*   **Scene:** Colorful tents with flags.
*   **Text:** "David went to the army camp. Color the bright tents!"

### Page 8: Simple Maze - Find the Brothers
*   **Activity:** Maze.
*   **Description:** A simple path through some tents to help David find his brothers.
*   **Text:** "David has a basket of food. Can you help him find his brothers?"

### Page 9: Coloring Page - Meet Goliath
*   **Scene:** The very tall Goliath in his shiny armor.
*   **Text:** "Goliath is very big! Use your silver crayon for his armor."

### Page 10: Counting Activity - Armor Pieces
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** Pictures of Goliath's helmet, shield, and sword.
*   **Text:** "Goliath has many things! Count the pieces of armor: [ ]"

### Page 11: Coloring Page - King Saul's Crown
*   **Scene:** King Saul sitting on his throne with his gold crown.
*   **Text:** "King Saul is the king. Color his beautiful gold crown!"

### Page 12: Tracing Activity - C-R-O-W-N
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the word "CROWN".
*   **Text:** "Kings wear crowns. Trace the word!"

### Page 13: Coloring Page - David Tries the Armor
*   **Scene:** David wearing a helmet that is way too big and covering his eyes.
*   **Text:** "The armor is too big for David! He is very funny."

### Page 14: Connect-the-Dots - David's Sling
*   **Activity:** Connect-the-dots (Numbers 1-10).
*   **Description:** An outline of David's leather sling.
*   **Text:** "David has a special tool. Connect the dots to see it!"

### Page 15: Coloring Page - The Sparkling Stream
*   **Scene:** David kneeling by the stream picking up stones.
*   **Text:** "David found five smooth stones in the stream. Color the water!"

### Page 16: Counting Activity - Smooth Stones
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** A picture of the five stones in David's hand.
*   **Text:** "Count the stones David picked up: [ ]"

### Page 17: Coloring Page - David is Brave
*   **Scene:** David standing in front of the giant Goliath.
*   **Text:** "David is small, but he is very brave. Color David and Goliath!"

### Page 18: Tracing Activity - B-R-A-V-E
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the word "BRAVE".
*   **Text:** "God helps David be brave. Trace the word!"

### Page 19: Coloring Page - Whish! Whish! Whish!
*   **Scene:** David swinging his sling in a circle.
*   **Text:** "David whirled his sling around. Whish! Whish! Whish!"

### Page 20: Spot the Difference - Stream Side
*   **Activity:** Spot 3 Differences.
*   **Description:** Two pictures of David and Woolly by the stream.
*   **Differences:** (1) The number of stones on the ground, (2) A butterfly on a flower, (3) Woolly's tail position.

### Page 21: Coloring Page - The Giant Sits Down
*   **Scene:** Goliath sitting on the ground looking surprised but okay.
*   **Text:** "Thump! Goliath fell down. Everyone was surprised!"

### Page 22: Drawing Activity - My Shield
*   **Activity:** Creative Drawing.
*   **Description:** A large blank shield shape.
*   **Text:** "If you were a brave soldier, what would your shield look like? Draw it here!"

### Page 23: Matching Activity - Story Match
*   **Activity:** Matching.
*   **Description:** Drawing a line from a character to their item (David -> Sling, King Saul -> Crown, Goliath -> Shield, Woolly -> Grass).
*   **Text:** "Can you match the character to their special thing?"

### Page 24: Brave Shepherd Certificate
*   **Activity:** Award Page.
*   **Description:** "Congratulations! [Name] is a Brave Shepherd!" Decorated with stars and a little lamb.
