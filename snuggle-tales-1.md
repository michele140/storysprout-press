# THE SNUGGLE TALES
## BOOK 1: THE JOURNEY TO JELLYCAT CITY

**FORMAT:** Dork Diaries style — mix of text and black & white illustrations on every page
**TONE:** First-person, conversational, diary-like.
**CHARACTERS:** Pandy (Panda), Dirty (Elephant), Luna (Rabbit), Daisy (Lamb)

---

# [PAGE 1 - TITLE PAGE]

**[ILLUSTRATION: A large, hand-drawn title in bubbly letters: "THE SNUGGLE TALES". Below it, in a slightly messy font: "The Journey to JellyCat City". All around the title are doodles of stars, hearts, and tiny stuffed animals.]**

**[ILLUSTRATION: The four main characters—Pandy, Dirty, Luna, and Daisy—are standing together in a group hug. They look soft, floppy, and very happy.]**

---

# [PAGE 2]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy the Panda is sitting on a large, soft bamboo leaf. She has a notebook on her lap and is holding a pen. She looks thoughtful.]**

Dear Diary,

My name is Pandy. I’m a panda. But I’m not just *any* panda. I’m a Smudge Panda. That means I’m extra floppy, extra soft, and extra good at snuggling. 

I live in the Bamboo Forest. It’s a very nice place. There’s plenty of bamboo to munch on, and the leaves make the best umbrellas when it rains. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy holding a giant bamboo leaf over her head while it rains tiny hearts instead of raindrops.]**

Most days, I’m perfectly happy. I read my books, I take my naps, and I daydream about things that haven’t happened yet. But lately, I’ve been feeling a little bit... restless. Like there’s a big secret out there waiting for me to find it.

---

# [PAGE 3]

Today was one of those "restless" days. I was sitting by the Silver Stream, trying to read "The Great Bamboo Heist" for the third time, but I couldn't focus.

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy by the stream, her book is upside down. She is staring into the water.]**

The water was sparkling today. It looked like someone had dumped a bag of glitter into the river! I was just about to pack up and head back to my favorite hollow log when I saw something.

It was floating down the stream. Something bright. Something... **GLOWING**.

**[ILLUSTRATION: A small, rolled-up piece of parchment is floating on a leaf in the stream. It’s giving off a soft, magical light.]**

*GASPPP!* 

I almost dropped my bamboo stick! (And it was a really good one—very crunchy!)

---

# [PAGE 4]

I reached out with my floppy paw and scooped the glowing thing out of the water. It felt warm, like it had been sitting in the sun all day.

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy holding the glowing scroll. Her eyes are wide with wonder.]**

I carefully unrolled it. It wasn't just paper. It was a **MAP**! 

But it wasn't a map of the Bamboo Forest. I’ve seen those. They mostly just show where the best bamboo is (which is helpful, don't get me wrong).

This map showed a city. A city made of pillows, clouds, and buttons! And at the very top, in sparkly gold letters, it said:

**[ILLUSTRATION: A close-up of the map. It shows "JELLYCAT CITY" in the center, surrounded by "Whispering Woods", "Rolling Hills", and "Sunny Savannah".]**

"TO THE ONE WHO DREAMS OF MORE."

*EEK!* That’s me! I’m the one who dreams of more!

---

# [PAGE 5]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy standing up, looking determined. She’s wearing a small backpack made of woven vines.]**

I knew right then and there. I couldn't stay in the Bamboo Forest anymore. I had to find this JellyCat City. 

But I’m a very shy panda. I don't usually go on "adventures." Adventures are for Brave Rabbits or Energetic Elephants. I’m a "stay-at-home and read" kind of girl.

My tummy did a little flip. *FLIP-FLOP.* 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A tiny doodle of a heart with a nervous face in the margin.]**

"You can do this, Pandy," I whispered to myself. "It’s just a walk. A long, mysterious, potentially dangerous, but probably very soft walk."

I packed my extra-crunchy bamboo, my favorite book, and my map. 

I was ready. (I think.)

---

# [PAGE 6]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy walking through the tall bamboo. The stalks are swaying in the wind.]**

The Bamboo Forest felt different today. The shadows were longer, and the wind seemed to be whispering my name. Or maybe it was just the leaves rustling. It’s hard to tell.

*Swish-swish... Pandy... swish-swish...*

I followed the map. It told me to head toward the "Glowing Horizon." 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy looking at the map. An arrow points toward a distant, golden light.]**

I’ve never been to the Glowing Horizon. Mostly because my mom told me it was where the "Big Adventures" lived, and I wasn't allowed to have Big Adventures until I finished my bamboo.

Well, I finished my bamboo. So here I go!

---

# [PAGE 7]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy crossing a bridge made of a fallen log. She looks a bit wobbly.]**

*WOBBLE... WOBBLE...*

Log bridges are not designed for floppy pandas. My paws are very soft, which is great for hugging, but terrible for balance!

*WHOA!* 

I almost slipped! My heart did a **THUMP-THUMP** in my chest. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy clinging to the log for dear life. Her legs are dangling.]**

I took a deep breath. "I am a panda of the forest. I am strong. I am... very, very soft."

I made it across. *PHEW!* 

---

# [PAGE 8]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy looking out at the edge of the forest. The trees are thinning, and a wide, open meadow is visible.]**

The Bamboo Forest was fading behind me. I felt a little bit sad. I love my forest. But the map was glowing even brighter now!

It was like the map was excited too. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: The map in Pandy's paw is sparking with tiny golden stars.]**

"Almost there," the map seemed to say. 

I stepped out of the shadows and into the sunlight. It was time for Pandy to see the world.

---

# [PAGE 9]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy sitting down in the meadow, looking at the stars. It’s getting dark.]**

I decided to camp for the night. The stars in the Wild Meadow are huge! They look like giant glowing buttons sewn into the sky.

I curled up in a ball (the Smudge Panda specialty) and looked at the map one last time. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy curled into a fluffy ball. A thought bubble shows the image of a soft city.]**

I wonder if I’ll meet anyone else on the way to JellyCat City. It would be nice to have a friend. Someone who also dreams of more.

Goodnight, Diary. Tomorrow, the adventure continues!

---

# [PAGE 10 - CHAPTER 2: DIRTY'S STORY]

**[ILLUSTRATION: A full-page title illustration. DIRTY THE ELEPHANT is standing in the middle of a dusty savannah. She is trying to use her trunk to spray water on a small flower, but she’s accidentally splashing herself.]**

Dear Diary,

MY TRUNK IS A DISASTER!

Seriously. I think it has a mind of its own. Today, I was just trying to be a "helpful elephant" and water the Tiny-Blue-Flowers in the Sunny Savannah. 

But did my trunk water the flowers? **NOPE.** 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty is completely soaked with water. The flower is dry and looking confused.]**

It watered *ME*. And then it sneezed. 

*ACHOOO!* 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty sneezing so hard her floppy ears fly backward.]**

---

# [PAGE 11]

My name is Dirty. I know, I know. It’s a weird name for an elephant. But I’m a Smudge Elephant, and I’m grey, and I’m always, *always* getting into a mess. 

If there’s a mud puddle within ten miles, I will find it. And I will fall in it.

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty sitting in a giant mud puddle, looking very happy.]**

My mom says I have "Adventurous Spirit." My dad says I’m "clumsy." I think I’m just... curious!

The Sunny Savannah is fun, but it’s very... yellow. Yellow grass. Yellow sun. Yellow lions (who are actually quite nice, once you get to know them).

I want to see something else. Something **SPARKLY**.

---

# [PAGE 12]

I was walking by the Golden Watering Hole, looking for a snack (I was really craving a peanut-butter-leaf sandwich), when I saw something stuck in the mud.

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty’s trunk is pointing at something shiny in the mud.]**

I used my trunk to pull it out. (I managed not to spray myself this time—PROUD MOMENT!)

It was a leaf. But not a normal leaf. It was made of **GOLD**.

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty holding a shimmering golden leaf. It has lines and drawings on it.]**

"Wait a minute..." I said (well, I trumpeted, which is elephant-talk for saying things). "This looks like a map!"

---

# [PAGE 13]

I wiped the mud off the golden leaf with my ear. (My ears are great for cleaning things, although they get very dirty in the process. Hence the name.)

The map showed a place called **JELLYCAT CITY**. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty looking at the golden leaf map. Her eyes are wide. She’s imagining a city made of giant stuffed animals.]**

*HONK-HONK!* 

I was so excited I did a little dance! 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty doing a "happy dance," her trunk is swinging wildly and she’s accidentally knocking over a small tree.]**

*CRASH!*

"Oops. Sorry, tree!" 

The map said to "FOLLOW THE SUNRISE."

---

# [PAGE 14]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty packing her things. She’s trying to stuff a giant pillow into a very small bag.]**

I decided to go. Right then. No time to waste! 

I packed my "Emergency Peanut Stash," my favorite mud-scraping brush, and my golden map. 

"I’m going on an adventure!" I trumpeted to the giraffes. 

They just blinked at me. Giraffes are very tall, but they aren't very good at getting excited. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A doodle of a bored-looking giraffe in the margin.]**

I started walking. My big grey feet went **THUMP-THUMP-THUMP** on the dusty ground. 

---

# [PAGE 15]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty walking through the tall yellow grass. She looks like she’s on a mission.]**

The Sunny Savannah felt extra big today. I felt like a very small elephant in a very large world. 

Suddenly, I saw something moving in the grass. 

*Rustle... rustle...*

My ears went **FLOP-FLOP**. Was it a lion? Was it a tiger? Was it a giant spider made of yarn? (I have a very active imagination.)

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty hiding behind a tiny bush. Her big bottom is sticking out.]**

I peeked through the grass.

---

# [PAGE 16]

It wasn't a lion. It was a butterfly! A bright, sparkly butterfly with wings like velvet.

**[ILLUSTRATION: A beautiful butterfly is fluttering in front of Dirty's trunk.]**

It landed right on the tip of my trunk. *Boop!*

"Hi there, butterfly," I whispered. "Are you going to JellyCat City too?"

The butterfly flapped its wings and flew toward the horizon. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty following the butterfly. She’s skipping and looks very happy.]**

"Wait for me!" 

---

# [PAGE 17]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty crossing a sandy area. She’s leaving giant footprints behind.]**

The ground was getting sandier. My feet felt heavy, but the golden leaf was glowing! 

It was like a little lamp in my trunk. 

*Glow... glow...*

I was getting closer. I could feel it. The air started to smell like... strawberry fluff? 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty sniffing the air. She looks like she’s in a dream.]**

"MMM! Smells delicious!"

---

# [PAGE 18]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty sitting on a sand dune, watching the sun go down. She’s eating a peanut.]**

I’m tired. My trunk is tired. Even my ears are tired of flopping. 

But I’m so close! The map says JellyCat City is just over the next ridge. 

I wonder if there are other elephants there. Or maybe someone who is good at cleaning ears. That would be nice. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty looking at the stars. A doodle of a "Jellycat City" sign in the margin.]**

Goodnight, Diary. Tomorrow, I’ll be a city elephant! 

---

# [PAGE 19 - CHAPTER 3: LUNA'S STORY]

**[ILLUSTRATION: A full-page title illustration. LUNA THE RABBIT is mid-hop. She is flying through the air, her long white ears trailing behind her. She looks like she’s having the time of her life.]**

Dear Diary,

I CAN’T STOP HOPPING! 

Seriously, it’s a problem. My name is Luna, and I’m a Smudge Rabbit. I’m white, I’m fluffy, and I have the most "Hoppy" legs in the Wild Meadow.

Today, I was trying to sit still and watch the clouds, but my legs had other plans. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Luna trying to sit still. Her legs are vibrating. A "BOING!" sound effect is written in big letters.]**

*BOING! BOING! BOING!*

Before I knew it, I was halfway across the meadow! 

---

# [PAGE 20]

I love the Wild Meadow. There are so many things to sniff! Flowers, clover, bees, even the occasional lost sock (which is a very interesting smell, let me tell you).

**[ILLUSTRATION: Luna sniffing a very colorful flower. Her nose is wiggling.]**

But I’m a very curious rabbit. I always want to know what’s over the next hill. And the hill after that. And the hill after *that*. 

My friends call me "Luna-tic" because I’m always hopping off on adventures. (I think it’s a compliment!)

Today, I found something VERY interesting. I was hopping along a hidden trail—one I’ve never seen before—and it was marked with **SPARKLY STONES**.

**[ILLUSTRATION: Luna looking at a trail of glowing, colorful pebbles leading into the woods.]**

---

# [PAGE 21]

Sparkly stones? *EEK!* 

I love anything that sparkles. I followed the stones. 

*Hop... hop... sparkle... hop...*

The stones led me into the Whispering Woods. Most rabbits are scared of the Whispering Woods. They say the trees talk. 

I think the trees are just bad at keeping secrets. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Luna leaning her ear against a tree trunk. The tree has a tiny mouth and is whispering "Psst..."]**

---

# [PAGE 22]

The stones led me to a clearing. And in the middle of the clearing was a signpost. 

It was made of candy canes and marshmallows! (I tried to nibble it, but it was a bit stale. 4 out of 10 stars.)

The signpost pointed deeper into the woods and said:

**[ILLUSTRATION: A signpost with an arrow pointing toward "JELLYCAT CITY".]**

"FOR THE ONES WHO NEVER STOP HOPPING."

*THAT’S ME!* I never stop hopping! 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Luna doing a giant victory hop. She is so high up she’s touching a cloud.]**

---

# [PAGE 23]

I didn't even need to pack. I have everything I need right here: my fluffy tail, my twitchy nose, and my adventurous spirit! 

"I’m going to JellyCat City!" I shouted to the squirrels. 

They just threw an acorn at me. Squirrels have no sense of adventure. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A doodle of an angry squirrel in the margin.]**

I hopped onto the sparkly trail. 

*BOING! BOING! BOING!*

The woods were getting darker, but the stones were glowing like little stars on the ground.

---

# [PAGE 24]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Luna hopping over a fallen branch. She’s mid-air, looking very graceful.]**

The Whispering Woods are actually very pretty. There are glowing mushrooms and fireflies that dance in patterns. 

I saw a family of hedgehogs. They looked very cozy in their mossy home. 

"Hi, hedgehogs! Want to come to a magical city?" 

The hedgehogs just curled into balls. They’re very shy. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Luna waving at three little balls of moss (hedgehogs).]**

"Your loss! More pillows for me!"

---

# [PAGE 25]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Luna sitting on a giant mushroom, looking at the moon. The moon is big and white, just like her.]**

I think I was named after the moon. We’re both white, we’re both round, and we both love the night! 

I’m resting on this mushroom for a minute. My legs are finally a bit tired. 

I wonder if JellyCat City has giant carrot cakes. Or maybe a field of unending clover. A rabbit can dream! 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Luna yawning. A thought bubble shows a giant carrot cake with "JELLYCAT" written on it.]**

Goodnight, Diary. I’ll hop to the city tomorrow! 

---

# [PAGE 26 - CHAPTER 4: DAISY'S STORY]

**[ILLUSTRATION: A full-page title illustration. DAISY THE LAMB is standing on a beautiful green hill. She is surrounded by buttercups. She looks a little bit lonely.]**

Dear Diary,

Sometimes, the Rolling Hills feel a little bit *too* rolling.

And a little bit *too* quiet.

My name is Daisy. I’m a Smudge Lamb. I have long floppy legs, soft white wool, and a heart that’s a little bit too big for my chest. 

I spend my days watching the clouds and listening to the wind. It’s very peaceful. But sometimes... I feel a little bit lonely.

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy sitting on the grass, looking at her own shadow. She’s trying to make a hand-shadow puppet with her hoof, but it just looks like a hoof.]**

---

# [PAGE 27]

I wish I had someone to talk to. Someone who likes to look at clouds as much as I do. 

"Look, shadow-me," I said today. "That cloud looks like a giant popcorn." 

My shadow didn't say anything back. Shadows are terrible listeners. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy pointing at a cloud. A doodle of a popcorn kernel in the margin.]**

I was feeling a bit "blue" (which is funny, because I’m white) when a butterfly landed on my nose. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A tiny, bright blue butterfly on Daisy's nose. Daisy is crossing her eyes to look at it.]**

*Wiggle-wiggle...*

---

# [PAGE 28]

The butterfly didn't fly away. It fluttered its wings and then started flying toward the horizon. 

It stopped and looked back at me. 

"Do you want me to follow you?" I asked. 

The butterfly did a little loop-de-loop. 

"Okay! I’m coming!" 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy standing up and shaking her wool. She’s following the butterfly down the hill.]**

I’ve never been to the bottom of the Rolling Hills. I always stay at the top because it’s safer. But the butterfly looked so sure of itself.

---

# [PAGE 29]

The butterfly led me to a path I’ve never seen. It was a path through the valley, lined with flowers that looked like they were made of silk. 

And then, I saw it. A sign hanging from a low branch. 

It was made of soft wool and had buttons for letters! 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A sign made of felt and buttons that says "JELLYCAT CITY: ALL FRIENDS WELCOME".]**

"ALL FRIENDS WELCOME." 

My heart did a little **JUMP**. *BOUNCE!* 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy doing a little "happy leap." Her long legs are all tangled up.]**

*THUD!*

"Oops. I’m okay!"

---

# [PAGE 30]

I packed my favorite ribbon and a bag of sweet clover. 

"Goodbye, Rolling Hills!" I called out. 

The hills just rolled. (They’re very consistent.) 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy walking away from the hills. She looks nervous but excited.]**

The path led me toward a great, shimmering light in the distance. The butterfly was still leading the way. 

"Wait for me, friend!" I called. 

I wasn't lonely anymore. I was an adventurer!

---

# [PAGE 31]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy walking through a field of tall flowers. They are taller than her!]**

The flowers here are amazing! They smell like vanilla and dreams. 

I saw a ladybug. She had sparkly spots. 

"Hi, ladybug! I’m Daisy. I’m going to JellyCat City to find friends." 

The ladybug just crawled under a leaf. She must be shy, just like me. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy peek-a-booing at a ladybug under a leaf.]**

"That’s okay. We can be shy together!"

---

# [PAGE 32]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy sitting by a small pond. The water is pink!]**

The water in this pond is pink! I tried a sip. It tastes like strawberry milk! 

*GULP! GULP!* 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy drinking from the pink pond. She has a pink milk-mustache.]**

I feel much braver now. Strawberry milk always makes me feel like I can conquer the world. Or at least walk a few more miles. 

The butterfly is waiting on a stone. 

"Okay, okay! I’m coming!"

---

# [PAGE 33]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy looking at the stars. She is curled up in a soft nest of grass.]**

It’s my first night away from home. I feel a little bit scared. But the stars are so bright! 

I wonder if there are other lambs in JellyCat City. Or maybe a panda. I’ve always wanted to meet a panda. I hear they’re very soft. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy yawning. A thought bubble shows her hugging a soft panda.]**

Goodnight, Diary. Tomorrow, I’ll find the city of friends! 

---

# [PAGE 34 - CHAPTER 5: TOGETHER IN JELLYCAT CITY]

**[ILLUSTRATION: A full-page title illustration. THE FOUR FRIENDS—Pandy, Dirty, Luna, and Daisy—are standing at the gates of a magnificent city. The gates are made of giant gold safety pins. The city inside is glowing and colorful.]**

Dear Diary,

TODAY WAS THE BEST DAY EVER! 

(Okay, maybe I say that a lot, but this time I REALLY MEAN IT!)

I was walking along the path—I’m Pandy, by the way, remember me?—and I saw a ridge. And when I climbed to the top of the ridge... 

**I SAW IT.**

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy standing on a ridge, looking down at JELLYCAT CITY. It’s a city of fluff, buttons, and pillows.]**

It was more beautiful than the map! There were buildings made of soft blankets, fountains that sprayed glittery bubbles, and a giant Ferris wheel made of ribbons!

---

# [PAGE 35]

But I wasn't the only one at the ridge. 

*THUMP-THUMP-THUMP...* 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty the Elephant is coming up the ridge from the other side. She’s covered in sand and looks very excited.]**

*BOING-BOING-BOING...*

**[ILLUSTRATION: Luna the Rabbit is hopping up the ridge from the woods.]**

*TRIP-STUMBLE-HOORAY...*

**[ILLUSTRATION: Daisy the Lamb is trotting up from the hills.]**

We all reached the top at the same time. We all stopped and stared at each other. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: The four of them standing in a circle, looking shocked and a bit nervous.]**

---

# [PAGE 36]

"Um... hi?" I said. I was feeling very shy. 

"HONK! I mean, hello!" Dirty the Elephant trumpeted. (She was so loud she accidentally knocked me over.) 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy on her bottom, looking surprised. Dirty looking apologetic.]**

"Are you guys going to JellyCat City too?" Luna the Rabbit asked. She couldn't stop hopping. 

"The map said... all friends welcome," Daisy the Lamb whispered. She looked like she wanted a hug. 

So, naturally, I gave her one. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy hugging Daisy. They both look very soft.]**

---

# [PAGE 37]

We all shared our stories. Dirty showed us her golden leaf. Luna showed us her sparkly stones. Daisy told us about her butterfly. 

We realized we were all following the same dream! 

**[ILLUSTRATION: The four of them looking at their maps and stones together. They are all smiling.]**

"Let’s go together!" Dirty said. 

"YES! A team!" Luna hopped. 

We walked down the hill and through the Golden Safety Pin Gates. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: The four friends walking through the gates. A sign above says: "WELCOME HOME, STUFFIES!"]**

---

# [PAGE 38]

JellyCat City is AMAZING! 

Everything is soft. Even the sidewalks are made of memory foam! 

*Squish... squish... squish...* 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Dirty walking on the sidewalk. Her feet are sinking in and then popping back up.]**

We visited the **Great Pillow Palace**. It’s a giant castle made of every kind of pillow you can imagine. Velvet, silk, cotton, even a giant marshmallow pillow! 

**[ILLUSTRATION: The four of them jumping into a giant pile of pillows inside the palace.]**

"This is the best place in the universe!" Dirty trumpeted. 

---

# [PAGE 39]

Then we rode the **Fluff Express**. It’s a train made of giant pom-poms that travels around the city on a track of rainbow ribbons. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: The friends sitting on a giant pink pom-pom train. Luna is leaning out the window, her ears flapping.]**

We saw the **Button Bakery**, where they make cookies that look like buttons. (They tasted like vanilla and happiness.) 

We saw the **Stitchery**, where stuffies get their loose threads fixed. 

"I’m never leaving," Luna said, munching on a button cookie. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: Luna with a mouth full of cookies. A doodle of a "Button Cookie" in the margin.]**

---

# [PAGE 40]

We spent the whole day exploring. We became best friends in about five minutes. 

Dirty is funny and brave. Luna is energetic and curious. Daisy is sweet and kind. And I’m... well, I’m happy to be with them. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: The four of them sitting on a giant cloud-bench, watching the sunset over the city.]**

"We should stay together forever," Daisy said. 

"The Snuggle Tales!" I said. "That’s what our adventure should be called." 

Everyone agreed. *HONK! BOING! BAA!* 

---

# [PAGE 41]

As the sun went down, we decided to take one last walk before bedtime. We headed toward the edge of the city, where the **Whispering Woods** begin. 

"The map says there’s a secret hidden in the woods," I said. 

"Let's find it!" Luna shouted. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: The four friends walking into the dark, mysterious woods on the edge of the city.]**

The trees were whispering louder than ever. 

*Look closer... look closer...* 

Suddenly, I tripped!

---

# [PAGE 42]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy tripping over a big, gnarled root. She’s falling forward.]**

*WHOA!* 

I grabbed Dirty’s trunk. Dirty grabbed Luna’s ears. Luna grabbed Daisy’s tail. 

We all went tumbling! 

*TUMBLE... ROLL... FLOP...* 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A big "dust cloud" of stuffies rolling down a hidden slope.]**

We landed in a giant pile of colorful autumn leaves. 

"Is everyone okay?" I asked, my head popping out of the leaves. 

---

# [PAGE 43]

**[ILLUSTRATION: The four friends climbing out of the leaf pile. They look confused. The background is no longer JellyCat City. It’s a strange land made of giant candies and lollipops.]**

We climbed out of the leaves and brushed ourselves off. 

But when we looked around... we weren't in JellyCat City anymore. 

The buildings weren't made of pillows. They were made of **CHOCOLATE**. The trees weren't bamboo. They were **LOLLIPOPS**.

"Where are we?" Daisy whispered. 

**[ILLUSTRATION: A giant sign in the distance: "WELCOME TO CANDYLAND."]**

*EEK!* 

TO BE CONTINUED...

**[ILLUSTRATION: The four friends standing in Candyland, looking completely lost. Below them, a small doodle of a candy cane with a "To be continued..." sign.]**

# [PAGE 44: PANDY'S BOOK NOOK]

**[ILLUSTRATION: Pandy is sitting in a giant pile of books. She has a pair of oversized reading glasses balanced on her nose (even though she doesn't really need them). A small lamp made of a glowing mushroom is by her side.]**

Dear Diary,

Before I left the Bamboo Forest, I had to decide which books to bring with me. This was the HARDEST decision of my life. (Even harder than choosing between crunchy bamboo and soft bamboo.)

I couldn't bring them all, because my backpack is only so big. So I made a list of my Top 5 favorites:

1. **"The Great Bamboo Heist"** — A classic. It’s about a panda who solves a mystery using only her nose and a very large hat.
2. **"101 Ways to Fluff Your Ears"** — Very practical. 
3. **"The Secret Life of Clouds"** — This one is mostly pictures, but they’re very pretty pictures.
4. **"How to Win Friends and Influence Stuffies"** — I’m still working on the "influence" part.
5. **"The Panda’s Guide to the Galaxy"** — It says the answer to everything is 42... 42 pieces of bamboo!

**[ILLUSTRATION: A doodle of a giant "42" made out of bamboo sticks in the margin.]**

---

# [PAGE 45: TYPES OF BAMBOO (A PANDY STUDY)]

**[ILLUSTRATION: A scientific-looking chart. There are drawings of different bamboo stalks with labels.]**
