# Moses and the Amazing Sea Activity Book (Outline)

**Target Audience:** Ages 2-6
**Theme:** Bible Series - Miracles, Trust, and Celebration

---

## Page-by-Page Outline (24 Pages)

### Page 1: Cover Page
*   **Activity:** Title and Illustration
*   **Description:** "Moses and the Amazing Sea: A Bible Adventures Activity Book!" A large, bright illustration of Moses holding his staff high as the sea parts.

### Page 2: This Book Belongs To...
*   **Activity:** Name Plate
*   **Description:** A frame decorated with seashells, palm trees, and water ripples.
*   **Text:** "This Brave Explorer is: [Name]"

### Page 3: Coloring Page - Moses and the People
*   **Scene:** Moses talking to a group of happy families (the Israelites) as they prepare for their journey.
*   **Text:** "Moses is a kind leader. Color his long white beard!"

### Page 4: Tracing Activity - S-E-A
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the word "SEA".
*   **Text:** "Can you trace the word SEA?"

### Page 5: Coloring Page - Walking in the Desert
*   **Scene:** Families walking through the sandy desert with palm trees in the distance.
*   **Text:** "It is a long walk in the warm sand. Color the tall palm trees."

### Page 6: Tracing Activity - Wave Shapes
*   **Activity:** Shape Tracing.
*   **Description:** Dashed wavy lines representing the Red Sea waves.
*   **Text:** "The water has big waves. Trace the wavy lines!"

### Page 7: Coloring Page - By the Big Water
*   **Scene:** Moses and the people standing at the edge of the deep blue Red Sea.
*   **Text:** "The Red Sea is very big. Color the deep blue water."

### Page 8: Simple Maze - Help the Israelites Cross
*   **Activity:** Maze.
*   **Description:** A simple path between two water walls to help the people get to the other side.
*   **Text:** "Help the families find the way across the dry path!"

### Page 9: Coloring Page - The Parting Sea
*   **Scene:** The sea pulling apart, revealing a dry path in the middle.
*   **Text:** "Look! The water is moving! Color the shimmering path."

### Page 10: Tracing Activity - M-O-S-E-S
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the name "MOSES".
*   **Text:** "Can you trace Moses' name?"

### Page 11: Coloring Page - Walls of Water
*   **Scene:** A view from the path looking up at the tall walls of water on both sides.
*   **Text:** "The water stands up like tall curtains. Color the water windows."

### Page 12: Counting Activity - Fish in the Walls
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** Several fish visible through the transparent water walls.
*   **Text:** "How many fish are swimming in the water walls? Count them: [ ]"

### Page 13: Coloring Page - Walking Through
*   **Scene:** Children and families walking safely on the dry ground in the middle of the sea.
*   **Text:** "The ground is dry and safe. Color the happy children."

### Page 14: Connect-the-Dots - Moses' Staff
*   **Activity:** Connect-the-dots (Numbers 1-10).
*   **Description:** An outline of Moses' tall wooden staff.
*   **Text:** "Connect the dots 1-10 to see Moses' wooden staff!"

### Page 15: Coloring Page - Miriam’s Song
*   **Scene:** Miriam dancing and playing her tambourine on the new shore.
*   **Text:** "Miriam is so happy! Color her colorful dress."

### Page 16: Tracing Activity - J-O-Y
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the word "JOY".
*   **Text:** "The people feel so much JOY! Can you trace the word?"

### Page 17: Coloring Page - The Big Celebration
*   **Scene:** Everyone singing and dancing on the beach after crossing safely.
*   **Text:** "It is time to celebrate! Color the happy faces."

### Page 18: Spot the Difference - The Sea Parting
*   **Activity:** Spot 3 Differences.
*   **Description:** Two pictures of Moses holding his staff over the parting sea.
*   **Differences:** (1) The number of clouds, (2) A crab on the sand, (3) The color of Moses' headcloth.

### Page 19: Coloring Page - Safe in the New Home
*   **Scene:** The families resting under palm trees in their new, beautiful land.
*   **Text:** "God took care of everyone. Color the peaceful sunset."

### Page 20: Counting Activity - Happy Families
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** Small groups of families celebrating together.
*   **Text:** "How many families are happy today? Count them: [ ]"

### Page 21: Drawing Activity - My Amazing Water Wall
*   **Activity:** Creative Drawing.
*   **Description:** A large empty space between two lines representing the sea floor.
*   **Text:** "If you walked through the sea, what would you see? Draw your own water wall here!"

### Page 22: Tracing Activity - Star Shapes
*   **Activity:** Shape Tracing.
*   **Description:** Several dashed star shapes in the sky.
*   **Text:** "The stars are shining tonight. Trace the stars!"

### Page 23: Coloring Page - Brave Moses
*   **Scene:** Moses sitting on a rock, smiling as he watches the people.
*   **Text:** "Moses trusted God. Color Moses and his staff."

### Page 24: Brave Explorer Certificate
*   **Activity:** Award Page.
*   **Description:** "Congratulations! [Name] is a Brave Explorer!" Decorated with water drops, stars, and a big staff.
