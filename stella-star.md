# Stella the Star Who Found Friends

## Character Descriptions

*   **Stella**: A tiny, five-pointed star with a gentle, glowing face and big, dark eyes. Her light is a soft, warm gold that pulse slightly when she’s happy.
*   **Moona**: A large, elegant crescent moon with a calm, motherly smile. She wears a crown of stardust and has a voice like a soothing lullaby.
*   **Comet Carl**: A fast-moving comet with a bright blue face and a long, shimmering tail of icy crystals. He’s always in a hurry but has a very friendly laugh.
*   **The Twinkle Twins**: Two identical small stars that are always seen right next to each other. They finish each other's sentences and have high, giggly voices.

## Story Pages

### Page 1: Title Page
**Text:** Stella the Star Who Found Friends
**Scene Description:** Stella is floating in a vast, dark purple sky. She looks small but her golden light is very pretty.

### Page 2
**Text:** High above the world, where the air is thin and the night is deep, lived Stella. She was a tiny star in a very big sky.
**Scene Description:** A wide shot of the night sky over a sleeping village. Stella is just a small speck of light near the top.

### Page 3
**Text:** Stella was beautiful. She was sparkly. But Stella was also very, very lonely.
**Scene Description:** Close up of Stella. She has a tiny, sad frown on her face as she looks around the empty space around her.

### Page 4
**Text:** "I wish I had someone to play with," Stella whispered. Her light flickered low.
**Scene Description:** Stella looking down at the clouds far below. She looks like she’s about to cry a stardust tear.

### Page 5
**Text:** Suddenly, a bright blue streak zipped past her. *WHOOSH!*
**Scene Description:** Comet Carl zooming by, leaving a long trail of blue glitter. Stella is spinning in his wake.

### Page 6
**Text:** "Hello there!" a boisterous voice called out. It was Comet Carl. "Why the long face, little star?"
**Scene Description:** Comet Carl circling back to Stella. He’s smiling and his icy tail is sparkling.

### Page 7
**Text:** "I'm lonely, Carl," Stella said. "The sky is so big, and I'm so small."
**Scene Description:** Stella looking up at the giant comet. Carl looks thoughtful.

### Page 8
**Text:** "The sky is only big if you look at the empty spots!" Carl laughed. "Follow me! I'll show you something amazing!"
**Scene Description:** Carl zooming off again, gesturing for Stella to follow.

### Page 9
**Text:** Stella tried to move, but she was rooted in her spot. "I can't!" she cried. "Stars stay still!"
**Scene Description:** Stella trying to kick her points, but she stays in place. Carl is already far away.

### Page 10
**Text:** Carl stopped and sighed. "Ah, I forgot. Well, don't you worry. If you can't come to the party, I'll send some guests your way!"
**Scene Description:** Carl waving his tail and disappearing into the distance. Stella is alone again.

### Page 11
**Text:** Stella waited. The night grew darker. She felt even lonelier than before.
**Scene Description:** A dark, quiet scene. Stella is looking down, her light very dim.

### Page 12
**Text:** Then, she heard a soft, silvery hum. A warm light began to grow around her.
**Scene Description:** A gentle glow starts to fill the page. Stella is looking up in surprise.

### Page 13
**Text:** "Hello, little Stella," a kind voice said. It was Moona the Moon. She looked like a giant, glowing cradle.
**Scene Description:** Moona drifting slowly toward Stella. Her face is full of love and warmth.

### Page 14
**Text:** "Moona!" Stella gasped. "Am I all alone up here?"
**Scene Description:** Stella looking tiny next to the massive, beautiful moon.

### Page 15
**Text:** Moona chuckled softly. "Oh, Stella. Look closer. Not at the dark, but at the light."
**Scene Description:** Moona pointing her crescent tip toward the distance.

### Page 16
**Text:** Moona breathed a gentle mist of stardust across the sky.
**Scene Description:** A wave of sparkles moving across the dark background.

### Page 17
**Text:** As the mist cleared, Stella saw them. Thousands and thousands of stars!
**Scene Description:** The sky is suddenly full of stars of all shapes and sizes. They were always there, but now they are bright.

### Page 18
**Text:** Right next to her were two little stars. "Hi!" said one. "Hello!" said the other. It was the Twinkle Twins.
**Scene Description:** The Twinkle Twins giggling and waving their points at Stella.

### Page 19
**Text:** "We've been here all along," the first twin said. "We were just waiting for you to say hello," said the second.
**Scene Description:** Stella looking at her new neighbors with wide, happy eyes. Her glow is becoming bright gold again.

### Page 20
**Text:** Stella realized that the sky wasn't empty at all. It was a giant blanket of friends!
**Scene Description:** A wide shot showing Stella, the Twins, Moona, and many other stars all smiling together.

### Page 21
**Text:** Comet Carl zipped by again. "See? I told you!" he yelled. He threw a handful of blue glitter at them.
**Scene Description:** Carl zooming through the group, everyone is laughing.

### Page 22
**Text:** Stella began to pulse her light. *Glow... dim... glow!*
**Scene Description:** Stella looking radiant. Her light is so bright it creates a little halo around her.

### Page 23
**Text:** All the other stars began to pulse with her. The whole sky was twinkling like a beautiful song.
**Scene Description:** A beautiful pattern of twinkling stars across the sky, creating a sense of rhythm and harmony.

### Page 24
**Text:** Stella wasn't lonely anymore. She knew that even a tiny star is part of a very big, very bright family.
**Scene Description:** Stella sleeping peacefully, tucked in near the Twinkle Twins. Moona is watching over them all.

**The End.**
