# World Cup Series Book #3: DC's World Cup Welcome

**Target Age:** 5-9 years
**Theme:** History, unity, welcoming the world, big dreams.
**Characters:**
- **Maya:** A curious 9-year-old from Washington DC who loves history and soccer.
- **Mom:** Maya’s mother, who works in the city and loves sharing DC's landmarks.
- **FedEx Giant:** A massive, powerful stadium character with a heart of gold and a cape made of a giant flag.
- **Team England:** The "Three Lions" team in their classic white and red kits.

---

## Page 2
**Visual:** Maya and Mom are at breakfast. Maya has a map of DC and a soccer schedule spread out. Mom is pointing to FedExField in Landover, Maryland.
**Text:**
In Washington DC, every building has a story to tell. But today, Maya was excited about a story that was just beginning. 

“Is it time to go to the stadium yet, Mom?” Maya asked, her eyes sparkling. 

“Almost, Maya,” Mom smiled. “Today, we’re not just seeing history; we’re part of it! England is playing at FedExField!”

---

## Page 3
**Visual:** Maya and Mom on a Metro train (Orange Line). The train is full of fans. Many are wearing England jerseys, others have USA gear, and some have flags from all over the world.
**Text:**
They hopped on the Metro. The train buzzed with the sound of many different voices and languages. 

“Welcome to the capital!” Maya said to a fan wearing a red-and-white scarf. The fan smiled and gave her a "thumbs up." 

In DC, people from everywhere come together. Maya felt like the whole world was her neighbor today.

---

## Page 4
**Visual:** Arriving at the stadium. FedExField is huge, with its multi-colored seats and wide open concourses. The stadium character, the FedEx Giant, is a massive figure with a heart-shaped video screen and a cape made of the "Welcome" flag.
**Text:**
As they walked up the hill toward FedExField, the stadium character—the FedEx Giant—rose to meet them. 

The Giant was strong and wide, like the monuments in the city. He wore a giant cape that said "WELCOME THE WORLD" in every language. His heart was a glowing video screen that showed highlights of great goals.

---

## Page 5
**Visual:** The FedEx Giant "salutes" the fans. He has big spotlights on his shoulders that shine up into the sky. Maya is waving her DC-themed soccer scarf back at him.
**Text:**
The Giant gave a grand salute to the fans. He knew that hosting the World Cup was a big responsibility! 

“Welcome to the land of legends!” the stadium Giant seemed to rumble. Maya waved her scarf, feeling like a little explorer entering a giant’s palace.

---

## Page 6
**Visual:** Outside the stadium in the "Fan Fest." There are food trucks selling "half-smokes" (a DC specialty) and people playing mini-soccer games.
**Text:**
Before the game, they explored the Fan Fest. Maya tried a "half-smoke" hot dog, a DC favorite! 

“Look, Mom! They’re playing soccer with a giant ball!” Maya laughed as she watched a group of kids kick a ball twice as big as they were. The Giant stadium chuckled, making his seats rattle just a little.

---

## Page 7
**Visual:** Walking through the gates and seeing the massive bowl of the stadium. The grass is perfect, and the seats go up, up, up!
**Text:**
Walking into the main bowl was like entering a giant bowl of energy! The grass was a perfect emerald green, and the seats rose high into the Maryland sky. 

“Did you know FedExField can hold almost 60,000 people?” Mom said. Maya looked at the thousands of fans and imagined them all cheering at once.

---

## Page 8
**Visual:** The opening ceremony. A giant "3D" projection of the US Capitol and the Big Ben clock tower appears on the field. The FedEx Giant is "beaming" with pride.
**Text:**
The ceremony was magical! Giant projections showed the landmarks of DC and London side-by-side. 

The FedEx Giant beamed his lights onto the field, making the grass sparkle like diamonds. He was proud to host such a grand meeting of cultures.

---

## Page 9
**Visual:** Team England walks onto the field. The crowd is a sea of white and red. Maya is taking a picture with her phone.
**Text:**
Then came the "Three Lions"—Team England! They looked polished and professional. 

“They play with so much history,” Mom whispered. Maya watched them warm up, her heart beating like a drum. The Giant stadium stood tall, guarding the players with his massive walls.

---

## Page 10
**Visual:** The match begins! England is playing against Team Japan (in blue). The play is very tactical and fast.
**Text:**
*WHISTLE!* The game was afoot! 

England moved the ball with precision, like a perfectly timed clock. Japan was quick and nimble, darting around the field like blue dragonflies. 

“Go England!” Maya cheered, though she also clapped when the Japan goalkeeper made a great save.

---

## Page 11
**Visual:** A spectacular save. The Japanese goalkeeper dives to his right to stop a powerful shot from an English player. The FedEx Giant’s "eyes" (lights) go wide in surprise.
**Text:**
Suddenly, an English player took a massive shot! *BOOM!* 

The Japanese goalie dived through the air, stretching his fingers to the very edge of the post. He tipped it away just in time! The stadium Giant blinked his light-bulb eyes in amazement. What a save!

---

## Page 12
**Visual:** Half-time. Maya is looking at a "World Map of Soccer" mural in the stadium concourse.
**Text:**
At half-time, the score was still tied at zero. Maya found a mural that showed where every World Cup had been held. 

“Soccer brings the whole world together,” Maya said. 

“It sure does,” Mom agreed. “And today, DC is the center of it all!”

---

## Page 13
**Visual:** The second half starts. The clouds have moved in, making the stadium lights look even brighter. The FedEx Giant is "standing tall" against the clouds.
**Text:**
The second half began under a cloudy sky. The stadium lights turned on, making everything look like a stage. 

The FedEx Giant felt the cool breeze and stood even taller. He loved the drama of a close game!

---

## Page 14
**Visual:** England scores! A header from a corner kick. The players are celebrating in a circle.
**Text:**
Finally, a goal! From a corner kick, a tall English player jumped high—*BONK!*—and headed the ball into the net. 

*GOALLLLLL!*

The England fans erupted in a roar that Maya felt in her very bones. The FedEx Giant shook his flag-cape in celebration!

---

## Page 15
**Visual:** Japan is pressing hard for an equalizer. The English defenders are working together like a shield. 
**Text:**
Japan didn't give up. They attacked again and again! The English defenders formed a wall, blocking every shot. 

“It’s like a game of chess,” Maya said, watching the players move strategically. 

The FedEx Giant watched closely, his heart-screen showing the tension on the fans’ faces.

---

## Page 16
**Visual:** A close-up of a player handing the ball to an opponent for a throw-in, with a friendly nod.
**Text:**
Even though the game was intense, the players were respectful. When a ball went out of bounds, an English player handed it to a Japanese player with a nod. 

“That’s what the World Cup is all about,” Mom said. “Being great athletes and even better neighbors.”

## Page 17

**Text:**
*TWEET! TWEET! TWEET!* 

The match was over! England had won 1-0. The stadium was filled with the sound of singing. Maya didn't know all the words, but she sang along anyway. 

The FedEx Giant hummed a low, happy tune, satisfied with a great day of soccer.

As they walked toward the exit, Maya met a young fan from Japan. They traded stickers—Maya gave a DC flag, and the fan gave a Japanese soccer pin.

“In this city, Maya, anything is possible,” Mom whispered.
