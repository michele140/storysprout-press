# David and the Giant

## Character Descriptions

*   **David**: A young boy with brown hair and a kind face. He wears a simple tan shepherd's tunic and carries a small wooden staff and a leather sling.
*   **Goliath**: A very tall, big soldier with a booming voice. He wears shiny silver armor and a large helmet. Despite his size, he has a friendly, wide-eyed look.
*   **King Saul**: The king of Israel. He has a grey beard and wears a bright gold crown and a purple robe.
*   **Woolly**: A tiny, fluffy white lamb with a pink nose. Woolly is David's best friend and follows him everywhere.

## Story Pages

### Page 1: Title Page
**Text:** David and the Giant
**Scene Description:** David stands smiling in a bright green field with Woolly the lamb at his side. The sun is shining, and the sky is blue.

### Page 2
**Text:** High in the green hills, lived a boy named David. 
**Scene Description:** A wide shot of rolling sunny hills with many white sheep grazing peacefully.

### Page 3
**Text:** David was a shepherd. He took care of his fluffy sheep every single day.

### Page 4
**Text:** David had a special friend named Woolly. Woolly was a little lamb who followed David everywhere.
**Scene Description:** David is walking along a path, and Woolly the lamb is hopping along right behind him, looking very happy.

### Page 5
**Text:** One day, David’s father said, “Please take some bread and cheese to your brothers. They are at the army camp.”
**Scene Description:** David’s father is handing him a woven basket filled with food. David looks eager and ready for the trip.

### Page 6
**Text:** David and Woolly walked and walked. They saw many colorful tents and many tall soldiers.
**Scene Description:** David and Woolly arrive at a camp. There are many tents with flags waving in the breeze.

### Page 7
**Text:** Just then, a very big man stepped out. His name was Goliath. 
**Scene Description:** Goliath appears from behind a large tent. He is very tall, wearing shiny armor and a helmet, but he has a big, wide smile.

### Page 8
**Text:** Goliath was as tall as a tree! “Who will come and play a game with me?” he called out.
**Scene Description:** Goliath is standing with his hands on his hips, looking over the crowd of soldiers. The soldiers look small next to him.

### Page 9
**Text:** All the soldiers were afraid. They hid behind their big shields. But not David!
**Scene Description:** Soldiers are peeking out from behind large wooden shields. David stands in the middle of the camp, looking curious and not afraid at all.

### Page 10
**Text:** “I am not afraid,” David said. “God is with me, and God is very big and strong.”
**Scene Description:** A close-up of David looking brave. Woolly the lamb is peeking out from behind David’s leg.

### Page 11
**Text:** David went to see King Saul. The king sat on a throne and wore a bright gold crown.
**Scene Description:** David standing before King Saul. The King looks worried, leaning forward on his throne.

### Page 12
**Text:** “You are just a boy,” King Saul said. “Goliath is very big.” David smiled. “God helps me take care of my sheep. He will help me now.”
**Scene Description:** King Saul looks surprised. David is gesturing with his hands, looking very confident.

### Page 13
**Text:** King Saul tried to give David his armor. It was very heavy and very shiny.
**Scene Description:** David is trying on a silver helmet that is way too big—it has fallen down and covered his eyes. A heavy shield is leaning against his side.

### Page 14
**Text:** “This is too big!” David laughed. “I don't need a sword. I have my sling and my faith.”
**Scene Description:** David is stepping out of the heavy armor. He looks much more comfortable in his simple shepherd’s clothes again.

### Page 15
**Text:** David walked down to the sparkling stream. He looked for something special.
**Scene Description:** David is kneeling by a clear, bubbling stream. The sun makes the water sparkle like diamonds.

### Page 16
**Text:** *One, two, three, four, five.* David picked up five smooth, round stones.
**Scene Description:** A close-up of David’s hand holding five small, smooth grey stones.

### Page 17
**Text:** David put the stones in his pouch. He took his sling in his hand. “Let's go, Woolly!”
**Scene Description:** David is standing up, looking ready. Woolly the lamb is standing right beside him at the edge of the stream.

### Page 18
**Text:** Goliath saw David coming. “You are very small!” Goliath shouted. 
**Scene Description:** David is walking across a flat field toward the giant Goliath. Goliath is looking down at David with his hands on his knees.

### Page 19
**Text:** David shouted back, “You have a sword, but I have God on my side!” 
**Scene Description:** David is standing tall and brave. He looks very small next to Goliath, but he is holding his sling ready.

### Page 20
**Text:** David put a stone in his sling. He whirled it around and around. *Whish! Whish! Whish!*
**Scene Description:** David is swinging his sling in a circle above his head. You can see the motion lines of the sling.

### Page 21
**Text:** *Pop!* The stone flew through the air. It hit Goliath right on his forehead.
**Scene Description:** The small stone is flying through the air toward Goliath. Goliath’s eyes are wide with surprise.

### Page 22
**Text:** *Thump!* Goliath fell down. He was very big, so he made a very big sound.
**Scene Description:** Goliath is sitting on the ground, looking a bit dizzy. His helmet has tilted to the side. He is okay, just surprised.

### Page 23

### Page 24
**Text:** David went back to his sheep in the hills. He knew that with God’s help, anyone can be brave.
**Scene Description:** David and Woolly are walking back toward the green hills as the sun sets, painting the sky in pink and orange.
