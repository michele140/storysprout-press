# Stella the Star Who Found Friends Activity Book (Outline)

**Target Audience:** Ages 2-6
**Theme:** Space, Stars, Friendship, and Light

---

## Page-by-Page Outline (24 Pages)

### Page 1: Cover Page
*   **Activity:** Title and Illustration
*   **Description:** "Stella the Star's Twinkly Space Activity Book!" Stella pulsing with a bright golden light, surrounded by other smiling stars.

### Page 2: This Book Belongs To...
*   **Activity:** Name Plate
*   **Description:** A shooting star trail with a space for the child to write/trace their name.

### Page 3: Coloring Page - Stella in the Sky
*   **Scene:** Stella floating in a vast, dark sky.
*   **Text:** "Stella is a tiny star. Color her soft golden glow."

### Page 4: Tracing Activity - S-T-A-R
*   **Activity:** Letter Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed letters for the word "STAR".
*   **Text:** "Twinkle, twinkle! Can you trace the word?"

### Page 5: Coloring Page - Lonely Stella
*   **Scene:** Stella looking sad in the empty dark space.
*   **Text:** "The sky feels very big. Color the deep purple night."

### Page 6: Tracing Activity - Star Points
*   **Activity:** Shape Tracing.
*   **Description:** Several dashed 5-pointed star shapes.
*   **Text:** "Stella has five points. Trace the stars!"

### Page 7: Coloring Page - Comet Carl
*   **Scene:** Comet Carl zooming by with his shimmering blue tail.
*   **Text:** "Carl is a fast comet! Color his icy blue tail."

### Page 8: Simple Maze - Follow the Comet
*   **Activity:** Maze.
*   **Description:** Help Stella follow the trail Carl left behind (though she stays still, the maze represents her gaze).
*   **Text:** "Where did Carl go? Help find the path through the stardust!"

### Page 9: Coloring Page - Moona the Moon
*   **Scene:** Moona the crescent moon drifting toward Stella.
*   **Text:** "Moona is like a giant cradle. Color the beautiful crescent moon."

### Page 10: Tracing Activity - Moon Curves
*   **Activity:** Line Tracing.
*   **Description:** Large dashed crescent shapes.
*   **Text:** "Moona is curved and gentle. Trace the moon shapes."

### Page 11: Coloring Page - Stardust Mist
*   **Scene:** Moona breathing a mist of sparkles across the dark sky.
*   **Text:** "Moona shows the way. Color the sparkly stardust mist."

### Page 12: Connect-the-Dots - Big Star
*   **Activity:** Connect-the-dots (Numbers 1-15).
*   **Description:** A large star shape.
*   **Text:** "Connect the dots to make a giant star!"

### Page 13: Coloring Page - Surprise Friends
*   **Scene:** Stella surrounded by thousands of stars that were hidden in the dark.
*   **Text:** "You're not alone, Stella! The sky is full of friends."

### Page 14: Counting Activity - Twinkle Twins
*   **Activity:** Counting.
*   **Description:** A picture with several pairs of stars.
*   **Text:** "How many stars are in the twins' group? Count them!"

### Page 15: Coloring Page - Twinkle Twins
*   **Scene:** The two identical small stars giggling next to Stella.
*   **Text:** "Hi! Hello! Color the happy Twinkle Twins."

### Page 16: Tracing Activity - Shooting Stars
*   **Activity:** Line Tracing.
*   **Description:** Long, arced dashed lines with a star at the end.
*   **Text:** "Make a wish! Trace the shooting stars."

### Page 17: Coloring Page - Space Party
*   **Scene:** Stella, the Twins, Moona, and Carl all together.
*   **Text:** "Everyone is laughing! Color the big space family."

### Page 18: Spot the Difference - Twinkly Night
*   **Activity:** Spot 3 Differences.
*   **Description:** Two pictures of the sky full of stars.
*   **Differences:** (1) A planet, (2) A comet's direction, (3) A star's face.

### Page 19: Coloring Page - Radiant Stella
*   **Scene:** Stella pulsing her light, creating a halo around her.
*   **Text:** "Stella is so happy she is glowing! Color the bright halo."

### Page 20: Tracing Activity - Pulsing Light
*   **Activity:** Line Tracing.
*   **Description:** Concentric star-shaped dashed lines expanding from a center.
*   **Text:** "Glow... dim... glow! Trace the pulses of light."

### Page 21: Coloring Page - The Twinkling Song
*   **Scene:** All stars pulsing together in a beautiful pattern.
*   **Text:** "The whole sky is singing! Color the twinkly sky."

### Page 22: Drawing Activity - My Own Star
*   **Activity:** Creative Drawing.
*   **Description:** A large blank area with a dark background.
*   **Text:** "If you were a star, what would you look like? Draw yourself here!"

### Page 23: Matching Activity - Space Friends
*   **Activity:** Matching.
*   **Description:** Match the characters to their colors (Stella -> Gold, Carl -> Blue, Moona -> Silver).

### Page 24: Space Explorer Certificate
*   **Activity:** Award Page.
*   **Description:** "Congratulations! [Name] is a Twinkly Space Explorer!" Decorated with planets and stars.
